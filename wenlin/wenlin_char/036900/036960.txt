324  遠(S远) [yuǎn] far  [yuàn]
遠[远] yuǎn* {A} s.v. far; distant (lit./fig.) | chà de ∼ 差得∼ be far behind | ∼ zài shíyī shìjì ∼在十一世纪 as far back as the 11th century ◆b.f. keep away from; keep at a distance 敬而远之 jìng'éryuǎnzhī
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 远:) From (full form:) 袁 yuán or (simple form:) 元 yuán phonetic, and 辶(辵 chuò) 'go' (to go far).
