120  進(S进) [jìn] enter; go forward; 進步 jìnbù progress
進[进] ¹jìn* {A} v. ①advance ②enter; come/go into ③receive ④eat; drink; take ⑤submit; introduce ◆m. row of rooms separating courtyards ◆b.f. personal ranking 进士 ¹jìnshì ◆adv. into; in
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 进:) The full form 進 is 隹 (zhuī) a short-tailed bird on the 辶 go. In the simple form 进, 隹 has been replaced by 井 jǐng phonetic.
