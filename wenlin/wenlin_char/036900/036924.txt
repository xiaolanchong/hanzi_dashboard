1674 逼 [bī] force, compel; close in on
逼 ¹bī {B} v. ①force | Nǐ ∼ wǒ yě méiyòng. 你∼我也没用。 It's no use to press me. ②press for ③press on towards | Díjūn yǐ ∼jìn chéngmén. 敌军已∼进城门。 The enemy troops have pressed on to the city gate.
From 畐 fú phonetic and 辶(辵 chuò) 'go'.
畐 fú (ancient *piwek) is a phonetic component in 逼 bī (ancient *piek).
