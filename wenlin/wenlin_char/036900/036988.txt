1770 遼(S辽) [liáo] 遼闊 liáokuò vast, extensive; 遼寧 Liáoníng
遼[辽] ⁵liáo {E} b.f. distant 辽远 liáoyuǎn ◆n. ①short name for Liaoning ②Liao (Khitan) dynasty (907-1125)
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 辽:) From (full form:) 尞 liáo or (simple form) 了 liǎo phonetic, and 辶(辵 chuò) 'go'.
The primary meaning is 'faraway'.
