1759 邁(S迈) [mài] step, stride; old
邁[迈] ²mài {B} v. step; stride | ∼ yị̄ dà bù ∼一大步 make a giant step forward ◆b.f. old 老迈 lǎomài ◆m. 〈loan〉 mile
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 迈:) From 万(萬 wàn) 'ten thousand' and 辶(辵 chuò) 'go'.
"March, make a journey, go to; surpass, pass by -- 萬 many 辵 steps" --Karlgren.
