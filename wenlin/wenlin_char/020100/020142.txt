718  亮 [liàng] shine  [liáng]
亮 ¹liàng {A} s.v. ①bright; light | Tiānsè ²biàn de yuèláiyuè ∼ le. 天色变得越来越∼了。 The sky is getting brighter and brighter. ②loud and clear; sonorous ③enlightened ◆v. ①shine ②show
From 京 jīng 'capital' over 儿(人 rén) 'person'. Karlgren says 京 is phonetic. Notice the similarity to 高 gāo 'high' and 亭 tíng 'pavilion'.
