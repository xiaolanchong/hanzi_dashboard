==== 亻 [rén] (radical 9, component: 'person', same as 人; 单人旁 dānrénpáng)
No single-syllable words currently in dictionary.	add new s.s.w.
The form 亻, as it appears in 他個們你 etc., is actually closer to the oldest forms than 人 is. It represents a person seen in profile, the body and legs shown as a vertical line, and a single arm points off to the left side.
