637  云 [yún] say; (F雲) (云彩 yúncai) cloud
云[雲] ¹yún* {A} n. ①cloud ②short name for Yunnan ③Surname
云 ²yún {E} v. 〈wr.〉 say
Picture of clouds. The character 云 was borrowed for the word yún 'say' (now rare), which happened to sound the same, and the full form 雲 was created with 雨 (yǔ) 'rain' added to the top.
