2398 仆 [pū] fall forward  [pú] (F僕) 仆人 púrén servant
仆 ³pū {E} b.f. fall forward 颠仆 diānpū
仆[僕] ¹pú {E} b.f. servant 仆人 púrén*
From 亻(人 rén) 'person' and 卜 bǔ phonetic. For the pronunciation pú, the full form 僕 has 菐 pú as the phonetic part, but the form with 卜 is the original one for the pronunciation pū.
