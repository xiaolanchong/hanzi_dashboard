Sixth of the 214 radicals.
As a stroke type, 亅 is called shùgōu (竖钩) 'vertical with hook'. In the standard system of five stroke types (一 héng, 丨 shù, 丿 piě, 丶 diǎn, and 乛 zhé),  the second type 丨 shù includes 亅 shùgōu. However, in the system of 214 radicals, 亅 jué is a distinct radical from 丨 gǔn.
