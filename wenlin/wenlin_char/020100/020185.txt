1797 仙 [xiān] fairy, saint, immortal celestial being; 仙女 fairy maiden  [xiǎn]
仙 ⁴xiān {E} b.f. celestial being; immortal 仙人 ²xiānrén 神仙 shénxiān
From 亻(人 rén) 'person' and 山 shān phonetic.
山 'mountain' may also suggest the meaning.
“The Taoist Genii... According to the legends, they live on the mountains” --Wieger.
An old variant form is 僊.
