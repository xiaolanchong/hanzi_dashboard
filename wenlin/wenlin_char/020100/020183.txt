1845 仗 [zhàng] battle, war; 打仗 fight
仗 ³zhàng {E} b.f. ①weapons; weaponry 仪仗 ²yízhàng ②hold (a weapon) 仗剑 zhàngjiàn ③rely/depend on 仗势 ²zhàngshì ◆n. 〈wr.〉 battle; war
From 亻(人 rén) 'person' and 丈 zhàng 'ten hand-lengths'.
Cognate with 丈 and 杖 zhàng 'staff'.
"Long spear; weapon, fight; lean on, rely on" --Karlgren.
