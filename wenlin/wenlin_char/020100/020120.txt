==== 亘 [gèn] extend  [xuān] [gèng]
亘 ¹gèn {E} b.f. continuous (in space or time) 亘古 gèngǔ 弥天亘地 mítiāngèndì 盘亘 pángèn
The middle (now 日) used to be 舟 (zhōu) 'boat'. A boat passing from 一 one bank to the 一 other. That is one theory. But Karlgren(1940) shows oracle bone forms of 亘 which look like a spiral. Compare 回 huí and 亙 gèng.
