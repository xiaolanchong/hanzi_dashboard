542  仍 [réng] (仍然 réngrán, 仍旧 réngjiù) still, yet, as before
仍 réng* {B} adv. ①still; yet | Lǎoshī jiǎngle jǐ biàn, wǒ ∼ bù míngbai. 老师讲了几遍, 我∼不明白。 The teacher explained it a few times but I still didn't understand. ②〈wr.〉 again and again; over and over ◆conj. 〈wr.〉 hence ◆b.f. remain 一仍旧贯 yīréngjiùguàn
From 亻(人 rén) 'person' and 乃 (nǎi).
乃 here is a "technical abbreviation for another old character, also of uncertain analysis" --Karlgren.
Compare 扔 rēng 'throw'.
