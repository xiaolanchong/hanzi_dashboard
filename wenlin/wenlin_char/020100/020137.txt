2112 亩(F畝) [mǔ] sixth of an acre (0.0667 hectares)
亩[畝] ²mǔ {B} m. (of area equal to 0.0667 hectare; 1/6 acre)
田 tián 'field' suggest the meaning 'acre'. The full form 畝 has 亠 and 久 added to 田, the interpretation of 亠 and 久 being uncertain.
In the simple form 亩, the component 久 is left out.
A variant form is 畆.
