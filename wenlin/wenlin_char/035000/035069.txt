175  製(S制) [zhì] (製造 zhìzào) manufacture
製[制] ²zhì {C} v. make; manufacture; create
From 制 zhì and 衣 (yī) 'clothing'.
"Cut out (as a dress), fashion; prepare, make; invent, compose -- etymologically the same word as 制; character specifyingly enlarged by 衣" --Karlgren(1923).
Among simple form characters, 制 is used for both 制 and 製.
