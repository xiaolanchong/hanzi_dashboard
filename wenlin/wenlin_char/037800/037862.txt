xxxx 鏦(S𫓩) [cōng] short spear (ancient weapon); 鏦鏦 tinkling of metal
鏦[𫓩] ⁷cōng n. 〈trad.〉 short spear ◆char. 𫓩𫓩 ³cōngcōng 铮𫓩 zhēngcōng
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 𫓩:) From 钅(金 jīn) 'metal' and 从(從) cóng phonetic.
The simplified form is in Guifan Zidian, XHC and NYC, and was added to Unicode 5.2.
