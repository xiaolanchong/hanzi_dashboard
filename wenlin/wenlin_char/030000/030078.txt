==== 畾 [léi] space between fields
No single-syllable words currently in dictionary.	add new s.s.w.
From 田 (tián) 'field' tripled.
"Fields separated by dykes -- 田 tripled" --Karlgren.
"The fields, the country" --Wieger.
畾 originally was part of 累 lèi and 雷 léi, where it has been reduced to a single 田. In 垒(壘 lěi), the simple form has 厶 in place of 田.
