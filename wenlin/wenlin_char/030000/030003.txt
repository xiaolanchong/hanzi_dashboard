939  申 [shēn] explain; 9th 地支 Earthly Branch
申 ⁸shēn {E} n. ①ninth of the twelve earthly branches ②short name for Shanghai ③Surname ◆b.f. state; express; explain 申请 shēnqǐng 重申 chóngshēn 引申义 yǐnshēnyì
"Two hands stretching something 丨 long" --Karlgren.
Now 'stretch' is written 伸 shēn, with 亻 (rén) 'person' added. The meaning 'explain' may have been extended from the meaning 'stretch'.
