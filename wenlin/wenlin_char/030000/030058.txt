番/蕃 ⁵fān b.f. foreign 番茄 fānqié*
番 ²Pān char. 番禺 Pānyú
From 釆 (biàn) 'foot-print' over 田 (tián) 'field'.
The top 釆 is seven strokes, it is not 采 cǎi (eight strokes).
"The 釆 foot-print of a wild animal on 田 the field" --Karlgren.
