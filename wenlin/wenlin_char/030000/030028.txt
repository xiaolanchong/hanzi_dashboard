253  界 [jiè] boundary; 世界 shìjiè world    [gā] [gà]
界 ¹jiè {D} b.f. ①boundary 界限 ¹jièxiàn* ②scope; extent 眼界 yǎnjiè ③circles; group; division 科学界 kēxuéjiè
界 ⁸gā char. 不搭界 bù dāgā
界 ²gà char. 半间不界 bàngānbùgà
From 田 (tián) 'field' and 介 jiè 'introduce'. According to Karlgren, 界 jiè is etymologically the same word as 介 jiè, which originally also meant 'boundary'.
