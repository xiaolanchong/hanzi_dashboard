略 ¹lüè* {B} b.f. ①brief; sketchy 略图 lüètú ②summary; brief account; outline 要略 yàolüè ③strategy; plan; scheme 策略 cèlüè ④omit; delete; leave out 省略 shěnglüè ⑤overlook 忽略 hūlüè ⑥invade 侵略 qīnlüè ⑦capture; seize 攻城略地 gōngchénglüèdì ⑧supervise ◆adv. slightly; cursorily; somewhat | Zhè shìr wǒ ∼ zhī yī-èr. 这事儿我∼知一二。 I know a little something about the matter.
From 田 (tián) 'field' and 各 gè phonetic ("There was an Archaic kl- or gl-" --Karlgren).
The primitive meaning was 'to draw boundary lines', hence 田 'field' (compare 疆 jiāng). Extended meanings: outline, sketch, brief.
