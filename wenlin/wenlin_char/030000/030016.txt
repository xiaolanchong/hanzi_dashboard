==== 畀 [bì] (书) to present, give
畀 ²⁸bì v. 〈wr./topo.〉 give ◆b.f. ①turn over to; abandon to 投畀豺虎 tóubìcháihǔ ②depend on 倚畀 yǐbì
Picture of an arrow, like 矢 shǐ, with a different kind of arrowhead. The arrowhead now looks like 田 tián 'field'. The bottom was re-interpreted as a 丌 table, on which a gift might be presented.
Phonetic in 鼻 bí 'nose' etc.
