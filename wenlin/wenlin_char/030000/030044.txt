2071 畜 [xù] 畜牧 xùmù raise animals  [chù] 畜生 chùsheng beast
畜 ³chù {E} b.f. domestic animal; livestock 家畜 jiāchù 畜生 chùsheng
畜 ⁷xù {E} b.f. raise (domestic animals) 畜牧 ¹xùmù*
From 玄 (xuán) 'profound' and 田 (tián) 'field'.
"Interpretation doubtful because the analysis of 玄 is uncertain; possibly: to keep in one's 玄 house and one's 田 fields" --Karlgren.
(Karlgren speculates that 玄 represents the dark interior of a house.)
