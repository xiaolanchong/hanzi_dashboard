xxxx 畺 [jiāng] (old form of 疆; phonetic in 僵缰礓)  [jiàng]
No single-syllable words currently in dictionary.	add new s.s.w.
The original (?) form was 畕, composed of 田 (tián) 'field' doubled.
畺 has 三 (sān 'three') lines added.
"田 fields with 三 boundary lines marked" --Karlgren(1923).
This character, being too easy to write and understand, was replaced by 疆.
