2289 甸 [diàn] (ancient) suburb; pasture  [diān] 沉甸甸 chéndiāndiān heavy  [tián] [shèng] [yìng]
甸 ⁵diān {E} char. 沉甸甸 chéndiāndiān
甸 ⁸diàn {E} n. 〈wr.〉 ①pasture ②suburb
From 勹 and 田 tián 'field'.
"Imperial domain; to cultivate, govern" --Karlgren.
Cognate with 田 tián and 佃 tián 'rent land'.
Not to be confused with 旬 xún.
