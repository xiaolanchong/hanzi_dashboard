1705 膠(S胶) [jiāo] glue; gum; rubber; stick; sticky  [jiǎo] [háo] [nǎo]
膠[胶] ⁶jiāo {D} n. ①glue; gum ②rubber ③anything sticky ④resin; sap ◆v. glue ◆s.v. ①sticky; gluey ②stubborn
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 胶:) From 月(肉 ròu) 'flesh' and (simple form:) 交 jiāo or (full form:) 翏 liào phonetic.
(Not to be confused with 㬵 xiáo which looks the same but is classified under the 月 moon radical.)
