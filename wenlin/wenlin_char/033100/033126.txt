648  腦(S脑) [nǎo] (腦子 nǎozi) brain; 腦袋 head; 腦殻 skull  [nào]
腦[脑] ¹nǎo {E} n. brain ◆b.f. brain-like material 豆腐脑 dòufunǎo(r)
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 脑:) Originally 𡿺: 巛 hair over 囟 head; then 月(肉 ròu) 'flesh' was added, giving the full form 腦. The right side is 㐫 in the simple form 脑. (A variant form is 匘.) Compare 恼(惱) nǎo (as in 恼火 nǎohuǒ 'irritated').
