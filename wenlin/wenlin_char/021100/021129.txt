787  劉(S刘) [Liú] (surname)
劉[刘] Liú {E} n. Surname ◆char. 刘海 liúhǎi(r)* 虔刘 ²qiánliú
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 刘:) The full form 劉 is composed of (卯) mǎo phonetic (compare 留 liú), 金 (jīn) 'metal' and 刂(刀 dāo) 'knife' (apparently not related to 釗 zhāo 'encourage'). In the simple form 刘, the left side has been replaced by 文 (wén).
