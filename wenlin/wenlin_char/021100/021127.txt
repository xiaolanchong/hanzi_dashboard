805  劇(S剧) [jù] drama, play, opera; 劇烈 jùliè severe
劇[剧] ⁴jù {C} n. theatrical work; drama; play; opera ◆b.f. acute; severe; intense 剧烈 jùliè
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 剧:) The left side is (full form:) 豦 jù or (simple form:) 居 jū phonetic. The right side is 刂(刀 dāo) 'knife'.
(Karlgren says 豦 was co-signific in 劇: "Painful, intense; very; sport -- 豦 fight and 刀 sword".)
