521  功 [gōng] achievement; 成功 chénggōng succeed; 功夫 gōngfu
功 ⁶gōng {D} n. meritorious service; exploit ◆b.f. ①achievement; result; effect 成功 chénggōng ②skill 功夫 ²gōngfu
From 工 gōng 'work' and 力 (lì) 'strength'.
"Work done, achievement; meritorious deed, merit; effort -- etymologically the same word as 工; character enlarged by 力" --Karlgren.
Compare 攻 gōng 'attack'.
