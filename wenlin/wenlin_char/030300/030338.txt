2629 皂 [zào] 肥皂 féizào soap
皂 ⁶zào {E} b.f. ①black 皂鞋 ²zàoxié 皂白 zào-bái ②soap 肥皂 féizào 香皂 xiāngzào ③yamen runner 皂隶 ¹zàolì ④honey locust 皂刺 ²zàocì
"Herbs used for black-dyeing; black; (black-clothed:) servant, lictor, yamen-runner -- same character [as 草 cǎo] more or less deformed" --Karlgren.
The bottom is 七 (qī), not 匕 or 𠤎. A variant form is 皁. Compare 皀.
