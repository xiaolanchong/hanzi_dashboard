---- 癸 [guǐ] 10th 天干
癸 ⁵guǐ n. the last of the 10 Heavenly Stems
"The nicely disposed grass, on which the Ancients poured the libations offered to the Manes; see Graphies, page 362. This character, not easily written, was replaced, in the days of 李斯 Lǐsī, by 癶 plus 矢, probably the primitive form of 发 fā. The modern form has been arbitrarily mutilated by the scribes. Now 假借 jiǎjiè a cyclical character" --Wieger.
