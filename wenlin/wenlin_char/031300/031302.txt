2203 穆 [mù] 静穆 jīngmù solemn and quiet; (surname); 穆罕默德 Mohammed
穆 ⁹mù {E} b.f. respectful; dignified 肃穆 sùmù* 穆卜 mùbǔ
The right side is 白 over 小 over 彡, an obsolete character mù, used in 穆 as a phonetic. It meant "Fine lines, strokes -- from 彡 and 𡭴 fissure abbreviated" --Karlgren. "Striped; 细文也。从彡, 从𡭴省, 会意。...It forms 穆 mù, the waving of grain; 转注 zhuǎnzhù, grace, amenity" --Wieger.
"Nice, agreeable; virtuous, solemn, reverent; profound, mysterious; transcription of the first syllable in Mohammed" --Karlgren.
