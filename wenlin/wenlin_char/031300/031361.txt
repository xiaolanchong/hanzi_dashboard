680  突 [tū] charge forward; stick out; 突然 tūrán suddenly
突 ²tū {E} b.f. ①dash forward 突击 tūjī* ②project; stick out 突出 ¹tūchū* ③〈trad.〉 chimney 灶突 zàotū ◆adv. suddenly; abruptly
From 穴 (xué) 'hole' over 犬 (quǎn) 'dog'.
A 犬 dog suddenly charging into a 穴 hole, to catch a rabbit. Since the dog can't fit all the way into the rabbit hole, its tail sticks out.
