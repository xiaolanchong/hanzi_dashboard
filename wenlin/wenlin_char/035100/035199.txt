191  西 [xī] west; 东西 dōngxi thing
西 ¹xī* {A} n. west ◆b.f. Occidental; Western 西装 ¹xīzhuāng* 西式 ¹xīshì
"Image of a bird sitting on its nest... the West, for the birds go to roost when the sun is setting" --Wieger.
Compare 栖 qī '(of birds) to perch'.
The components 覀 and 襾 generally have different origins from 西 xī, though in traditional dictionaries they are treated as being the same.
