---- 褪 [tùn] slip out of   [tuì] take off, shed; fade
褪 ²tuì {E} v. ①shed (clothes/feathers/etc.) ②fade ③〈topo.〉 hide sth. in one's sleeve
褪 tùn v. slip out of sth.
From 衤(衣 yī) 'clothing' and 退 tuì 'retreat'.
"Draw the arm into the sleeve, draw in, take away; 退 withdraw inside the 衣 clothes" --Karlgren.
