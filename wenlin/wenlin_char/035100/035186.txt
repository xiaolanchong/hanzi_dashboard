1773 襲(S袭) [xí] make a surprise attack; carry on as before
襲[袭] ²xí {E} b.f. ①make a surprise attack on 袭击 ¹xíjī* ②carry on as before 沿袭 ²yánxí ③plagiarize 抄袭 ¹chāoxí* ④receive a hereditary rank 世袭 shìxí ◆m. (for clothes)
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 袭:) From 龙(龍 lóng) 'dragon' over 衣 (yī) 'clothing'.
"Court dress; lined garment, double; repeated; hereditary, inherit -- an 龍 (imperial) court 衣 dress" --Karlgren.
