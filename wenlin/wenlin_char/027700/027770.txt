341  決(S决) [jué] 解決 jiějué resolve; 決定 juédìng decide  [quē] [xuè]
決[决] ¹jué* {B} b.f. ①decide; determine 决定 juédìng* ②execute sb. 处决 chǔjué* ③bid farewell to ④sever relations 决裂 juéliè ⑤be breached; burst (of dikes/etc.) 决口 ¹juékǒu ⑥dredge ◆adv. definitely; certainly
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 决:) From 冫 or 氵 (水 shuǐ) 'water' and 夬 guài 'break through'.
"Bifurcation of a river; rupture, break off, cut off, decide; certainly..." --Karlgren.
Cognate with 夬 guài (name of the hexagram ䷪).
