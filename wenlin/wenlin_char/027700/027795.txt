==== 沓 [tà] piled up; repeated  [dá] (measure word for piles of paper etc.)
沓 ⁵tà {E} b.f. numerous; repeated; crowded 沓合 tàhé 飒沓 sàtà
沓 ⁴dá m. pad; pile (of paper/etc.)
From 水 (shuǐ) 'water' over and 曰 (yuē) 'say'.
According to 说文, the ancient meaning was 'to talk a lot': 語多沓沓也.
"Gabble, babble; to join -- a 水 stream of 曰 words" --Karlgren.
