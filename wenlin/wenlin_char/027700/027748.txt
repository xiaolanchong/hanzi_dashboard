1427 汤(F湯) [tāng] hot water, soup  [shāng] 汤汤
汤[湯] ¹tāng {A} n. ①hot/boiling water ②hot springs ③soup; broth ④decoction ⑤Surname
汤[湯] ⁷shāng char. 汤汤 shāngshāng
From 氵(水 shuǐ) 'water' and 𠃓(昜) yáng phonetic.
Compare the related word 烫 tàng 'boiling hot', which is pronounced in the falling tone and is written with 火 (huǒ) 'fire' below.
汤 is phonetic in 荡 dàng 'swing'.
