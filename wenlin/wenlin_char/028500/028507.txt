1560 潛(S潜) [qián] 潛水艇 submarine; 潛力 latent ability
潛[潜] ⁴qián {E} v. dive; submerge ◆b.f. ①hidden 潜伏 qiánfú ②in secret; stealthily 潜逃 qiántáo* ③latent 潜力 ¹qiánlì*
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 潜:) From 氵(水 shuǐ) 'water' and 朁 zǎn phonetic, abbreviated to 替 (tì) in the simple form 潜.
Primitive meaning: hide under water.
