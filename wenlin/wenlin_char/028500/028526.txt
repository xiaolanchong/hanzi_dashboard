1078 潮 [cháo] tide; (潮湿 cháoshī) moist; 高潮 gāocháo climax
潮 ²cháo {C} n./b.f. ①tide ②(social) upsurge; current ◆s.v. ①trendy; chic; fashionable ②damp; moist ③inferior; low grade ④unskillful
From 氵(水 shuǐ) 'water' and 朝 cháo zhāo 'morning'.
潮 originally meant 'morning tide', so 朝 and 潮 are cognate words. Compare 汐 xī 'night tide': 潮汐 cháoxī 'morning and evening tides'.
