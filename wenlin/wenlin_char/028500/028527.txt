==== 潯(S浔) [xún] water margin  [yín]
No single-syllable words currently in dictionary.	add new s.s.w.
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 浔:) From 氵(水 shuǐ) 'water' and 寻(尋) xún phonetic.
Formerly also used to write the word 海寻 hǎixún 'nautical fathom' as a single character. (Now written 英寻.)
