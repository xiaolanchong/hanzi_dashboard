---- 潸 [shān] tearful
潸 ¹²shān b.f. shed tears 潸然 shānrán 泪潸潸 lèishānshān
The left side 氵(水 shuǐ) 'water' suggests the meaning 'tearful'.
The right side is 林 (lín) over 月(肉 ròu). It was originally the same as the left side of 散 sàn ('break up'). 散 sàn (or the left side of it) is said to be phonetic in 潸 shān.
