---- 豳 [Bīn] (古地名) (old name of a place in 陝西)  [bān]
彬/邠/豳 Bīn b.f. name of a xiàn in Shaanxi; ancient home of the founder of the Zhou dynasty
From 豕 (shǐ) 'pig' (doubled) and 山 (shān) 'mountain'.
"A district in the 山 mountains of 陝西 Shaanxi, where 豕 boars formerly abounded" --Wieger.
Also written 邠.
Compare 幽 yōu.
