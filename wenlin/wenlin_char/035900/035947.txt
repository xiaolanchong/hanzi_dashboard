2185 豫 [yù] ䷏ (16) Enthusiasm; 犹豫 yóuyù hesitate  [xiè] [shū]
预[預/豫] ⁹yù {E} b.f. in advance 预备 yùbèi* 预测 yùcè 预报 yùbào*
豫 ¹¹yù {E} b.f. ①pleased; content; happy; comfortable 逸豫 ⁴yìyù 悦豫 ²yuèyù ②excursion 秋豫 ²qiūyù ③hesitate 犹豫 yóuyù*
豫 Yù n. ①short name for Henan ②ancient name for Nanchang
From 予 yù phonetic and 象 (xiàng) 'elephant'.
Karlgren(1923) treats 豫 as simply a variant for 预(預) yù (as in 预备 'prepare'). Mathews treats 豫 as standard and treats 預 as simply a variant of 豫. In the modern Mainland standard, 豫 is now mainly limited to the meanings 'comfortable' and 'happy'.
