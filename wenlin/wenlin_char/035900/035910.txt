1140 豆 [dòu] bean; 豆腐 dòufu tofu; 大豆 soybean; 豌豆 pea
豆 ¹dòu {E} n. ①legumes; pulses; beans; peas ②ancient stemmed cup ③hemispherical fruit bowl
Picture of a sacrificial vessel, or dish for food. Borrowed for dòu meaning 'bean'. In 登 dēng, however, 豆 represents a pedestal.
豆 is the 151st of the 214 radicals.
