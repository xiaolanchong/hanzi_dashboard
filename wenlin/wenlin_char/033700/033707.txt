1029 莫 [mò] nothing; not; 莫大 mòdà greatest; 莫名其妙 baffling  [mù]
莫 ⁴mò {D} adv. 〈wr.〉 not; be/do not ◆n. Surname
From 艹(草 cǎo) 'grass', 日(rì) 'sun' and 大 (dà).
The 日 sun fading away at the horizon, in the 艹大 plants (the bottom was originally also 艹(草 cǎo) 'grass', not 大 'big'). This meaning is preserved in the word 暮色 mùsè 'twilight' (莫 mò and 暮 mù are related).
The similarity of 没 mò 'disappear' and 莫 mò 'not exist', must be coincidental -- the old sounds were different: 没 *muet and 莫 *mak.
