163  華(S华) [huá] magnificent; China    [Huà] (surname); 華山  [huā] [kuā]
華[华] ⁴huá {E} b.f. ①glory; splendor 光华 guānghuá ②essence 精华 ¹jīnghuá* ③China 华北 Huáběi* ④gray (of hair) 华发 huáfà ◆attr. 〈court./wr.〉 your
华[華] Huà n. Surname ◆b.f. name of a mountain in Shaanxi 华山 Huà Shān*
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 华:) "Etymologically the same word as 花; analysis of character uncertain, perhaps simply a picture of a luxuriant tree" --Karlgren(1923).
The simple form 华 is 化 huà phonetic over 十, which is just the bottom two strokes of the full form 華.
Compare 毕(畢) bì.
