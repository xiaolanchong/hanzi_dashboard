---- 菸 [yū] fade, wither  [yān] (=烟) tobacco  [yù]
烟[煙/菸] ¹yān {C} n. ①smoke ②mist; vapor ③tobacco; cigarette ④opium ◆v. be irritated by smoke
菸 ⁵yū b.f. withered/dry leaves 菸邑 yūyì
From 艹(草 cǎo) 'grass' and 於 yú phonetic.
"Faded leaves, fade; (because of this sense the character has been applied to and is chiefly used for 煙, 烟:) (yān) tobacco, smoke" --Karlgren(1923).
