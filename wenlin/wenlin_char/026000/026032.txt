135  新 [xīn] new; 重新 chóngxīn again; 新闻 xīnwén news
新 ¹xīn* {A} s.v. new; fresh; up-to-date | Nǐ kànle nàge ∼ diànyǐng le ma? 你看了那个∼电影了吗? Have you seen that new movie? ◆b.f. ①renew 翻新 fānxīn* ②newly married 新人 xīnrén ◆n. Xin dynasty (9-24 A.D.) ◆ab. Xīnjiāng
From 亲 qīn phonetic and 斤 (jīn) 'axe'.
亲 qīn (as in 母亲 mǔqīn 'mother') is from 辛 xīn phonetic and 木 (mù) 'tree'.
斤 jīn 'axe' suggests the meaning 'new' because you chop wood to make something new.
