845  施 [shī] carry out; 措施 cuòshī measure, step; 施工 construction  [yì] [shǐ]
施 ⁵shī {D} b.f. ①execute; carry out 实施 ¹shíshī ②bestow; give 施舍 shīshě* ③use; apply 施肥 shīféi 施粉 shīfěn ◆v. exert; impose | shī yālì 施压力 exert pressure ◆n. Surname
From  (yǎn) 'flag' and 也 yě phonetic.
 depicts a flag; it is also a component in 旗 qí 'flag', 族 zú 'nationality', etc. It has no relation to 方 fāng.
Compare 拖 tuō 'drag', composed of 扌 and 㐌.
