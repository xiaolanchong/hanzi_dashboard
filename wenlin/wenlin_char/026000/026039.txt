534  斷(S断) [duàn] cut off; 不斷 búduàn unceasing 判斷 pànduàn judge
斷[断] ²duàn {B} v. ①break ②cut off ③give up ④decide ◆adv. 〈wr.〉 absolutely; decidedly
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 断:) The left side (㡭) is the old form of 继(繼) jì 'continue'; it represents lots of silk threads. 斤 (jīn) 'axe' severs the connection.
