2077 旨 [zhǐ] 意旨 yìzhǐ intention
旨 ⁵zhǐ {E} b.f. ①meaning; intention; aim 主旨 zhụ̌zhǐ ②decree; edict 圣旨 shèngzhǐ ③〈wr.〉 delicious 甘旨 gānzhǐ 旨酒 zhǐjiǔ
From 匕 (bǐ) 'spoon' and 日 (rì) (originally 甘 gān 'sweet').
"Good-tasting, good, nice, excellent; aspire to, purport, wish, will; emperor's will, imperial decree -- the seal has 匕 spoon and 甘 sweet, the latter now corrupted" --Karlgren.
