==== 旡 [jì] (component: 'belch' -- 欠 backwards; as in 既簪 etc.)
No single-syllable words currently in dictionary.	add new s.s.w.
"To belch -- the seal was a symbol analogous to those of 气 and 欠; to be distinguished from 无" --Karlgren.
A very similar character was pronounced zān and meant hair-pin; it is now written 簪 zān; also see 昝 zǎn.
