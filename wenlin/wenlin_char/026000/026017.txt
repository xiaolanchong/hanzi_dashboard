---- 斡 [wò] 斡旋 wòxuán mediate; good offices  [guǎn]
斡 ⁴wò {E} b.f. revolve; spin; rotate 斡旋 wòxuán*
From 倝(乾) qián and 斗 (dǒu) 'ladle'. 倝(乾) qián was phonetic in 斡, but then the character 斡 was borrowed for a different word with a different pronunciation.
"To conduct, to direct; (for unknown reasons applied to a word... (wò) to revolve, turn; a handle" --Karlgren.
