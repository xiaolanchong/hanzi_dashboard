827  旅 [lǚ] travel; 旅馆 inn; 旅行社 travel agency; ䷷
旅 ²lǚ {D} b.f. ①travel; stay away from home 旅行 ¹lǚxíng* ②troops; force 军旅 jūnlǚ* ◆n. 〈mil.〉 brigade
From  (yǎn) 'flag' and , which was originally 从 two people gathered beneath the flag. (No relation to 派 pài.)
 is also a component in 旗 qí 'flag', 族 zú 'nationality', 施 shī 'carry out', etc. It has no relation to 方 fāng.
