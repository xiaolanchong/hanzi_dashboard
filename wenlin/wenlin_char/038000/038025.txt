2097 钉(F釘) [dīng] a nail; to follow closely  [dìng] to nail
钉[釘] ²dīng {C} n. nail; tack ◆v. ①follow closely; tail ②urge; press ③gaze at
钉[釘] ³dìng {D} v. ①nail ②sew on
From 钅(金 jīn) 'metal' and 丁 dīng.
丁 is a picture of a nail. When 丁 was borrowed to be the fourth of the ten 天干 tiāngān heavenly stems, the character 钉 was created to represent the original meaning 'nail'.
