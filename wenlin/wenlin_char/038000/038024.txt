1156 针(F針) [zhēn] needle; 方针 policy; 针刺 zhēncì acupuncture
针[針/鍼] ²zhēn {B} n. ①needle; pin ②stitch ③injection; shot ◆b.f. acupuncture 针灸 ¹zhēnjiǔ*
From 钅(金 jīn) 'metal' and 十. Here 十 is not shí 'ten', but a depiction of a threaded needle.
Variant forms are 鍼 (with 咸 xián 'bite') and 箴 (with 竹 zhú 'bamboo').
