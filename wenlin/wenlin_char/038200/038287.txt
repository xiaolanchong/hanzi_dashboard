---- 閏(S闰) [rùn] intercalary; 閏年 rùnnián leap year
閏[闰] ²rùn {E} b.f. intercalary 闰月 rùnyuè
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 闰:) From 门(門 mén) 'door' and 王 (wáng) 'king'.
"Intercalary (moon) -- during the intercalary moon the 王 king sat in the 門 gate, not in the hall, says 说文 Shuōwén. Or was 壬 ... phonetic ...?" --Karlgren.
