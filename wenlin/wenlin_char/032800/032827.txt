2251 耻(F恥) [chǐ] 耻辱 chǐrǔ shame, humiliation; 可耻 shameful; 无耻 shameless
耻[恥] ³chǐ {E} b.f. shame; disgrace; humiliation 耻辱 chị̌rǔ*
From 耳 (ěr) 'ear' and (simple form:) 止 zhǐ phonetic or (full form:) 心 (xīn) 'heart'.
Does 耳 'ear' mean you are ashamed when people HEAR about your misbehavior, or that your ears turn red?
