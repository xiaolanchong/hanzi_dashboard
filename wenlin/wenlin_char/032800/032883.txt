2386 聳(S耸) [sǒng] towering, lofty; alarm, shock; 高聳 gāosǒng stand tall
聳[耸] ¹sǒng {D} b.f. ①be towering/lofty 耸立 ¹sǒnglì* ②arouse attention; alarm; shock 耸人听闻 sǒngréntīngwén ③shrug 耸肩 sǒngjiān*
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 耸:) From 从(從) cóng phonetic over 耳 (ěr) 'ear'.
"To deafen, excite, arouse; ambitious; lofty, high" --Karlgren.
According to Karlgren, 耸 is etymologically the same word as 怂 sǒng.
