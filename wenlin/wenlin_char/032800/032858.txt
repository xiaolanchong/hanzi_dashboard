1143 聚 [jù] gather, get together; 核聚变 nuclear fusion
聚 ⁸jù {C} v. assemble; gather; get together
The top is 取 qǔ phonetic. The bottom 乑 is a variant of 众(眾 zhòng) 'crowd', depicting three 人 (rén) people. The middle person is 亻, the person on the left is two 丿 strokes, and the one on the right is 丿 attached to .
