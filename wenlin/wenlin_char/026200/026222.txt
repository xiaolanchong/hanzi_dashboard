765  普 [pǔ] 普通 pǔtōng common; 普遍 pǔbiàn universal
普 ²pǔ {E} b.f. common; widespread; popularize 普通 pǔtōng* 科普 kēpǔ ◆n. Surname ◆char. 吉普车 jípǔchē
From 並 (bìng) 'together' over 日 (rì) 'sun'. 並 is a variant form of 并 bìng, two people 立 (lì) standing side by side.
"Universal, all, vast, great -- 並 taken together all the places where 日 the sun shines; cf. the phrase 普天下" --Karlgren(1923).
