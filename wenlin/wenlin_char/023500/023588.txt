1118 尤 [yóu] fault; outstanding; 尤其 yóuqí especially
尤 ⁵yóu {E} adv. especially; particularly ◆b.f. ①fault; wrongdoing 效尤 xiàoyóu ②blame; indict 怨天尤人 yuàntiānyóurén ◆n. Surname
“Fault, evil; transgress; still more, furthermore; extraordinary; (find fault:) grudge --- a deformed 大 man? Cf. 尢 wāng” --Karlgren.
By far the most common usage of the character 尤 yóu is in the word 尤其 yóuqí 'especially'.
尤 is a component in 优(優) yōu 'excellent'.
Don't confuse 尤 with 龙(龍) lóng 'dragon'.
