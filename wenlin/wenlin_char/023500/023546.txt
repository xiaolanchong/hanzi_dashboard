1861 寺 [sì] temple, Buddhist monastery  [shì]
寺 ³sì {D} b.f. temple 寺庙 sìmiào
"From 寸 hand (in the 'thumb' variation) signific and 之 zhī phonetic, here distorted into 土" --Karlgren.
There are lots of chores to do in a temple, hence the 寸 (cùn) 'hand' component. The priests of 少林寺 Shǎolínsì are known for making especially effective use of their hands.
