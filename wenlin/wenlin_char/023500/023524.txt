---- 寤 [wù] (書) awake
悟/寤 ⁴wù {D} v. realize; awaken ◆b.f. (come to) understand 领悟 lǐngwù* 悟性 wùxing
寤 ¹²wù b.f. awake 寤寐 wùmèi 寤觉 wùjué
From 宀 (mián) 'roof', 爿 (qiáng) 'bed', and 吾 wú phonetic.
Etymologically the same word as 悟 wù 'realize, awaken' and 晤 wù 'meet' (according to Karlgren).
Compare 寢 qǐn and 寐 mèi.
