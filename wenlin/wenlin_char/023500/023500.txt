692  富 [fù] (富有 fùyǒu) rich; 丰富 fēngfù abundant; 财富 wealth
富 ⁴fù {B} s.v. wealthy; abundant | Tāmen bǎ ∼ rìzi dāng qióng rìzi guò. 他们把∼日子当穷日子过。 They are thrifty even in days of affluence. ◆v. become wealthy ◆n. Surname
From 宀 (mián) 'roof' and an obsolete character 畐 fú 'abundance', which may have been a cognate word to 富 fù 'rich'. Compare 福 fú 'blessing', etc.
