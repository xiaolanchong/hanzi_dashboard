1166 尖 [jiān] point, tip; 尖锐 sharp
尖 ²jiān(r) {B} n. ①point; tip; top ②roadside meal ③〈coll.〉 cream of the crop ◆s.v. sharp
From 小 (xiǎo) 'small' and 大 (dà) 'big'.
A 大 big thing that gets 小 small on top: tip, point.
The hook on 小 is usually omitted: the middle stroke is 丨 not 亅.
Compare 尘 chén 'dust'.
