1058 寻(F尋) [xún] (寻找 xúnzhǎo) seek   [xín] 寻思 xínsi
寻[尋] ¹xún* {C} v. search; seek ◆b.f. ordinary 寻常 ¹xúncháng* ◆m. (of length equal to about eight ¹chǐ)
寻[尋] xín {E} See ¹xún
The full form is 尋: 彐 over 工 and 口 over 寸.
", 寸 two hands working with 工, 口 instruments for unraveling thread: unravel, investigate" --Karlgren.
工 and 口 are omitted in the simple form 寻.
