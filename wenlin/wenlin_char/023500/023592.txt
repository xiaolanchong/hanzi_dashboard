==== 尨 [máng] shaggy dog; variegated  [páng] (=庞)  [méng] 尨茸
尨 ⁶máng b.f. ①long-haired dog; shaggy 尨眉 mángméi ②variegated 尨杂 mángzá
From 犬 (quǎn) 'dog' and 彡 (shān) 'hair'.
"A 彡 shaggy 犬 dog" --Wieger.
"Shaggy-haired dog; variegated -- 犬 dog with 彡 shag" --Karlgren(1923).
"Blended; variegated; striped" --Mathews.
