2793 寝(F寢) [qǐn] sleep; bedroom; imperial tomb
寝[寢] qǐn {E} b.f. ①sleep 就寝 jiùqǐn* ②bedroom 寝室 qǐnshì ③〈wr.〉 stop; end ④imperial tomb/mausoleum
From 宀 (mián) 'roof', 丬(爿 qiáng) 'bed' and 𠬶 qīn phonetic.
"To sleep; sleeping place, dwelling room; back room of an ancestral hall -- signific: a 爿 bed in 宀 the house" --Karlgren.
Compare 寤 wù and 寐 mèi.
