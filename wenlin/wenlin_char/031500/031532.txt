148  第 [dì] number, -th; 第三世界 Third World; 第一次 1st time
第 ¹dì* {A} pref. marker of ordinal numerals | dì-yī 第一 first | dì-èr 第二 second ◆b.f. ①sequence; order 次第 cìdì* ②rank; grade; degree 品第 pǐndì ③mansion; residence 宅第 ¹zháidì
From 竹 (zhú) 'bamboo' and 弟 dì ('brother') phonetic. 弟 is slightly abbreviated, missing 丷. 竹 probably stands for 竹简 zhújiǎn, the bamboo strips used in ancient times for writing on. Hence, record keeping, sequence, order. Compare 等 déng.
