From 金 'metal' and 失 shī.
The standard full form is 鐵, and the simple form is 铁. Obviously 铁 is derived from 鉄. 鉄 is not a modern simplification, but an old character (formed independently of 鐵), from 金 metal and 失 shī phonetic.
