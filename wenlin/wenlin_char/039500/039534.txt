---- 驮(F馱) [tuó] carry on the back  [duò] 驮子 duòzi pack animal's load
驮[馱] ¹tuó {C} v. carry on the back
驮[馱] ⁷duò b.f. pack a load on a pack animal 驮子 duòzi 骡驮子 luóduòzi
From 马(馬 mǎ) 'horse' and 大 dà phonetic.
Etymologically the same word as 驼 as in 骆驼 luòtuo 'camel'; don't confuse the two characters 驮 and 驼.
