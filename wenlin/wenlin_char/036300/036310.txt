==== 跖 [zhí] metatarsus
跖 Zhí b.f. short for Dào Zhí, a notorious brigand of ancient times 跖狗吠尧 zhígǒufèiyáo
跖/蹠 ¹¹zhí b.f. sole of the foot; metatarsus 跖骨 ²zhígǔ 鸭跖草 yāzhícǎo 高掌远跖 gāozhǎngyuǎnzhí
From ⻊(足 zú) 'foot' and 石 shí phonetic.
"Sole of the foot; tread on" --Karlgren.
A variant form 蹠 has 庶 shù in place of 石.
