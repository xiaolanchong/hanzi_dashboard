1786 嘗(S尝) [cháng] taste
嘗/嚐/甞[尝] ⁴cháng {B} v. ①taste ②experience; come to know | ∼dàole xuéxí de tiántou ∼到了学习的甜头 come to know the benefits of study ◆adv. ever; once
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 尝:) From 尚 shàng phonetic over 旨 (zhǐ) something good-tasting.
In the simple form 尝, the whole lower part under 龸 is reduced to 云 (yún 'cloud'). Variant forms are 嚐 and 甞.
