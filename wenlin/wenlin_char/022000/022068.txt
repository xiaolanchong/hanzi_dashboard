932  嘴 [zuǐ] (嘴巴 zuǐba) mouth; 嘴唇 zuǐchún lip
嘴/觜 zuǐ {A} n. mouth; snout; bill ◆v. talk; speak | Bié duō ∼. 别多∼。 Shut up!
Originally 觜, from 此 cǐ phonetic and 角 (jiǎo) 'horn'. The primitive meaning was a bird's beak, which resembles a horn. 口 (kǒu) 'mouth' was added to clarify the extended meaning, which is 'mouth'.
An old variant character is 咀 zuǐ.
