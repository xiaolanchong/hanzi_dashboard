==== 嘌 [piào] 嘌呤 piàolíng purine  [piāo] fast, speedy
嘌 ⁴piào char. 嘌呤 piàolìng
From 口 and 票.
Hanyu Da Zidian gives the first tone reading for the archaic meaning "fast, speedy" (疾速), and the fourth tone reading only for 嘌呤 piàolíng 'purine'. Guifan Zidian, on the other hand, gives only the falling tone and the meaning 疾速.
