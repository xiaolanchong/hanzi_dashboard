---- 虞 [yú] (书) prediction; anxiety; deceive
虞 ¹⁶yú {E} b.f. ①predict 不虞 ¹bùyú ②worry; anxiety 无虞 ²wúyú ③cheat; deceive 尔虞我诈 ěryúwǒzhà ◆n. ①legendary dynasty ②a state during the Zhou dynasty ③Surname
From 虍(虎 hǔ) 'tiger' and 吴 wú phonetic.
(There does not seem to be a corresponding form in Unicode with 吳 in place of 吴.)
