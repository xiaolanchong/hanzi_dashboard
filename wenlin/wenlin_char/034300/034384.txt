---- 虐 [nüè] cruel; 虐待 mistreat
虐 ²nüè {E} b.f. cruel; tyrannical 虐待 nüèdài 暴虐 bàonüè
From 虍(虎 hǔ) 'tiger' and 爪 (zhuǎ) 'claws' (sideways).
"Cruelly destroy; oppress -- the seal shows the lower part to be 爪, here turned on the side; the 爪 claws of the 虍 tiger" --Karlgren.
Etymologically the same word as 疟(瘧) nüè 'fever'.
