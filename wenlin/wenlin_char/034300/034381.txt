==== 虍 [hǔ] (radical 141, component: same as 虎 hǔ 'tiger'; 虎字头)  [hū]
No single-syllable words currently in dictionary.	add new s.s.w.
"Originally a picture of a tiger, then considered as a picture of its striped skin" --Karlgren.
See 虎 hǔ for a picture and explanation. 虍 hǔ is phonetic in 虚 xū 'empty', and probably also phonetic in 虑 lǜ 'consider', "...in which case" says Karlgren, "there must have been an Archaic guttural + l".
虍 is the 141st of the 214 radicals.
