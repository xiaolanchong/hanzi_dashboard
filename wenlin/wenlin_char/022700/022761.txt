2649 壩(S坝) [bà] dam; dyke, embankment
壩[坝] ⁵bà {C} n. ①dam ②dike; embankment | Zhè ⁴zuò ∼ shì xīn xiū de. 这座∼是新修的。 This is a newly built dam. ③〈topo.〉 sandbar
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 坝:) From 土 (tǔ) 'earth' and (full form:) 霸 bà phonetic or (simple form:) 贝(貝) bèi phonetic.
The form 垻 is included in the eleventh century dictionary 廣韻.
