==== 壴 [zhù] (drum; component in 鼓喜嘉彭尌)
No common single-syllable words.
Picture of a 口 drum on a stand, with a 士 decoration on top.
壴 no longer occurs as a stand-alone character. It is a signific component in 鼓 gǔ 'drum', 喜 xǐ 'happiness', 嘉 jiā 'good', and 彭 Péng (a surname). 壴 is a phonetic component in 尌 shù, which in turn is phonetic in 樹 shù 'tree'.
