---- 龔(S龚) [Gōng] (a surname)
龔[龚] Gōng n. Surname
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 龚:) From 龙(龍) lóng ('dragon') phonetic and 共 (gòng) 'to share'.
Said to be etymologically the same word as 恭 gōng 'respect' and 供 gōng 'supply'.
An older form is 龏, with 廾 (gǒng) 'two hands' at the bottom.
