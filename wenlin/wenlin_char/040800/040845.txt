856  龍(S龙) [lóng] dragon; 恐龍 kǒnglóng dinosaur  [máng]
龍[龙] ¹lóng* {B} n. ①dragon ②Surname ◆b.f. ①imperial 龙袍 lóngpáo ②dinosaur 恐龙 kǒnglóng
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 龙:) "The dragon. When it ascends to heaven and flies, it rains; when it hides in the wells, there is a drought. Vapors and clouds personified." --Wieger.
Don't confuse the simple form 龙 with 尤 yóu (as in 尤其 yóuqí 'especially').
