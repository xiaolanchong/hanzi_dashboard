1582 陶 [táo] pottery; 陶瓷 táocí ceramics  [yáo] [dào]
陶 ⁴táo {E} b.f. ①earthenware 陶瓷 táocí* ②cultivate 熏陶 xūntáo* ③happy; happy-go-lucky 陶然 táorán* 陶醉 ¹táozuì ◆n. Surname
Originally 匋, from 勹 (bāo) 'wrap' and 缶 (fǒu) 'jar'.
"Kiln; earthenware, pottery... 勹 enclosure for making 缶 earthenware" --Karlgren.
From 阝(阜 fù) 'hill' was added later.
