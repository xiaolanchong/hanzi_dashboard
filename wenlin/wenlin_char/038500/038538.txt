257  隊(S队) [duì] team; 部隊 bùduì army; 隊伍 duìwǔ troops  [zhuì] [suì]
隊[队] ²duì {B} n./m. ①a row of people; line ②team; group ③detachment; contingent (of soldiers/policemen/etc.)
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 队:) The left side is 阝(阜 fù) 'hill'. The simple form 队 has 人 (rén) 'person' where the full form 隊 has 㒸 suì, phonetic.
