1475 陷 [xiàn] pit, trap; cave in; 陷入 sink/fall into
陷 ⁵xiàn {C} v. ①fall into; get stuck or bogged down ②become sunken; cave in ◆b.f. ①frame/trump (up) 诬陷 wūxiàn ②be captured; fall (of a town/etc.) 沦陷 lúnxiàn ③pitfall; trap 陷阱 xiànjǐng* ④defect; deficiency 缺陷 quēxiàn
From 阝(阜 fù) 'hill' and 臽, which is ⺈(人 rén) 'person' over 臼 (jiù).
"人 man falling into a 臼 pitfall...enlarged by 阜" --Karlgren.
Compare 馅掐阎谄.
