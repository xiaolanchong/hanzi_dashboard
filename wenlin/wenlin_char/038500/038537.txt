==== 隉(S陧) [niè] 阢隉 wùniè (書) unstable, unpeaceful
隉[陧] ¹²niè char. 阢陧不安 wùnièbù'ān
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 陧:) From 阝(阜 fù) 'hill' and 圼 niè phonetic.
The full form 隉 was current already when 说文 was written, though 陧 was the original form according to 正字通. 隉 is described as containing 毁 (huǐ) 'destroy', abbreviated.
