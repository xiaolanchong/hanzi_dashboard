108  隻(S只) [zhī] (measure word): 一隻貓 a cat  [huò]
隻[只] ²zhī {A} m. (for animals, vessels, some utensils, one of a pair of things) ◆b.f. single 只字未提 zhīzìwèití
From 隹 (zhuī) 'bird' over 又 (yòu) 'hand'.
"Single bird; one of a pair ...single piece" --Karlgren.
Compare 雙(双) shuāng, a pair of birds.
Among simple form characters, 只 is used instead of 隻.
