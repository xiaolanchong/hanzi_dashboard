2319 陵 [líng] hill, mound; tomb; 十三陵 the 13 Ming tombs
陵 ⁵líng {E} b.f. ①hill; mound 丘陵 qiūlíng* ②imperial tomb; mausoleum 陵墓 língmù*
Originally written 夌, from 圥 (lù) 'mound' over 夂 (zhǐ) 'walk slowly'.
"From 夂 walk with difficulty and 坴 mound abbreviated" --Karlgren(1923).
阝(阜 fù) 'hill' was added later.
Don't confuse 陵 líng with 陆(陸) lù 'land'.
