1201 妙 [miào] wonderful; subtle; 莫名其妙  [miǎo]
妙 ²miào {B} s.v. ①wonderful; excellent; fine | Qíngkuàng bụ̀ ∼. 情况不∼。 The situation isn't encouraging. ②ingenious; clever; subtle ◆n. mystery; charm
From 女 (nǚ) 'woman' and 少 (shǎo) 'few'.
"少 small 女 woman" --Karlgren.
Etymologically related to 杪 miǎo 'tiny', and 秒 miǎo 'second' etc.
