---- 妾 [qiè] concubine
妾 ⁴qiè {E} n. 〈wr.〉 concubine ◆pr. 〈trad./humb.〉 I (self-address by women)
From 立 (lì 'stand') and 女 (nǚ) 'woman'.
"Maid-servant; concubine, wife of second or lower rank -- the seal has not 立 but: a 女 woman who is a (slave:) servant because of 辛 a crime (here abbreviated) -- her own or her family's, cf. 童" --Karlgren.
Phonetic in 接 jiē and 霎 shà.
