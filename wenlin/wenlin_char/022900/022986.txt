2979 姊 [zǐ] elder sister; 姊妹 zǐmèi sisters  [jiě] (=姐)
姊 ⁵zǐ {E} b.f. elder sister 姊妹 zǐ-mèi* 大姊 ¹dàzǐ
From 女 (nǚ) 'woman' and 𠂔 zǐ phonetic.
姊 is reportedly sometimes used (especially in Taiwan) for the common word jiě, but the standard way to write jiě is 姐. (The Far East dictionary has 姊 as jiě, 讀音 zǐ.) A variant form is 姉.
