---- 朔 [shuò] new moon
朔 ¹shuò {E} b.f. ①new moon 朔月 shuòyuè ②first day of the lunar month 朔日 shuòrì ③north 朔风 shuòfēng
The left side 屰 depicts a person upside-down, the old form of 逆 nì, 'opposition'. The right side is 月 (yuè) 'moon'.
The new moon occurs when the earth and sun are on opposite sides of the moon.
朔 shuò is phonetic in 塑 sù (塑料 sùliào 'plastic').
