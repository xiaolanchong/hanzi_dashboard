2485 曠(S旷) [kuàng] vast; to waste time; 曠野 wilderness
曠[旷] ³kuàng {E} s.v. ①vast; spacious ②free from worries ③emancipated ◆v. ①neglect (one's duties) ②waste
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 旷:) From 日 (rì) 'sun' and 广(廣) guǎng ('wide') phonetic.
"(Sunburnt:) desolate, waste, wild; empty; negligent; vast" --Karlgren(1923).
"Waste (time); to neglect, as 曠工 'to neglect one's work', 曠課 'to cut classes'" --Chao & Yang.
