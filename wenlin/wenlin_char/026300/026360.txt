230  書(S书) [shū] book; 書記 shūji clerk; 圖書館 library
書[书] ¹shū {A} n. book ◆b.f. ①style of calligraphy; script 楷书 kǎishū* ②letter 书信 shūxìn* ③document 证书 ¹zhèngshū* ④write 书写 ¹shūxiě*
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 书:) From 聿, a  hand holding a 丨 pen, writing on 二 bamboo strips, and 者 zhě phonetic. 者 was long ago reduced to 日. The simple form 书 is only four strokes.
