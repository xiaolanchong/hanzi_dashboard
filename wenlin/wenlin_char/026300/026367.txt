1101 替 [tì] in place of; (代替 dàitì) replace, substitute
替 ¹tì {B} v. ①take the place of; replace; substitute | Nǐ ²xiē yị̄huìr, wǒ lái ∼ nǐ. 你歇一会儿, 我来∼你。 Have a rest. I'll take your place. ②〈wr.〉 decline ◆cov. for; on behalf of
One 夫 (fū) man 曰 (yuē) speaking on behalf of, in place of, another 夫 man. (Originally it was two 立 people standing on top, and sometimes the bottom was 自 zì rather than 曰 yuē.)
