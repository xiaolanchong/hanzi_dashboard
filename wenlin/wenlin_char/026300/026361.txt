1878 曹 [Cáo] (surname)
曹 ¹cáo {E} n. Surname ◆b.f. ①〈trad.〉 plaintiff and defendant ②group (of about 50 people) ③division; office ④official ⑤people of a certain kind
曹 Cáo was the name of a place in 山东 Shāndōng province.
"The seal has 东 doubled above, now contracted: the 曰 name of 东 the eastern regions" --Karlgren.
