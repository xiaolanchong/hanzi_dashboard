---- 曝 [pù] expose; sun  [bào] 曝光 bàoguāng exposure (in photography)
曝 ⁸bào {E} b.f. expose 曝光 bàoguāng*
曝/暴 ³pù {E} b.f. expose to the sun; expose 曝晒 pùshài 献曝 xiànpù
From 日 (rì) 'sun' and 暴 bào.
Cognate with 暴 bào, the original meaning of which was 'to dry in the sun' (compare 暴露 bàolù 'expose').
Bàoguāng is now considered the standard pronunciation, pùguāng out-dated.
