111  月 [yuè] month; (月亮 yuèliang, 月球 yuèqiú) moon  [rù]
月 ¹yuè* {A} n. month ◆b.f. ①moon 月亮 yuèliang ②monthly 月刊 yuèkān* 月薪 ¹yuèxīn*
Picture of the moon.
月 is the 74th of the 214 radicals.
When you see a character which contains the component 月, it might be from 月 yuè 'moon', or it might be from 肉 ròu 'meat' (radical 130), or, in a few cases, from 舟 zhōu 'boat'.
