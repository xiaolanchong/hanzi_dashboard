321  程 [chéng] 过程 process; 工程 engineering; 程度 extent
程 ⁶chéng {E} b.f. ①rule; regulation 章程 zhāngchéng* ②order; procedure 程序 chéngxù* ③journey 路程 lùchéng ④distance 短程 duǎnchéng* ◆n. Surname
From 禾 (hé) 'grain' and 呈 chéng phonetic.
The old meaning was 'amount'. 禾 represents an amount of grain sufficient for making a journey of a given length. The extent of a journey, the process of getting somewhere.
