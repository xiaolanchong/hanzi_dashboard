442  稱(S称) [chēng] weigh; name  [chèn] 對稱 duìchèn symmetric  [chèng]
稱[称] ¹chēng {B} v. ①call; name ②say; state ③commend; praise ④weigh | ∼ yī̠ ∼, kàn nǐ yǒu duō zhòng. ∼一∼, 看你有多重。 Step on the scale and see how heavy you are. ◆b.f. name 名称 míngchēng* ◆cons. ∼ A wéi B name A as B; call A B
称[稱] ³chèn {E} b.f. appropriate 称心 chènxīn* 对称 duìchèn
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 称:) The left side is 禾 (hé) 'grain'. In the full form 稱, the right side is 爯: a 爫 hand holding 冉 scales, weighing the grain. In the simple form 称 the right side is 尔 ěr, like the right side of 你 nǐ. (称 is also the Japanese form.)
Compare 秤 chèng 'balance', which is a cognate word.
