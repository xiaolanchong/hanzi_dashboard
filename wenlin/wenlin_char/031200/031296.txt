1135 穀(S谷) [gǔ] (穀物) grain; 穀子 millet
穀[谷] ⁵gǔ {E} b.f. ①cereal; grain 五谷 ¹wụ̌gǔ ②millet ③〈topo.〉 unhusked rice
From 殻 ké phonetic (slightly distorted), and 禾 (hé) 'grain'.
Among simple form characters, 谷 is used instead of 穀. 谷 gǔ is an old character meaning 'valley'; the simplification consists of using 谷 for both gǔ 'valley' and gǔ 'grain.'
Compare 轂 gǔ and 彀 gòu.
