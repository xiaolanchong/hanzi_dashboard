2161 惟 [wéi] 惟一 wéiyī only; sole (same as 唯一)  [wěi]
惟/唯 ³wéi {E} adv./conj./cov. only; alone; but ◆v. 〈wr.〉 think; ponder See also shìqiú
From 忄(心 xīn) 'heart' and 隹 zhuī phonetic.
"To think, reflect; in fact, namely, particle opening a sentence and functioning as a copula; strictly speaking, only, but" --Karlgren.
Compare 唯 wéi.
