2022 悅(S悦) [yuè] (喜悅 xǐyuè) happy, joyful
悅[悦] ⁶yuè {E} b.f. ①happy; delighted 喜悦 ¹xǐyuè* ②to please; delight 悦目 yuèmù
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 悦:) From 忄(心 xīn) 'heart' and 兑(兌) duì 'joy'.
兑 is phonetic as well as signific; compare 阅 yuè, etc. In fact, Karlgren says 悦 yuè and 兑 duì may be cognates.
