胡 Hú {E} n. Surname ◆b.f. 〈hist.〉 non-Han peoples in the northwest 胡人 Húrén
胡[衚] ¹⁶hú char. 胡同 hútòng(r)
From 古 gǔ phonetic and 月(肉 ròu) 'flesh'.
胡 originally meant 'beard'. It took on additional meanings, and the full form 鬍 was created for the specific meaning 'beard', with the extra component 髟 (biāo) 'hair'.
The variant form 衚 occurs only in the word hútòng 'alley'.
