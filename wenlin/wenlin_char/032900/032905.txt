878  肉 [ròu] flesh, meat; 肌肉 jīròu muscle  [rù]
肉 ròu* {A} n. ①meat; flesh ②pulp; “flesh” of melons/etc. | Zhèi ²táor tài xiǎo, méi ∼. 这桃儿太小, 没∼。 This peach is too small to have any “flesh.” ◆s.v. 〈coll.〉 ①spongy (of melons/etc.) ②slow-moving | ∼ píqi ∼脾气 phlegmatic
Picture of meat. As a component, 肉 is usually written 月, the same as 月 yuè 'moon', as in 脸 liǎn 'face', 股 gǔ 'thigh', 脑 nǎo 'brain' etc.
