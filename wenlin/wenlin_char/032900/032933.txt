1359 肥 [féi] (肥胖) fat; fertile; (肥料) fertilizer; 肥皂 féizào soap  [bǐ]
肥 ¹féi {B} s.v. ①fat ②fertile; rich ③loose-fitting; loose; large ◆v. fertilize ◆n. fertilizer; manure
From 月(肉 ròu) 'flesh' and 巴 bā 'boa'.
It is not clear whether 巴 is phonetic or cosignific in 肥.
"肉 flesh of a 巴 boa?" --Karlgren.
