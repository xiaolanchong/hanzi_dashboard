---- 肫 [zhūn] gizzard; sincere  [chún] [tún] [zhuō]
肫 ²zhūn {F} b.f. ①gizzard 鸡肫 jīzhūn 鸭肫 yāzhūn ②sincere 肫笃 zhūndǔ
From 月(肉 ròu) 'flesh' and 屯 tún phonetic.
An ancient meaning was 'cheek' (面颊), found in 说文. The meaning 'gizzard' is found in 玉篇.
Compare 谆 zhūn which has similar meaning 'sincere', might be etymologically the same? 肫 is not found in Karlgren's Analytic Dictionary.
