==== 胠 [qū] steal, pilfer; flank; (右胠) right flank of an army
胠 ¹⁷qū b.f. ①flank; side ②pry open (a box); steal 胠箧 qūqiè
From 月(肉 ròu) 'flesh' and 去 qù phonetic.
"Flank of an animal" (Mathews) appears to be the primary meaning.
"从旁边打开 (open from the side)" (现代汉语词典) appears to be a derived meaning: flank --> side --> pry open (a box) from the side --> steal, pilfer.
