916  股 [gǔ] thigh; section
股 ²gǔ {C} b.f. ①thigh 屁股 pìgu* ②section 股长 ²gǔzhǎng ③share in a company; equal part of capital/etc. 股票 gǔpiào ◆n. strand; ply ◆m. ①(for anything string-shaped (e.g., a skein of thread)) ②(for air/fragrance/strength/etc.) ③(for groups of people (usu. derog.))
From 月(肉 ròu) 'flesh' and 殳 (shū) 'beat'.
"Thigh, rump... part... the 月 flesh which is 殳 beaten" --Karlgren.
