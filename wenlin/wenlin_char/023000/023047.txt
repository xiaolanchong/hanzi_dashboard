2345 娇(F嬌) [jiāo] graceful, delicate; 娇嫩 jiāonèn tender and lovely
娇[嬌] ⁴jiāo {D} s.v. ①fragile; frail; delicate ②squeamish; finicky ◆b.f. tender; lovely; charming 娇嫩 jiāonen ◆v. pamper; spoil
From 女 (nǚ) 'woman' and 乔(喬) qiáo phonetic.
Compare 姣 jiāo, very similar in meaning; the pronunciations were different in ancient Chinese, and still are different in Cantonese.
