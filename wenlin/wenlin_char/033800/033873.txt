==== 葑 [fēng] turnip; mustard plant; trifling  [fèng] roots of wild rice
葑 ¹¹fēng char. 葑菲 fēngfěi 不弃葑菲 bùqìfēngfěi
From 艹(草 cǎo) 'grass' and 封 fēng phonetic.
"Turnip; mustard plant; trifling" --Karlgren(1923).
The modern word for 'turnip' is 芜菁.
The meaning of 葑菲 is plants of which only some parts are good to eat, such as the leaves, while other parts are bitter, such as the stems and roots.
