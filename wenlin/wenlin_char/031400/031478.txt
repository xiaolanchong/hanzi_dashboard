1266 競(S竞) [jìng] 競爭 jìngzhēng compete; 競賽 contest
競[竞] ¹²jìng {E} b.f. compete; contest 竞争 jìngzhēng*
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 竞:) Originally 誩, 言 (yán) 'words' doubled, then two 儿 people 言 arguing, disputing, competing. The modern full form is 競, and the simple form is 竞.
Compare 竟 jìng 'finish', the top of which is from 音 yīn.
