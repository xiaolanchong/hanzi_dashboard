2133 竖(F豎) [shù] vertical, perpendicular; vertical stroke "丨"
竖[豎] ³shù {D} s.v. vertical; upright ◆v. set upright; erect; stand | Bǎ ³huàr ∼zhe ná. 把画儿∼着拿。 Hold the picture upright. ◆n. vertical stroke (in characters) ◆b.f. 〈wr.〉 houseboy; young servant 竖子 ²shùzǐ
The top (臤) is the same as 坚(堅 jiān) 'firm', minus 土.
The bottom of the full form 豎 is 豆 dòu phonetic.
In the variant form 竪 and the simple form 竖, the bottom is 立 (lì) 'stand': To 立 stand 坚 firm, upright, perpendicular.
