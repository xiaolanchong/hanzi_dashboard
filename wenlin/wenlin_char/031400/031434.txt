2093 竊(S窃) [qiè] 竊聽 qiètīng eavesdrop; 盜竊 dàoqiè steal
竊[窃] ³qiè {E} b.f. ①steal 偷窃 tōuqiè* ②secretly; furtively 窃听 qiètīng ◆pr. 〈humb.〉 I | ∼ yǐwéi ∼以为 in my humble opinion
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 窃:) The simple form 窃 is composed of 穴 (xué) 'hole' over 切 qiè phonetic.
The full form 竊 is 穴 over 釆 (biàn) and 禼 xiè phonetic.
"The seal has not 釆 but 廿 and 米: 穴 break a hole and rob 廿 plenty of 釆 rice?" --Karlgren.
