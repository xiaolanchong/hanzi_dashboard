2776 竄(S窜) [cuàn] flee, scurry; exile; change wording  [cuān]
竄[窜] ¹cuàn* {C} v. ①flee; scurry away ②exile; expel ◆b.f. alter (the wording/etc.) 窜改 ¹cuàngǎi
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 窜:) The top is 穴 (xué) 'hole'. The bottom is 鼠 (shǔ) 'mouse' (in the full form 竄), or 串 chuàn phonetic (in the simple form 窜).
