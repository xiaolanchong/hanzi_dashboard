636  章 [zhāng] chapter; seal, stamp; medal, badge; rules  [zhàng]
章 ²zhāng {B} b.f. ①chapter; section 章节 zhāngjié ②composition; structure 文章 ¹wénzhāng* ③rules; regulations; constitution 章程 zhāngchéng* ④seal; stamp 图章 túzhāng* ⑤badge; medal; emblem 徽章 ¹huīzhāng ⑥order ⑦memorial to the throne 奏章 zòuzhāng ⑧make known ◆m. (for chapters/etc.) ◆n. Surname
From 音 (yīn) 'sound' over 十 (shí) 'ten'.
"十 ten 音 sounds" --Karlgren.
A section of music, or a verse or chapter of poetry (unrelated to 立 lì 'stand' or 早 zǎo 'early'). (However, 立 is the Kangxi radical of 章.)
