2932 窪(S洼) [wā] swamp, hollow; 低窪 dīwā low-lying
窪/凹[洼/-] ⁴wā {E} v. be hollowed-out; low-lying ◆n. low-lying area; depression M:piàn/²kuài 片/块
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 洼:) From 氵(水 shuǐ) 'water' and 圭 guī phonetic.
The full form 窪 also contains 穴 (xué) 'hole'; this was a later addition.
