2155 銘(S铭) [míng] 銘刻 míngkè inscription; engrave on one's mind
銘[铭] ⁴míng {E} b.f. ①engrave; carve 铭刻 míngkè ②engrave in one's memory 铭心 ¹míngxīn ◆n. 〈hist.〉 inscription
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 铭:) From 钅(金 jīn) 'metal' and 名 míng 'name'.
"To record the name (fame) of; inscribe, engrave -- etymologically the same word as 名; character specifyingly enlarged by 金" --Karlgren.
