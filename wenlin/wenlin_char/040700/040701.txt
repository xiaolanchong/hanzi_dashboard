==== 黽(S黾) [měng] radical 205, frog  [mǐn] 黽勉 exert oneself  [Miǎn] (=澠)  [méng]
黽[黾] ⁶mǐn char. 黾勉 ²mǐnmiǎn 水黾 shuǐmǐn
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 黾:) Picture of a frog.
The simple form 黾 was included in the dictionary 字彙, 1615 A.D.
The modern word for 'frog' is 蛙 wā or 青蛙 qīngwā.
