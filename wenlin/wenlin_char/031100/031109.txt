2308 禅(F禪) [chán] meditation, Zen, Dhyana  [shàn] 禅让 abdicate
禅[禪] ³chán {E} b.f. 〈Budd.〉 ①Sanskrit dhyana; meditation; abstraction; trance 坐禅 zuòchán ②Zen (Buddhism) 禅宗 Chánzōng
禅[禪] ¹¹shàn b.f. abdicate 禅位 shànwèi 封禅 ²fēngshàn
From 礻(示 shì) 'altar' and 单(單) dān phonetic. Often used in the expression 坐禅 zuò chán 'sit in meditation'. From the Sanskrit word Dhyana, pronounced Zen in Japanese. Name of a sect of Buddhism.
