2308 禪(S禅) [chán] meditation, Zen, Dhyana  [shàn] 禪讓 abdicate  [tán]
禪[禅] ³chán {E} b.f. 〈Budd.〉 ①Sanskrit dhyana; meditation; abstraction; trance 坐禅 zuòchán ②Zen (Buddhism) 禅宗 Chánzōng
禅[禪] ¹¹shàn b.f. abdicate 禅位 shànwèi 封禅 ²fēngshàn
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 禅:) From 礻(示 shì) 'altar' and 单(單) dān phonetic. Often used in the expression 坐禅 zuò chán 'sit in meditation'. From the Sanskrit word Dhyana, pronounced Zen in Japanese. Name of a sect of Buddhism.
