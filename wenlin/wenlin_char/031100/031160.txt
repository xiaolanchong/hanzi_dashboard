==== 禸 [róu] (radical 114; component: traces of animal footsteps)
No common single-syllable words.
"Picture of the hind legs and tail of an animal" --Karlgren.
禸 is the 114th of the 214 radicals. Traditionally counted as five strokes, but actually written with four, as in 离 and 禽; but in 禺 and 禹 it really is five strokes.
