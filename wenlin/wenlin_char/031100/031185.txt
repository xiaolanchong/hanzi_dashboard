333  科 [kē] department; 科学 kēxué science; 外科 wàikē surgery  [kè]
科 ³kē {B} b.f. branch of study 文科 wénkē* ◆n. ①administrative section ②〈bio.〉 family (in biological taxonomy) ③〈thea.〉 stage directions ④rules; laws ◆v. mete out (punishment); levy (taxes/etc.); fine sb.
From 禾 hé ('grain') phonetic and 斗 (dǒu) 'measure'. 斗 is a picture of a measuring cup. Science has to do with measuring things.
