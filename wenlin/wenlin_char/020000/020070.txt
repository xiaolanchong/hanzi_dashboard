230  书(F書) [shū] book; 书记 shūji clerk; 图书馆 library
书[書] ¹shū {A} n. book ◆b.f. ①style of calligraphy; script 楷书 kǎishū* ②letter 书信 shūxìn* ③document 证书 ¹zhèngshū* ④write 书写 ¹shūxiě*
From 聿, a  hand holding a 丨 pen, writing on 二 bamboo strips, and 者 zhě phonetic. 者 was long ago reduced to 日. The simple form 书 is only four strokes.
