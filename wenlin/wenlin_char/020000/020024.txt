2534 丸 [wán] pill; pellet; ball
丸 ³wán {C} b.f. ①anything like a small ball; pellet 弹丸 dànwán ②pill; bolus 药丸 yàowán(r/zi) ◆m. 〈Ch. med.〉 (for pills/etc.)
From 人 (rén) 'person' and ⺄ a steep slope. This person is rolling down the slope like a ball.
Except for 芄 wán and 纨 wán, most of the characters which contain 丸 are historically derived from  (see 巩 gǒng).
