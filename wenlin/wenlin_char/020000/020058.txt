In 乱 luàn, 乚 represents a thread. In 礼 lǐ, 乚 is an abbreviation.
In some contexts, 乚 is said to be from an obsolete character meaning 'swallow' (the bird --- now called 燕 yān; cf. 鳦, 𠃉).
"It represents the jerking flying of this bird, 飛之形。" --Wieger.
