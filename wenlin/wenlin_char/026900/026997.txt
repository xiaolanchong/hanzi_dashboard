406  極(S极) [jí] extreme; pole; 太極 Tai Ji ☯  [jǐ]
極[极] ¹jí {B} adv. extremely | ∼ zhòngyào ∼重要 extremely important ◆b.f. ①extremity 终极 ²zhōngjí ②utmost point 极端 jíduān* ③earth's pole 北极 Běijí* ④electrical/magnetic pole 正极 zhèngjí 负极 ¹fùjí ⑤throne 登极 dēngjí ⑥primary 极强音 jíqiángyīn
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 极:) The signific part is 木 (mù) 'tree'.
The full form 極 has 亟 jí phonetic; the simple form 极 has 及 jí phonetic.
Not to be confused with 级 jí 'grade'.
