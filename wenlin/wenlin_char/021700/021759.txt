==== 哿 [kě] good, apt, possible  [gě]
No single-syllable words currently in dictionary.	add new s.s.w.
From 加 jiā phonetic and 可 (kě) 'possible'. Strangely, 加 rather than 可 is considered the phonetic part.
The radical was 可 in Shuōwén, but the Kāngxī radical is 口. Which 口? Probably the 口 in 可 rather than the 口 in 加; on the other hand, the radical of 嗧 is also 口.
