2024 漆 [qī] lacquer, paint; 油漆 paint; 漆黑 pitch-black  [qiè]
漆 ³qī {C} n. ①varnish; lacquer; paint ②Surname ◆v. paint
Originally 桼, depicting a 木 (mù) a varnish tree with 人 drops and 水 (shuǐ) fluid flowing. Later 氵(水 shuǐ) 'water' (liquid) was added.
Compare 膝 xī 'knee' (in which 桼 is phonetic).
Also compare 黍 shǔ 'broomcorn millet'.
