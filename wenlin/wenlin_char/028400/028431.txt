1848 漏 [lòu] (漏水 lòushuǐ) leak  [lóu]
漏 ¹lòu {B} v. ①leak out | Nǐ de chē zài ∼ ²yóu. 你的车在∼油。 Your car is leaking oil. ②divulge; leak ③be missing; leave out | Yóuxíng bié bạ̌ wǒ ∼ le. 游行别把我∼了。 Don't leave me out when you go demonstrating. ◆b.f. ①funnel 漏斗 lòudǒu(r)* ②water clock; hourglass 漏壶 lòuhú ③〈rel.〉 depravity
From 氵(水 shuǐ) 'water', 尸, and 雨 (yǔ) 'rain'.
Here 尸 means 屋 (wū) 'room'. Rainwater leaking through the roof.
