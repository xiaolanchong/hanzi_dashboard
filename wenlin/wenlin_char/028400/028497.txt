潑[泼] ³pō {C} v. splash; spill; sprinkle ◆s.v. ①acerbic ②detestable ③impetuous; bold and vigorous
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 泼:) From 氵(水 shuǐ) 'water' and 发(發) fā ('emit') phonetic.
发 also suggests the meaning: to 发 emit 水 water, sprinkle.
Compare 拨(撥) bō 'poke'.
