1427 湯(S汤) [tāng] hot water, soup  [shāng] 湯湯  [tàng] [yáng]
湯[汤] ¹tāng {A} n. ①hot/boiling water ②hot springs ③soup; broth ④decoction ⑤Surname
湯[汤] ⁷shāng char. 汤汤 shāngshāng
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 汤:) From 氵(水 shuǐ) 'water' and 𠃓(昜) yáng phonetic.
Compare the related word 烫 tàng 'boiling hot', which is pronounced in the falling tone and is written with 火 (huǒ) 'fire' below.
汤 is phonetic in 荡 dàng 'swing'.
