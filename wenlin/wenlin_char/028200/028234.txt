1988 湊(S凑) [còu] gather together; happen by chance
湊[凑] ¹còu {C} v. ①gather together; pool; collect ②happen by chance ③take advantage of ④move close to; press near
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 凑:) From 冫 or 氵 (水 shuǐ) 'water' and 奏 zòu phonetic.
"Streams joining into one; join, collect, bring together; hit upon, occur" --Karlgren(1923).
