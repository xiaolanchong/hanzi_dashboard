---- 湮 [yān] (湮没 yānmò) fall into oblivion  [yīn] (=洇)
湮/洇 ¹⁰yān b.f. inundate; bury; cover up 湮没无闻 yānmòwúwén 湮厄 yān'è
洇/湮 ⁶yīn v. blotch; splotch
From 氵(水 shuǐ) 'water' and 垔 yīn phonetic.
Compare 淹 yān 'drown', as in 淹没 yānmò 'flood; inundate', which is probably etymologically the same word as 湮没 yānmò 'fall into oblivion'.
