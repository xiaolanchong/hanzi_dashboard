"The interstices of a woven material, between the crossed threads [爻]; 从巾, 从爻, 象。转注 zhuǎnzhù, loose, not close, thinly, scattered, infrequent. Different 假借 jiǎjiè. Now 稀 xī, literally 禾 grain 希 thin-sown" --Wieger.
