---- 巳 [sì] The 6th of the 12 地支 Earthly Branches  [yǐ]
巳 ⁸sì n. 6th of the 12 Earthly Branches
"The figure of an embryo, a fetus. See 包 bāo. In the maternal womb, the child is 巳 or 包; at birth,  or 㐬; when swaddled, 子; when it begins to walk, 兒" --Wieger.
Phonetic in 熙, 祀.
Not to be confused with 己 jǐ or 已 yǐ.
