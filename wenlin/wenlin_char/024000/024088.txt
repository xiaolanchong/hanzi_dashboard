2325 帘 [lián] flag as shop sign; (F簾) curtain  [chén]
帘[簾] ⁵lián {D} b.f. curtain 窗帘 chuānglián(r/zi)*
帘 ¹¹lián b.f. flag as a shop sign 酒帘 jiǔlián*
There are two characters, 帘 and 簾. Among simple form characters only 帘 is used. 帘 is composed of 穴 (xué) 'hole' (window) and 巾 (jīn) 'cloth'.
The full form character 簾 is from 竹 (zhú) 'bamboo' and 廉 lián phonetic.
