---- 帑 [tǎng] state treasury  [nú]
帑 ⁵tǎng b.f. 〈wr.〉 monetary reserves of a state; national treasury; public funds 国帑 guótǎng
From 奴 (nú 'slave') and 巾 (jīn) 'towel'.
According to Karlgren, 帑 was originally a variant of 孥 nú 'wife and children'. The form 帑 was applied "through a metaphor" to the word tǎng 'treasury'.
