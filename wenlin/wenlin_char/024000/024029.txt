1217 川 [chuān] river; 四川省 Sichuan Province; 冰川 glacier
川 ²chuān {E} b.f. ①river 河川 héchuān* ②plain 平川 píngchuān* ◆ab. Sìchuān
Picture of water flowing. Sometimes written 巛 as a component in other characters. Compare 水 shuǐ 'water', 州 zhōu 'land'.
A rare word, except as an abbreviation for 四川. The common words for 'river' are 河 hé and 江 jiāng.
