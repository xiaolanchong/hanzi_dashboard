2809 巫 [wū] (巫婆 wūpó) witch, sorceress; shaman; wizard; (surname)
巫 ⁵wū {E} b.f. witch; wizard; shaman; mage (in Shang and early Zhou) ◆n. Surname
From 工 (gōng) 'work' and 人 (rén) 'person' doubled.
"The 工 work of 从 witches; magic, incantations. Two witches who dance to obtain rain. 祝也。女能以舞降神者也。象。从工, 两人舞形。" --Wieger.
巫 is a signific component in 筮 shì and 靈(灵) líng.
