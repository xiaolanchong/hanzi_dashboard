711  州 [zhōu] prefecture; 广州 Guǎngzhōu Canton
州 ⁴zhōu {D} n. ①〈trad.〉 administrative division ②(autonomous) prefecture
"Main lands inhabitable (isles or continents), surrounded by waters... composed of two 巛 superposed. 水中可居曰州。从重川。会意" --Wieger.
洲 zhōu 'continent' is written with 氵(水 shuǐ) 'water' added.
