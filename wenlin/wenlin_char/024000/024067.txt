432  布 [bù] cloth; spread
布 ⁴bù {A} n. ①cloth ②ancient copper coin
布[-/佈] ⁵bù {E} b.f. ①declare 公布 gōngbù* ②spread 传布 ¹chuánbù ③distribute 分布 ¹fēnbù ④arrange 布置 ¹bùzhì*
The top was 父 fù, phonetic, now reduced to 𠂇.
The bottom is 巾 (jīn) 'towel'.
For the meaning 'spread', there is a variant full form 佈 with 亻 added.
