1593 疾 [jí] (疾病 jíbìng) illness; 疾苦 jíkǔ hardship
疾 ⁹jí {E} b.f. ①disease; illness 疾病 ¹jíbìng* ②suffering; difficulty 疾苦 jíkǔ* ③fast 疾走 jízǒu ④hate 疾恶如仇 jí'èrúchóu
Originally 矢 (shǐ) an arrow hitting 大 a person; then 矢 an arrow hitting 疒 (nì) a person lying in bed sick. Notice that the picture is written sideways; the bed is on the left.
Compare 病 bìng, 痛 tòng, etc.
