---- 餚(S肴) [yáo] sacrificial meats; meat dishes
餚[肴] ⁵yáo {E} b.f. food consisting of or containing meat/fish 菜肴 càiyáo* 酒肴 jiǔyáo
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 肴:) From 爻 yáo phonetic over 月(肉 ròu) 'meat' signific.
The resemblance to 有 yǒu is accidental.
The full form 餚 has 飠(食 shí) 'eat' added to it.
