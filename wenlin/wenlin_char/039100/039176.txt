==== 餈 [cí] (variant of 糍)
No single-syllable words currently in dictionary.	add new s.s.w.
From 次 cì phonetic and 食 (shí) 'food'.
"'糍' 的异体" --简化字繁体字异体字辨析手册.
Also written 粢.
Also written 糍 as in 糍粑 cíbā 'glutinous rice cake'.
漢語大字典 gives "糍粑" as a definition for 餈, however it's not clear whether cíbā is ever written 餈粑.
