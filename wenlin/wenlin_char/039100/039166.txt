1573 飾(S饰) [shì] decoration; dress up; impersonate  [chì]
飾[饰] ¹⁵shì {E} b.f. ①decoration; ornament 首饰 shǒushi ②adorn; dress up 装饰 ¹zhuāngshì* ③cover up 掩饰 ¹yǎnshì* ◆v. play the role of | Tā zài nà chū xì li ∼ zhǔjuér. 他在那出戏里∼主角儿。 She plays the major role in that drama.
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 饰:) From 饣(食) shí ('eat') phonetic and 巾 (jīn) 'napkin' signific. The two strokes 𠂉 are from 人 (rén) 'person'.
