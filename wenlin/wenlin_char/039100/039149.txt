==== 飭(S饬) [chì] (書) put in order; orderly  [shì]
飭[饬] ⁹chì b.f. put in order 饬厉 ⁴chìlì 督饬 dūchì
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 饬:) From 飤 sì phonetic and 力 (lì) 'strength'.
飤 is an old variant of 饲(飼) sì 'feed', composed of 食 shí 'food' and 人 (rén) 'person'. In 饬(飭), 人 has become 𠂉.
Compare 伤(傷) shāng.
