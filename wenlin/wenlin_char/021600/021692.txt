==== 咼(S呙) [Guō] (surname)  [wāi] [hé] [wǒ] [wō] [guǎ]
咼[呙] Guō n. Surname
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 呙:) From 冎 guǎ ('skeleton') phonetic over 口 (kǒu) 'mouth'.
"The mouth awry, distorted" --Karlgren.
The simple form 呙 is 口 over 内 (nèi), an arbitrary abbreviation of 咼.
Compare 另 lìng which was a variant of the same old picture.
