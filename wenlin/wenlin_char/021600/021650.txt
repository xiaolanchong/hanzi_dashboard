2768 咒 [zhòu] 咒骂 zhòumà, 诅咒 zǔzhòu swear, curse; 赌咒 take oath
咒 ⁴zhòu {E} n. incantation ◆v. curse; damn; swear
From 口 (kǒu) 'mouth' doubled and 几(人 rén) 'person'.
In fact, 咒 was formerly written as 口 plus 兄. Now the two 口 are side by side at the top, and the bottom 儿 has become 几.
Etymologically related to 祝 zhù, but with a negative connotation.
