---- 咎 [jiù] blame, fault; 咎由自取 have only oneself to blame  [gāo]
咎 ⁹jiù {E} 〈wr.〉 v. censure; punish; blame ◆n. fault; blame
The modern character seems to be composed of 处 chù and 口 (kǒu) 'mouth'.
It may have come from 各 (gè) 'each' and 人 (rén) 'person' (人 has become 卜).
"Fault, error; to blame; misfortune -- a 各 selfish 人 man" --Karlgren(1923).
"Explanation of graph uncertain" --Karlgren(1940).
