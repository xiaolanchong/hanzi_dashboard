1031 野 [yě] wild  [shù]
野 ²yě {D} b.f. ①open country 野外 yěwài ②limit; bounds 分野 fēnyě* ③people not in power 在野 zàiyě* ◆s.v. ①wild; undomesticated ②boorish; rude ③unruly ◆suf. 〈coll.〉 extremely | Tiān lěng∼ le. 天冷∼了。 It's getting bitterly cold.
From 里 (lǐ) 'village' and 予 yǔ phonetic.
里 contains 土 (tǔ) 'land' and 田 (tián) 'field'. 野 yě means wild land that has not been cultivated.
