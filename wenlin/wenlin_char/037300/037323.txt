1042 釋(S释) [shì] 解釋 jiěshì explain; 釋放 shìfàng release  [yì]
釋[释] ²⁰shì {E} b.f. ①explain 解释 ¹jiěshì* ②set free 释放 shìfàng* ③short for Sakyamuni; Buddha; Buddhism 释迦牟尼 Shìjiāmóuní 释教 Shìjiào
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 释:) From 釆 (biàn) 'distinguish' and 𠬤(睪) yì phonetic.
