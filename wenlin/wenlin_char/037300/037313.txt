---- 釁(S衅) [xìn] offer blood in sacrifice
釁[衅] ²xìn {E} b.f. ①〈trad.〉 offer blood sacrifice; anoint with blood 衅鼓 xìngǔ ②quarrel; dispute 挑衅 tiǎoxìn*
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 衅:) "Offer blood in sacrifice; smear with blood; bloodshed, feud, rupture" --Karlgren.
The simple form 衅 is composed of 血 (xuè) 'blood' and 半 (bàn) 'half'. It dates back at least to the sixth century A.D. (玉篇).
The full form 釁 is possibly related to 爨 cuàn 'oven'. The top resembles 興 (xìng). The middle is 酉 (yǒu) 'wine', and the bottom is 分 (fēn) 'divide'.
"Explained by some commentators as: 分 to distribute 酉 spirits over (爨 cuàn) the stove . . . sacrifice to the atrium -- very uncertain" --Karlgren.
