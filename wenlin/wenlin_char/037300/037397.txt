---- 鈕(S钮) [niǔ] 電鈕 diànniǔ push-button  [chǒu]
鈕[钮] ³niǔ {E} b.f. ①knob; handle 门钮 ménniǔ ②button (such as on-off switch) 电钮 diànniǔ
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 钮:) From 钅(金 jīn) 'metal' and 丑 chǒu phonetic.
钮 is sometimes used instead of 纽 niǔ 'button'; it is etymologically the same word as 纽 and 扭 niǔ 'twist'.
