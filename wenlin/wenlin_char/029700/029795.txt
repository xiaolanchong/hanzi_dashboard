2732 瑣(S琐) [suǒ] 瑣碎 suǒsuì trivial; 煩瑣 loaded down with trivia
瑣[琐] ⁴suǒ {E} b.f. trivial; petty 琐碎 suǒsuì 繁琐 fánsuǒ*
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 琐:) From 王(玉 yù) 'jade' and () suǒ, which is from 小 (xiǎo) 'small' and 贝(貝 bèi) 'cowrie shell'.
"Fragment -- 小 small 貝 cowries...character enlarged by 玉" --Karlgren.
