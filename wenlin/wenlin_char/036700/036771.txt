1923 辣 [là] (spicy) hot; 辣椒 làjiāo hot pepper; 毒辣 dúlà sinister [lā]
辣 ¹là {C} s.v. ①peppery; hot ②sharp; spicy; biting (of smell/taste) | Sìchuān cài hěn ∼. 四川菜很∼。 Sichuan food is hot and spicy. ③vicious; ruthless
辣 ³lā {E} char. 热辣辣 rèlālā 火辣辣 huǒlālā
From 辛 (xīn) 'bitter suffering' and 束 (shù) 'bundle'.
辣 là is etymologically the same word as 剌 là 'slash, cruel, perverse', from 束 'bundle' and 刂 ( = 刀 dāo) 'knife, cut'.
四川菜 Sichuan cuisine is especially 辣 là.
