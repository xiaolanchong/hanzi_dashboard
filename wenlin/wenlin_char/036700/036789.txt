xxxx 辵 [chuò] (component: 'go'. Now written 辶)
No single-syllable words currently in dictionary.	add new s.s.w.
The top 彡 was originally 彳 (chì) 'footprint', and the bottom  was 止 (zhǐ) 'foot'. The oldest forms of 辵 (on oracle-bones) were composed of 止 inside of 行 (xíng).
In the character 徒 tú, made of 辵 and 土 tǔ phonetic, 辵 kept its parts 彳 and 止 distinct. Normally 辵 has become written 辶.
