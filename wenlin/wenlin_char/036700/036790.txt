==== 辶 [chuò] (radical 162, component: 'go', from 辵. 走之旁 zǒuzhīpáng)
No single-syllable words currently in dictionary.	add new s.s.w.
辶 derives from 辵 chuò. The top 彡 was originally 彳 (chì) 'footprint', and the bottom  was 止 (zhǐ) 'foot'.
Since the meaning is like 走 zǒu 'walk', and the modern shape happens to resemble 之 zhī, 辶 is commonly known as 走之旁 zǒuzhīpáng (旁 páng 'side' meaning 'component').
