1677 轰(F轟) [hōng] BANG, BOOM! 轰轰烈烈 grand, spectacular
轰[轟] ¹hōng {E} on. bang!; boom! ◆v. ①rumble; boom ②drive out ◆b.f. attack; bomb 轰炸 hōngzhà
The full form 轟 is 車 (chē) 'car' tripled.
"Rumbling of carriages" --Karlgren.
In the simple form 轰, the top car is 车 and the bottom two are abbreviated to 又 (yòu).
