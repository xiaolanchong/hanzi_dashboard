802  善 [shàn] good; 改善 gǎishàn improve; 善于 be good at
善 ²shàn {D} s.v. ①good ②charitable; kind ◆b.f. ①friendly 友善 yǒushàn ②familiar 面善 miànshàn ③kindness; good deed 行善 xíngshàn* ④do sth. well 善于 ¹shànyú* ⑤properly 善自保重 shànzìbǎozhòng ⑥make perfect 善后 shànhòu ⑦be apt/prone to 善忘 shànwàng
Originally from 羊 (yáng) 'sheep' over 言 (yán) 'words'. 羊 'sheep' means 'good' here, as it does in 美 měi.
