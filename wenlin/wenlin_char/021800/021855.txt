1316 啟(S启) [qǐ] open; start; enlighten; 啟發 arouse, inspire
啟[启] ³qǐ {E} b.f. ①open (up) 启封 ²qǐfēng ②enlighten; expound 启蒙 ¹qǐméng* ③state; inform 启事 ²qǐshì
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 启:) From 户 (戶 hù) 'door' and 口 (kǒu) 'mouth'.
"To 口 open a 户 shutter" --Kargren.
The full form 啟 and the variant form 啓 have 攵(攴 pū) 'beat' added, but the simple form 启 is older.
