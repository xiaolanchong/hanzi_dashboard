---- 臼 [jiù] mortar; joint
臼 ⁵jiù {E} n. mortar ◆b.f. ①resembling a mortar in shape 臼齿 jiùchǐ ②joint (of bones)
"This character represents a mortar; 象形。The first ones, says the Glose (说文), were holes made in the earth; hence the meanings, pit, large hole, in the compounds. Later on, the mortar was made first of wood, then later of metal" --Wieger.
Compare 旧(舊) jiù.
