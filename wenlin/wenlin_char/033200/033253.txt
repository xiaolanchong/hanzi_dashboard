1905 臥(S卧) [wò] lie down; 臥室 wòshì bedroom
臥[卧] ²wò {C} v. ①lie down ②crouch; sit (of animals/birds) ③〈topo.〉 poach (eggs)
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 卧:) The left side 臣 (chén 'minister') depicts an eye sideways, the way a person's eye looks when they are lying down.
The right side is 人 (rén) 'person', written like 卜 (bǔ) in the simple form.
卧 occurs as a component in 监(監) jiān and 临(臨) lín.
