興[兴] ²xīng {D} v. ①prosper; rise; prevail; become popular ②start; begin ③encourage; promote ④〈wr.〉 get up; rise ⑤〈topo.〉 permit; allow ◆adv. maybe; perhaps
興[兴] ⁵xìng {E} b.f. mood/desire to do sth.; interest; excitement 兴趣 xìngqù* 高兴 gāoxìng*
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 兴:) The full form 興 is 舁 (yú) 'lift up' plus 同 (tóng) 'same'.
"舁 hands lifting 同 with joint effort . . . (xīng) lift, raise, rise, begin, originate; prosper; raise or rise in rank; . . . (xìng) high spirits, elation, joy, joyful; desire, passion" --Karlgren.
Compare 与(與) yǔ 'and'.
