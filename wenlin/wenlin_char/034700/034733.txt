==== 螭 [chī] hornless dragon
螭 ¹¹chī b.f. a hornless dragon (art motif) 螭陛 chībì 蟠螭纹 pánchīwén
From 虫 (chóng) 'worm' and 离 lí.
Hornless dragons were common decorations in ancient art.
The character 螭 also occurs as a variant of 魑 chī as in 魑魅 魍魉 chīmèi-wǎngliǎng 'demons and monsters'.
Is 离 lí phonetic or signific? (离 depicted some sort of rare beast).
A variant character is 彲.
