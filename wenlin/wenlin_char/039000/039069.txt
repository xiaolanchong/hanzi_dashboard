946  额(F額) [é] (前额) forehead; 定额 quota
额[額] ³é {D} n. forehead ◆b.f. ①specified quantity 名额 ¹míng'é ②horizontal tablet 匾额 biǎn'é ③〈art〉 hanging scroll mounted as panel; tablet-like mountings 横额 héng'é*
