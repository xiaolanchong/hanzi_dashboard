149  產(S产) [chǎn] (產生 chǎnshēng) produce
產/産[产] ¹chǎn* {D} v. ①give birth to; be delivered of ②produce ◆b.f. ①product; produce 产品 chạ̌npǐn ②property; estate 财产 cáichǎn
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 产:) The full form 產 or 産 is like 彦(彥) yàn 'virtue', but with 生 shēng 'life' in place of 彡. In the simple form 产, 生 has been left out. 彦 yàn, which occurs in 颜 (颜色 yánsè 'color'), represents lines and wrinkles (like 文 wén).
"The 彦 wrinkles formed on the body consequent upon 生 child-birth. Fecundity, to bear, to produce" --Wieger.
"The signific is 生, interpretation of the rest uncertain" --Karlgren(1923).
