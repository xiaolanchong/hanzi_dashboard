2106 履 [lǚ] shoes; ䷉ Treading
履 ⁶lǚ {E} b.f. ①shoes 革履 gélǚ* ②step/tread on; follow 步履 bùlǚ ③carry out 履行 ²lǚxíng
"A shoe; to tread, walk -- the seal contains 尸 body, 彳 and 夂 to walk, and above the latter a picture of a shoe of the same type as 舟" --Karlgren.
So, according to Karlgren, 履 lǚ is unrelated to 复(復) fù.
