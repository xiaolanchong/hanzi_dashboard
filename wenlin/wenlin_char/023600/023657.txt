xxxx 屩(S𪨗) [juē] straw sandals
屩/屫[𪨗] ³juē b.f. straw sandals 蹑𪨗担簦 nièjuēdāndēng
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 𪨗:) From 履 (lǚ) 'shoes' abbreviated (尸 plus 彳), and 乔(喬) qiáo phonetic.
A variant form is 屫. Also sometimes written 蹻.
The simplified form 𪨗 is in 现代汉语词典 and was added to Unicode 5.2.
