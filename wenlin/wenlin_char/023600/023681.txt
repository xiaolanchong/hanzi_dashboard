524  岁(F歲) [suì] years old; 岁数 age; 万岁 long life
岁[歲] ¹suì* {A} m. year (of age) | jiǔ ∼ 九∼ nine years old ◆b.f. year 年岁 niánsuì* 岁末 ²suìmò
From 戌 xū phonetic and 步 (bù) 'step'. 步 was cut into two pieces, the top going over 戌 and the bottom going inside 戌.
In the simple form 岁, the top 止 has been altered to 山 (shān 'mountain'), and the whole lower part replaced by 夕 (xī 'evening').
Compare 梦(夢) mèng 'dream'.
