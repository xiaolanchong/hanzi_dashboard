628  層(S层) [céng] layer, level, storey
層[层] ¹céng* {A} m. layer; story; floor; stratum | dì-yī ∼ 第一∼ in the first place | Tāmen de huà háiyǒu yī̠ ∼ yìsi. 他们的话还有一∼意思。 What they said has further implications.
