1908 戒 [jiè] forbid; 戒严 jièyán enforce martial law; 戒指 jièzhi ring
戒 ⁵jiè {E} b.f. ①guard against 戒备 jièbèi* ②exhort; admonish; warn 告诫 ¹gàojiè* ③give up; drop; stop 戒烟 jièyān ④(finger) ring 戒指 jièzhi ◆n. ①〈Budd.〉 monastic discipline ②prohibition
From 戈 (gē) 'weapon' and 廾( gǒng) two hands.
"To daunt, to forbid with threats; 从持戈, 会意。Two hands holding a halberd" --Wieger.
Etymologically the same word as 诫 jiè 'warn', which has 讠(言 yán) 'words' added. Cognate with 械 xiè 'weapon; tool'.
Compare 戎 róng.
