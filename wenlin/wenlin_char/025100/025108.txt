==== 戔(S戋) [jiān] 戔戔 small  [cán]
戔[戋] ²¹jiān char. 戋戋 ²jiānjiān 戋戋之数 jiānjiānzhīshù
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 戋:) From two 戈 (gē) spears, one on top of the other.
Karlgren(1940) speculates that 戔 might be the primary graph of 殘 cán 'injure'.
This is a rare character, but a common phonetic component.
