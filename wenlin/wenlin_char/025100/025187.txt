1561 扣 [kòu] button up; 纽扣儿 niǔkòur button; 扣子 kòuzi knot, button
扣 ¹kòu {B} v. ①button/buckle/tie up ②cover with concave object ③detain; arrest ④deduct; discount ⑤smash or spike (a ball) ◆n. ①knot; button; buckle ②discount | Wǒ gẹ̌i nǐ dǎ bā ∼. 我给你打八∼。 I'll give you a 20 percent discount. ◆m. (for bundles/bunches/etc.)
From 扌(手 shǒu) 'hand' and 口 kǒu phonetic.
