1268 戴 [dài] put on; 戴眼镜; 戴上帽子
戴 ³dài {A} v. wear; put on (of accessories) | Bǎ màozi ∼shang. 把帽子∼上。 Put on your hat. ◆b.f. respect; honor; support 爱戴 àidài ◆n. Surname
From 𢦏 zái phonetic and 異, which is the full form of 异(異) yì 'different': 田 over 共. You become different when you put on a hat or glasses.
