1223 趙(S赵) [Zhào] (surname)  [diào]
趙[赵] Zhào {E} n. ①a state in the Eastern Zhou period ②ancient term for present-day southern Hebei area ③Surname
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 赵:) From 走 (zǒu) 'go' and 肖 xiāo phonetic.
In the simple form 赵, 肖 is replaced by 乂.
"To hasten to; N. pr. (ancient state and family name)" --Karlgren.
