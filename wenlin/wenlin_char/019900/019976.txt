1195 丈 [zhàng] (length:) 3 1/3 meters; 丈夫 zhàngfu husband
丈 ⁴zhàng {B} m. (of length equal to 10 ¹chǐ (3 1/3 meters)) ◆v. measure land ◆b.f. ①〈court.〉 male elder relative/person 丈人 zhàngren* ②husband 丈夫 zhàngfu*
Originally 十 (shí) 'ten' over 又 (yòu) 'hand', now contracted. 十 ten 又 hand-lengths.
Compare 支 zhī 'branch'.
