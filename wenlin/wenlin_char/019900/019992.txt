2462 丘 [qiū] mound; grave; 丘陵 qiūlíng hills; 沙丘 shāqiū dune
丘 ²qiū {E} b.f. ①mound; hillock 丘陵 qiūlíng* ②grave 坟丘 fénqiū ◆n. Surname
The oldest forms appear to depict two hills. Later forms had 北 (běi) two people back to back, over a 一 line representing the ground.
No relation to 斤 jīn 'axe', 兵 bīng 'soldier', or 乒乓 pīngpāng; but 虚 xū originally had 丘 at the bottom instead of 业.
