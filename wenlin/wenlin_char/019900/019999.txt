1450 丟(S丢) [diū] lose, misplace
丟[丢] ¹diū {A} v. ①lose; misplace | Bié ∼le nǐ de qiánbāo. 别∼了你的钱包。 Don't lose your wallet. ②discard ③put aside | Wǒ de Zhōngwén ∼le shíjǐ nián le. 我的中文∼了十几年了。 I haven't used my Chinese for more than ten years. ④toss; throw | Bié luàn ∼ dōngxi. 别乱∼东西。 Don't throw (your) things around.
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 丢:) From 丿 a falling stroke over 去 (qù) 'go away'.
"To 丿  drop 去 away" --Karlgren(1923).
