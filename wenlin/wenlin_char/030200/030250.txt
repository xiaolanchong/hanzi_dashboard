---- 瘪(F癟) [biě] shrivelled, shrunken  [biē] 瘪三 biēsān beggar/thief
瘪[癟] biě {E} s.v. shriveled; shrunken ◆v. ①shrivel ②become dejected
瘪[癟] ³biē {E} char. 瘪三 biēsān(r) 受瘪 ²shòubiē
From 疒 (nì) 'sick' and 自 (zì) 'nose' over 仑(侖 lùn) 'logical consequence'.
I can't find an explanation of the combination 自 plus 侖. A variant form (俗字) has 龠 at the bottom instead of 侖. (No explanation of shape in 汉语大字典.)
