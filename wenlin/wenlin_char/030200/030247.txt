---- 瘧(S疟) [nüè] fever  [yào] 瘧子 yàozi malaria
瘧[疟] ¹nüè {E} b.f. malaria 疟疾 nüèji 避疟 bìnüè
疟[瘧] ⁶yào b.f. (this pronunciation used only in yàozi) malaria 疟子 ³yàozi
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 疟:) From 疒 (nì) 'sick' and 虐 nüè 'cruel' (虐 is abbreviated to the bottom three strokes in the simple form 疟).
疟 'fever' and 虐 'cruel' are etymologically the same word.
