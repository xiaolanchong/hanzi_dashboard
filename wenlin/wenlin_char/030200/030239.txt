---- 瘟 [wēn] acute communicable diseases; plague; 瘟疫 wēnyì pestilence  [wò] [yūn]
瘟 ²wēn {E} b.f. epidemic; plague 瘟疫 wēnyì ◆v. ①(of animals) catch an acute communicable disease ②(of traditional opera) be dull and insipid
From 疒 (nì) 'sick' and 昷 (or 𥁕) wēn phonetic.
(The glyphs in the Unicode 2.0 and 3.0 books have 9-stroke 昷. The Unicode 1.0 book had 10-stroke 𥁕.)
