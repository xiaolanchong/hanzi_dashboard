1496 柔 [róu] yielding; 柔软 róuruǎn soft; 柔和 róuhe gentle
柔 ²róu {E} b.f. ①soft; supple 柔软 róuruǎn ②gentle 柔和 ¹róuhé ◆v. soften
From 矛 (máo) 'spear' and 木 (mù) 'wood'.
"木 wood used for making 矛 lances" --Karlgren.
Flexible wood which bends instead of breaking. The opposite is 刚 gāng 'firm'. Compare 软 ruǎn 'soft', and 弱 ruò 'weak'.
