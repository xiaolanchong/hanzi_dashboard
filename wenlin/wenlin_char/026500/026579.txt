1331 染 [rǎn] to dye; catch (disease); 染料 dye; 污染 pollution
染 ¹rǎn* {B} v. ①dye ②catch (illness/disease) ③soil; pollute ④make strokes (in painting/etc.)
From 氵(水 shuǐ) 'water', 九 (jiǔ) 'nine', and 木 (mù) 'tree'.
"To dye; imbue with, infect -- the commentator says: 九 nine dippings into a 水 decoction of a special kind of 木 tree" --Karlgren.
Probably no historical relation to 氿 guǐ.
