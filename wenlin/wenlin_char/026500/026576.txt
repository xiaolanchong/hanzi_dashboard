562  某 [mǒu] certain, some; 某人 mǒurén somebody  [méi]
某 mǒu* {B} pr. ①certain; some | Lị̌ ∼ 李∼ a certain person called Li | zài ∼̣zhǒng chéngdu shàng 在∼种程度上 to some extent ②I (self-address after one's surname)
From 甘 (gān) 'sweet' and 木 (mù) 'tree'.
The character 某 was originally used for méi 'plum', which is now written 梅 méi. 某 was then borrowed for the word mǒu 'certain', which happened to sound similar.
