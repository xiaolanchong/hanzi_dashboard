86   面 [miàn] face, surface; (F麵) wheat flour; (面条 miàntiáo) noodle

面[麵] ²miàn {E} n. ①noodles (made with wheat flour) ②(wheat) flour ◆b.f. ①foods made with wheat flour 面包 miànbāo* ②powder-like 胡椒面 hújiāomiàn(r) ◆s.v. 〈topo.〉 ①soft and floury ②weak; lax; slow
From 𦣻(首 shǒu) 'head', with the face circled.
The full form character for miàn 'noodle' is 麵, from 麥 (mài) 'wheat' and 面 miàn phonetic. A variant form is 麪, with 丏 miàn phonetic. Among simple form characters, 面 is used in place of 麵 or 麪.
面 is the 176th of the 214 radicals.
