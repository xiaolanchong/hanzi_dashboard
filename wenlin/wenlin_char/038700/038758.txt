---- 靦 [miǎn] (S腼) 靦腆 miǎntian shy  [tiǎn] (=覥) (=腆)
靦[腼] ⁷miǎn {E} char. 腼腆 miạ̌ntiǎn
觍[覥/靦] ⁴tiǎn b.f. 〈wr.〉 shame 觍颜 tiǎnyán 觍冒 tiǎnmào 觍着脸 tiǎnzhe liǎn
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 腼:) The simple form 腼 is composed of 月(肉 ròu) 'flesh' and 面 miàn ('face') phonetic.
The full form 靦 has 見 (jiàn) 'see' instead of 月.
