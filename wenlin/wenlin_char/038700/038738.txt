405  青 [qīng] green (blue, black); 青年 youth; 青蛙 frog  [jīng]
青 ³qīng {B} s.v. ①nature's color; green; blue; greenish black ②not ripe | Júzi hái ∼ ne. 橘子还∼呢。 The oranges are not ripe yet. ◆b.f. youth; young (of people) 青年 qīngnián ◆n. short for Qinghai province and Qingdao city in Shandong
龶 stands for 生 shēng 'life', and 月 for 丹 (dān) 'red' (丹田 dāntián).
"The 丹 hue of 生 growing plants. . . . But 丹 means RED! Was the inventor of 青 a Daltonian?" --Wieger. (Daltonism = red/green color blindness.)
"The 丹 color of 生 vegetation, 生 in the same time phonetic" --Karlgren. (Karlgren regarded this explanation as "far-fetched".)
A variant form is 靑.
