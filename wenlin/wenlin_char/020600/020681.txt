==== 僉(S佥) [qiān] together; 僉同 unanimously agree
僉[佥] ¹¹qiān b.f. 〈wr.〉 unanimous; together 佥同 qiāntóng
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 佥:) "Meeting, together; 皆也。From  to gather, from 人人 several men, from 口口 several mouths..." --Wieger.
The simplified form is from traditional 草书.
Mostly used as a phonetic component, as in 脸验检险签剑...
