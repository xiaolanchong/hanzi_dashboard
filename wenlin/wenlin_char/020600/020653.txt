2587 傭(S佣) [yōng] 傭人 servant; 雇傭 hire  [yòng] 傭金 commission  [chōng]
傭[佣] ³yōng {E} b.f. ①hire 雇佣 gùyōng ②servant 佣妇 yōngfù
佣[傭] ²yòng {E} b.f. commission; brokerage fee 佣金 yòngjīn*
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 佣:) From 亻(人 rén) 'person' and (full form:) 庸 yōng 'usual' or (simple form:) 用 yòng 'use'.
Cognate with both 庸 yōng 'usual' and 用 yòng 'use'.
"(Use a person:) engage for hire; ordinary" --Karlgren.
