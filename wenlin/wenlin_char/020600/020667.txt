1697 傻 [shǎ] stupid, idiotic; 傻子 shǎzi, 傻瓜 shǎguā idiot
傻 shǎ {B} s.v. ①stupid; muddleheaded | Wǒ zhēn ∼. 我真∼。 I'm so stupid. ②stunned | Tā tīngdào zhè xiāoxi ²shí, zhěnggè rén dōu ∼ le. 他听到这消息时, 整个人都∼了。 He was utterly stunned when he heard the news.
From 亻(人 rén) 'person', 囟 a block-head, 八 tiny arms, and 夂 a foot pointing downwards (meaning that the idiot walks slowly).
