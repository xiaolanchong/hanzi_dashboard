151  话(F話) [huà] speech; 说话 shuōhuà talk, speak    [huo] 白话
话[話] ¹huà* {A} n. ①speech; talk; conversation; words; sayings; story M:²jù 句 ②language; parole ③dialect ◆suf. supposition | bùrán de ∼ 不然的∼ if not ◆v. 〈wr.〉 talk; narrate
话[話] huo b.f. empty talk 白话 báihuo 瞎白话 xiābáihuo
From 讠(言 yán) 'words' and 舌 (shé) 'tongue'.
See 活 huó ('live') for more about 舌, which in this case was not originally shé 'tongue', but a phonetic component, with the meaning 'hold in the mouth'.
