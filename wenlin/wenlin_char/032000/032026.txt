382  級(S级) [jí] grade, level; 階級 (social) class
級[级] ⁴jí {B} n. ①level; rank; grade ②school grade/class/form ◆v.m. ①(for steps/stages) ②〈lg.〉 degree
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 级:) From 纟(糸 sī) 'silk' and 及 jí phonetic. The original meaning was the grading of silk according to its quality.
