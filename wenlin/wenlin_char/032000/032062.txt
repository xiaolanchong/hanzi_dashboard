xxxx 紾(S) [zhěn] twist  [tiǎn] [jǐn]
紾[] ⁷zhěn b.f. twist; turn 臂 zhěnbì
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form :) From 纟(糸) and 㐱.
The simplified form  is not in Unicode 6.0. It appears in the ABC Chinese-English Comprehensive Dictionary (DeFrancis 2003, print edition), and in 《现代汉语词典》 and in 《形音義規範字典》.
