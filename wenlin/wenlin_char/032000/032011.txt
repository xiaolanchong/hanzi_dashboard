1660 紋(S纹) [wén] lines, veins, (wood) grain  [wèn]
紋/文[纹/-] ³wén {E} n. ①vein; grain ②lines/patterns (in cloth/etc.)
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 纹:) From 纟(糸 sī) 'silk' and 文 wén 'literature'. Etymologically 纹 is the same word as 文, which depicted a person with a decorated chest.
