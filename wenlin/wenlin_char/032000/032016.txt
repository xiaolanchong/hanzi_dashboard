1342 紐(S纽) [niǔ] 紐扣兒 niǔkòur button; knob, handle; tie
紐[纽] ²niǔ {E} n. handle; knob ◆b.f. ①button 纽扣 niǔkòu(r) ②pivot; key position 枢纽 shūniǔ* ③bond; tie 纽带 niǔdài ◆n. 〈lg.〉 ①initial consonant of a Sinitic syllable ②ancient initial ③character representing an ancient initial
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 纽:) From 纟(糸 sī) 'silk' and 丑 chǒu phonetic.
Etymologically the same word as 扭 niǔ 'turn, twist'; sometimes written 钮 niǔ (as in 电钮 diànniǔ) with 钅(金 jīn) 'metal' instead of 纟(糸 sī) 'silk'.
丑 chǒu is phonetic ("with a curious alternance of initials" --Karlgren). Since 丑 chǒu is a picture of a tied hand, it also suggests the meaning.
