948  紹(S绍) [shào] 介紹 jièshào introduce  [chāo]
紹[绍] ³shào {E} b.f. ①continue; connect 绍继 shàojì 介绍 jièshào ②short for Shàoxīng (in Zhejiang)
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 绍:) From 纟(糸 sī) 'thread' and 召 zhào phonetic. 糸 suggests the meaning "tie together, connect", which is what you do with two people when you introduce them.
