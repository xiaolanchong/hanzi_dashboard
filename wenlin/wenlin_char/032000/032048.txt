676  細(S细) [xì] thin; 仔細 zǐxì careful; 細胞 xìbāo cell; 詳細 detailed
細[细] ¹xì {A} s.v. ①thin; slender ②fine; in small particles ③thin and soft ④exquisite; delicate ⑤careful; meticulous; detailed ⑥minute; trifling
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 细:) From 纟(糸 sī) 'thread' and 田 (tián 'field').
"The 糸 (thread:) hair of the 田 head, see 思" --Karlgren.
田 by itself means 'field', but here it represents a head.
