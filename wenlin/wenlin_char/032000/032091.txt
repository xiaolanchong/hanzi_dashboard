---- 絛(S绦) [tāo] silk ribbon; 絛蟲 tapeworm
絛[绦] ⁵tāo {E} b.f. ①silk braid or ribbon 绦子 tāozi ②ribbon-like object 绦虫 tāochóng*
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 绦:) From 纟(糸 sī) 'silk' and (simple form:) 条 tiáo phonetic or (full form:) 攸 yōu phonetic.
The full form 絛 is like 條 (tiáo) with the bottom right part of 條 replaced by 糸.
Variant forms are 縧 and 縚.
