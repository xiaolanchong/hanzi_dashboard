736  紙(S纸) [zhǐ] paper; 報紙 newspaper
紙[纸] ³zhǐ {A} n. paper ◆m. (for pieces/sheets of paper) | yī̠ ∼ gōngwén 一∼公文 a document
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 纸:) From 纟(糸 sī) 'silk' and 氏 shì phonetic.
In ancient times, silk cloth was used for writing on. It was nicer than writing on bamboo strips, which was the more economical alternative. Paper didn't become popular until the Tang dynasty (618-906).
