749  衛(S卫) [wèi] 保衛 defend; 衛生 sanitary; 衛星 satellite
衛[卫] ⁸wèi {E} b.f. ①guard; defend 保卫 ¹bǎowèi 卫兵 wèibīng* ②protect (against disease) 卫生 wèishēng* ◆n. Surname
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 卫:) The full form 衛 is 韋 wéi phonetic inside of 行 (xíng) 'walk'.
Cognate with 围(圍) wéi 'surround', but 卫(衛) wèi is pronounced with the falling tone.
