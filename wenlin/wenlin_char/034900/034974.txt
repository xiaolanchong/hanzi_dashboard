==== 袞(S衮) [gǔn] ancient monarch's ceremonial attire
袞[衮] ⁶gǔn b.f. ceremonial dress for royalty 衮服 gǔnfú
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 衮:) From 衣 (yī) 'clothing' and 公 (gōng) 'official'.
"Imperial robe (dragon-embroidered) -- an 公 official 衣 robe..." --Karlgren.
Notice how 衣 is split in half, with 亠 on top,  on the bottom, and 公 in the middle. In the full form 袞, the bottom of 公 is written 口 insted of 厶.
