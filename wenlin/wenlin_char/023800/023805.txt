1648 峽(S峡) [xiá] (峽谷) gorge, canyon; 海峽 strait, channel; 三峽
峽/硤[峡/硖] ¹xiá {D} b.f. ①gorge; canyon (often used in place names) 峡谷 ¹xiágǔ* 三峡 Sān Xiá* ②strait 海峡 hǎixiá*
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 峡:) From 山 (shān) 'mountain' and 夹(夾) jiā 'squeeze'.
Cognate with 夹; etymologically the same word as 狹 xiá 'narrow'.
