2300 崖 [yá] (崖壁 yábì) precipice, cliff
崖 ³yá {D} b.f. precipice; cliff 山崖 shānyá* 悬崖 xuányá
Originally 厓, from 厂 'cliff' and 圭 guī:
"圭...either is phonetic or, rather, signifies the 土 earth layers of a slope" --Karlgren.
The 山 (shān) 'mountain' was added later. Etymologically the same word as 涯 yá (天涯 edge of the world).
