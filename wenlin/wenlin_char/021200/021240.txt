1678 勸(S劝) [quàn] advise
勸[劝] ¹quàn {B} v. ①exhort; urge; persuade | Wǒ ∼ ²tā qù xiūxi. 我∼她去休息。 I urged her to go and have a rest. ②propitiate; calm down
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 劝:) The full form 勸 is composed of 雚 guàn phonetic and 力 (lì) 'strength'.
In the simple form 劝, 雚 is replaced by 又 (yòu).
