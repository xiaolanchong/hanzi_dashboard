==== 匚 [fāng] (radical 22, component: 区字框 qūzìkuàng)  [fàng]
No single-syllable words currently in dictionary.	add new s.s.w.
Picture of a basket or water-jug, turned on its side.
22nd of the 214 radicals.
Compare 匸 xì, which is the 23rd radical. In modern writing there is rarely any distinction between 匚 and 匸.
