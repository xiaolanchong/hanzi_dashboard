---- 匡 [kuāng] rectify; (surname)  [wāng]
匡 ⁴kuāng b.f. ①correct (an error) 匡谬 kuāngmiù ②assist 匡助 kuāngzhù 匡复 kuāngfù
From 匚 (fāng) 'basket' and 王 wáng phonetic.
"Square basket; rectilinear, straight, correct; to correct, regulate; assist" --Karlgren.
Etymologically the same word as 框 kuāng 'frame', 筐 kuāng 'basket'.
