260  北 [běi] north; 东北 dōngběi northeast; 北京 Běijīng  [bèi]
北 běi {A} n. north ◆v. 〈wr.〉 be defeated
Two people back to back.
"The opposite of 从 cóng. Not to follow each other, to turn one's back..." --Wieger.
Related to 背 bèi 'back'. In China, the north has traditionally been considered the "back side". Traditional compasses (指南针) point south.
