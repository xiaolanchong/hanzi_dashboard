The simple form 会 is 𠆢 over 云 yún 'cloud'. The full form 會 is  over  over 曰, for which there are various dubious interpretations.
"To  meet (ancient form of 集) round the  fire (cf. 黑) and 曰 chat" --Karlgren.
會 may have depicted a rice-pot with a  lid, which you "put together" with the pot. Compare 合 hé 'combine'. Also compare 曾 céng.
