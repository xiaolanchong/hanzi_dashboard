1504 伏 [fú] bend over; subside; 伏天 hot summer days; 起伏 rise & fall  [fù]
伏 ⁴fú {D} v. ①bend over; lie prostrate ②subside; go down ③hide; conceal ④subdue; tame ⑤admit (defeat/guilt) ◆n. ①hot summer days; dog days ②Surname ◆m. volt
From 亻(人 rén) 'person' and 犬 (quǎn) 'dog'.
"Dog and man: to admit defeat, give up, struck to the ground. The various meanings of the character demonstrate some of the Chinese attitudes toward dogs" --Lindqvist.
