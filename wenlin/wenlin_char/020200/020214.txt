件 ²jiàn {A} m. (for articles/items/etc.) ◆b.f. correspondence; paper; document 文件 ¹wénjiàn*
From 亻(人 rén) 'person' and 牛 (niú) 'ox'.
"The idea is represented by a representative of the two nobler categories, a 亻 man and an 牛 ox; 从人, 从牛, 会意。" --Wieger.
