xxxx 鴗(S𫁡) [lì] kingfisher; 笑鴗 kookaburra
鴗[𫁡] ⁴⁴lì char. 笑𫁡 ³xiàolì
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 𫁡:) From 立 and 鸟(鳥).
"鴗, 天狗也。从鳥, 立聲" --说文.
天狗 means 'Heavenly Hound', but is also an old name for 鱼狗 'kingfisher'.
The simplified form is not in XHC, HDZ, Cihai. It was added to Unicode 5.2.
