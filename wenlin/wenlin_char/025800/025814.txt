==== 擖 [kā] cut; scrape  [qiā] [jiā] [zhá] [guā] [yè] [gē] [liè]
擖 ⁴kā v. scrape; scrape with a knife ◆char. 擖油 kāyóu 擖哧 ²kāchi
From 扌(手 shǒu) 'hand' and 葛 gé phonetic.
According to 漢語大字典, the character 擖 has seven different pronunciations: qiā, jiā, zhá, guā, yè, gē, and liè -- none of these matches kā, which is the only pronunciation in 现代汉语规范字典!
