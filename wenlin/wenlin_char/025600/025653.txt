xxxx 搵(S揾) [wèn] press; wipe; 搵淚 wipe tears; 搵錢 make money
搵[揾] ³wèn b.f. ①press with the finger ②wipe 揾泪 wènlèi ◆char. 揾钱 wènqián
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 揾:) From 扌(手 shǒu) 'hand' and 昷(𥁕) wēn phonetic.
揾 also has the meaning "press down", which appears to be cognate with 摁 èn; 揾 may even be pronounced èn with this meaning (acc. to Mathews).
