xxxx 搊(S) [chōu] play (musical instruments)  [zǒu] [zhū]
搊[] ³chōu v. 〈wr.〉 play (certain musical instruments)
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form :) From 扌(手) 'hand' and 刍(芻) chú phonetic.
The simplified form  is not in Unicode 6.0. It appears in the ABC Chinese-English Comprehensive Dictionary (DeFrancis 2003, print edition), in 《形音義規範字典》 (2003), and in 《现代汉语规范词典》(2004).
