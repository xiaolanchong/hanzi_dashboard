1538 搭 [dā] put up; hang over; lift together  [dá] [tà]
搭 ¹dā {B} v. ①put up; construct ②hang over ③join together ④travel by or take (a conveyance) | ∼ Mǎ xiānsheng de chē. ∼马先生的车。 Get a ride with Mr. Ma. ⑤lift together ⑥add (money/etc.)
搭 ⁶dá char. 搭茬儿 dáchár* 哪搭 nǎdá(r)*
From 扌(手 shǒu) 'hand' and 荅 dā phonetic.
The right side is 荅, not 答. Compare 拾 shí 'pick up'.
