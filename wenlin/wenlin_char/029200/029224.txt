---- 爨 [cuàn] cook with fire; oven
爨 ³cuàn b.f. build fire and cook 爨婢 cuànbì 析爨 xīcuàn
"Upper part contains in the seal a picture of a stove; below, there is 廾 two hands putting 火 fire to 木 wood (doubled) -- earlier forms may have been simpler" --Karlgren.
The similarity of the top to 興(兴 xìng) seems to be coincidental.
Compare 釁(衅) xìn.
