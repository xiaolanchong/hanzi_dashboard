1056 牆(S墙) [qiáng] (牆壁 qiángbì) wall
牆/墻[墙] ²qiáng {A} n. wall
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 墙:) The right side is 啬(嗇 sè) 'to store; miserly', depicting 回 double walls for 來 grain.
The left side is 爿 qiáng phonetic in the full form 牆, or 土 (tǔ) 'earth' in the variant form 墻 and the simple form 墙.
Compare 蔷嫱樯 qiáng.
