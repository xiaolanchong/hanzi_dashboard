From 牛 (niú) 'ox' and 也 yě phonetic (or 也 may derive from 它 tā).
Among simple form characters, only the earlier form 它 is used for tā 'it'. Among full form characters, 牠 and 它 occur in free variation. 牠 is used more often for animals, and 它 for inanimate objects.
