We call 爲 an "old" form, and 為 (not 爲) the "full" form since only 為 is in the full-form Big5 standard. Otherwise we would have called 爲 the full form (it's still in common use), and called 為 a half-simple form, or (in an optimistic mood) a half-full form, midway between 爲 and 为.
