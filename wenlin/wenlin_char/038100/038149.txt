1355 锅(F鍋) [guō] pot; wok, frying pan; 锅贴 guōtiē pot sticker
锅[鍋] ¹guō {B} n. ①pot; wok; pan; boiler ②bowl (of a pipe/etc.) M:kǒu 口
From 钅(金 jīn) 'metal' and 呙(咼) guō phonetic.
The Cantonese pronunciation is "wok", which has become the English word for a particular kind of Chinese-style deep round-bottomed frying pan.
