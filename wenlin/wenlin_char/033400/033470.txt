---- 芾 [fú] (plants:) luxuriant  [fèi] 蔽芾 bìfèi (leaves:) very small
芾 ²⁶fú b.f. flourishing; growing abundantly 芾芾 ⁶fúfú
芾 ¹⁰fèi char. 蔽芾 ²bìfèi 李芾甘 Lǐ Fèigān
From 艹(草 cǎo) 'grass' and 巿 fú.
巿 may have depicted lianas (trees with air-roots). 巿 and 芾 have sometimes been used interchangeably, for meanings related both to 'knee covers' and to 'abundant vegetation'.
