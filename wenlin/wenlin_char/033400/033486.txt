==== 苎(F苧) [zhù] 苎麻 zhùmá ramie
苎[苧] ¹⁸zhù char. 苎麻 zhùmá*
From 艹(草 cǎo) 'grass' and 宁 zhù phonetic.
Here 宁 is not the simple form of 寧 níng, but an old character which is also found in 佇(伫) zhù and 貯(贮) zhù.
Among simple form characters, since 宁 is the simplified form of 寧 níng, to avoid confusion (?) the old component 宁 is further simplified to 宀 over 一.
