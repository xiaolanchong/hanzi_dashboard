==== 芻(S刍) [chú] (書) hay, fodder; 反芻 ruminate; 芻狗 straw dog  [zōu]
芻[刍] ⁶chú {E} b.f. grass/hay for animal feed 刍秣 chúmò 苾刍 ²bìchú
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 刍:) "A hand and two plants: to cut grass; hay, fodder" --Lindqvist.
刍 chǔ is phonetic in 皱 zhòu 'wrinkle'.
急 (jí 'urgent') is really from 及 jí, not from 刍.
