2139 矣 [yǐ] (grammatical particle)  [xián]
矣 ⁶yǐ {E} m.p. (classical particle, similar in function to modern ¹le) ◆b.f. 殆矣哉 dàiyǐzāi 已矣 ²yǐyǐ
From 厶 (here derived from 已 yǐ 'already') and 矢 (shǐ) 'arrow'.
"Final particle denoting finality, decided subjective opinion -- etymologically cognate to 已... 'finished'; character enlarged by 矢" --Karlgren.
