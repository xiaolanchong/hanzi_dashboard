141  知 [zhī] (知道 zhīdao) know; 知识 zhīshi knowledge  [zhì] (=智)
知 ³zhī {C} v. know; realize; sense ◆b.f. ①inform; notify 通知 tōngzhī ②〈trad.〉 administer 知县 ²zhīxiàn ③be expert in ④knowledge; information 求知 qiúzhī
智/知 ¹²zhì {E} b.f. wisdom; resourcefulness; wit 智慧 ¹zhìhuì*
From 矢 (shǐ) 'arrow' and 口 (kǒu) 'mouth'.
"Know, understand, perceive -- 'to 口 speak so as to 矢 hit the mark,' says the scholastic commentator" --Karlgren.
