---- 瞭 [liào] (瞭望 liàowàng) watch  [liǎo] (variant of 了 in 了然)
瞭/了 ³liào {E} b.f. observe; look out over 瞭望 liàowàng 瞭望塔 liàowàngtǎ
From 目 (mù) 'eye' and 尞 liáo ('torchlight') phonetic.
"Bright eyes, clear of sight" --Karlgren(1923).
The modern standard (since 1986) is to use 瞭 for liào, 了 for liǎo.
