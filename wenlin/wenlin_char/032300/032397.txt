1123 纍(S累) [léi] (rare:) join, bind, tie; 纍纍 léiléi heaps of  [lěi] [lèi]
纍[累] ³léi {E} char. 累赘 léizhui 累累 léiléi
纍, from 畾 léi phonetic and 糸 (sī) 'thread', is the original form of 累. Among modern full form characters both 纍 and 累 are used, 纍 with the reading léi and 累 with the readings lèi and lěi. Among modern simple form characters, only the form 累 still occurs.
