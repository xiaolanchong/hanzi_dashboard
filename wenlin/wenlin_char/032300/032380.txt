683  繼(S继) [jì] (繼續 jìxù) continue; 繼承 jìchéng inherit
繼[继] ⁹jì {D} b.f. ①continue; succeed; follow 继续 ¹jìxù* ②step- (father, mother, etc.) 继父 ¹jìfù ◆adv. then; afterwards
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 继:) From 纟(糸 sī) 'thread' and (㡭) more threads. Karlgren describes the right side 㡭 as "幺 silk thread doubled twice; meaning of the rest uncertain".
Compare 断(斷) duàn 'cut off'.
