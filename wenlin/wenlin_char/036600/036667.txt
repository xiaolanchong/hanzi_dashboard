2513 輻(S辐) [fú] (輪輻 lúnfú) spoke; 輻射 fúshè radiation
輻[辐] ¹²fú {E} b.f. ①spoke (of wheel) 辐条 fútiáo ②radiate 辐射 fúshè
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 辐:) From 车(車 chē) 'car' and 畐 fú phonetic.
The spokes of a wheel radiate out from the center of the wheel. Hence the extended meaning 'radiation'.
"三十辐共一毂(gǔ)。当其无有, 车之用。" --道德经.
