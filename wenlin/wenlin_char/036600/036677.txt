---- 轅(S辕) [yuán] shafts of cart, yamen
轅[辕] ¹¹yuán {E} n. shafts of a cart/carriage ◆b.f. 〈hist.〉 government office 行辕 xíngyuán
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 辕:) From 车(車 chē) 'car' and 袁 yuán phonetic.
"Shafts of a cart; entrance (marked by carts with raised shafts) to the emperor's tent in camp; railing in front of a yamen; a yamen" --Karlgren.
