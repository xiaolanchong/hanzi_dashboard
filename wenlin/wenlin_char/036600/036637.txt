1246 輝(S辉) [huī] shine; 光輝 radiance, glory; 輝煌 -huáng brilliant
輝[辉] ³huī {E} b.f. ①brightness; splendor 光辉 guānghuī ②shine 辉映 huīyìng*
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 辉:) From 光 (guāng) 'light' and 军(軍) jūn phonetic.
Compare 挥 huī 'wave'. Why these both contain 军 jūn is "phonetically very curious" --Karlgren.
