1460 輩(S辈) [bèi] generation; 先輩 older generation, ancestors
輩[辈] ⁵bèi {C} b.f. ①lifetime 一辈子 yị̄bèizi ②generation 长辈 zhǎngbèi(r) ③people of a certain kind 无能之辈 wúnéngzhībèi
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 辈:) From 非 fēi phonetic over 车(車 chē) 'car'.
"Row of carriages; row, group, class, rank, sort; generation" --Karlgren.
