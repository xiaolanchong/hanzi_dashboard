xxxx 軿(S𫐌) [píng] (軿車, 軿輅) curtained carriage
軿[𫐌] ¹⁴píng b.f. ①covered vehicle 𫐌辂 pínglù 辎𫐌 zīpíng* ②vehicle covering
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 𫐌:) From 车(車 chē) 'car' and 并 bìng phonetic.
The simplified form is in Guifan Zidian (p.738) and was added to Unicode 5.2.
Probably the same word etymologically as 屏 píng 'screen'.
