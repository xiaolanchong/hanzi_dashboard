2540 軸(S轴) [zhóu] axle; axis; spool   [zhòu] [zhú]
軸[轴] ¹zhóu {E} n. ①axle; shaft ②axis ③spool; rod ◆m. (for thread/paintings/etc.)
軸[轴] ⁷zhòu {E} char. 大轴子 dàzhòuzi 压轴戏 yāzhòuxì
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 轴:) From 车(車 chē) 'car' and 由 yóu phonetic.
Besides being phonetic, 由 looks like it could represent an axis.
