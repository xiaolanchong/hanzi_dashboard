xxxx 輶(S) [yóu] ancient light-weight carriage
輶[] ¹⁴yóu b.f. ancient light-weight carriage 车 ²yóuchē 轩 yóuxuān
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form :) From 车(車) 'car' and 酋 yǒu phonetic.
The simplified form  is not in Unicode 6.0. It appears in the ABC Chinese-English Comprehensive Dictionary (DeFrancis 2003, print edition), in 《现代汉语词典》, and in 《现代汉语规范字典》.
