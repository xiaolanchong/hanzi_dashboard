---- 噩 [è] shocking
噩/咢 ¹¹è {E} b.f. 〈wr.〉 ①shocking ②suffering bad luck 厄运 èyùn* ③severe; serious ④fierce; frightening 恶梦 èmèng ⑤simple ⑥fat; heavy
An older form is 咢, from 吅 and 亏.
"Beat the drum, give alarm; startle, startling -- the first form (咢) has 屰 below in the seal, now deformed: to attack with 吅 great shouts; second form (噩) is a modern corruption" --Karlgren(1923).
Etymologically the same word as 愕 è as in 愕然 èrán 'astounded'.
Compare 恶 è 'evil'.
