1514 嚇(S吓) [xià] frighten, scare  [hè] (恐嚇) threaten; (interj.)
嚇[吓] ²xià {B} v. frighten; scare; intimidate
嚇[吓] ²hè {E} intj. tut-tut ◆v. threaten; intimidate
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 吓:) From 口 (kǒu) 'mouth' and 下 xià phonetic. The full form 嚇 contains 赫 hè 'very red' (赤 chì 'red' doubled) rather than 下.
