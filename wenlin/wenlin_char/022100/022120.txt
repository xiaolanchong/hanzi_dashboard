622  器 [qì] utensil; (机器 jīqì) machine; 武器 wǔqì weapon
器 ²qì {D} b.f. ①vessel; utensil; ware 瓷器 ¹cíqì* ②instrument 器具 ¹qìjù* ③weapon 武器 wǔqì* ④organ (of the body) 器官 ¹qìguān* ⑤talent; aptitude 大器晚成 dàqìwǎnchéng
From four 口 (kǒu) mouths and a 犬 (quǎn) dog.
Utensils for feeding dogs? Dog-feeding machine?
"The vessels for the 口 mouths, for eating. In the middle, a 犬 dog that cleans them" --Wieger.
