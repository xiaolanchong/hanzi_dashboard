---- 蕃 [fán] luxuriant; multiply  [fān] (=番)  [bō] 土蕃  [pí]
蕃 ¹⁰bō char. 土蕃 Tǔbō*
番/蕃 ⁵fān b.f. foreign 番茄 fānqié*
蕃 ⁵fán b.f. flourishing; luxuriant; abundant 蕃茂 ²fánmào
From 艹(草 cǎo) 'grass' and 番 fān phonetic.
Pronounced fán, probably etymologically the same morpheme as 繁 fán. The ancient pronunciations were the same according to Karlgren(1923).
