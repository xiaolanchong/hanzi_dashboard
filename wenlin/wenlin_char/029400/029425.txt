1539 狱(F獄) [yù] lawsuit; (监狱 jiānyù) prison, jail
狱[獄] ³yù {E} b.f. ①prison; jail 监狱 jiānyù* ②lawsuit; criminal case 冤狱 yuānyù*
From 犭(犬 quǎn) 'dog', 讠(言 yán) 'words' and 犬 (quǎn) another dog. Words between dogs: lawsuit: prison.
Compare 辩(辯) biàn 'argue': 言 words between 辛 criminals. Also 雠(讎) chóu 'enemy': 言 words between 隹 birds.
