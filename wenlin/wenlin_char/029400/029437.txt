2983 狽(S狈) [bèi] 狼狽 lángbèi in a difficult position
狽[狈] ⁹bèi {E} char. 狼狈 lángbèi
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 狈:) From 犭(犬 quǎn) 'dog' and 贝(貝) bèi phonetic.
Originally 狈 bèi meant "a wolf-like animal" (--Karlgren). Now it is used only in the word 狼狈 lángbèi "in a difficult position" (surrounded by wolves?).
