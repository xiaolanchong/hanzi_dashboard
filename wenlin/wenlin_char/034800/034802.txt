1017 蟲(S虫) [chóng] worm, insect; 爬蟲 reptile  [zhòng] [tóng]
蟲[虫] ¹chóng {E} n. insect; worm
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 虫:) Picture of a worm or snake.
The full form 蟲 is 虫 tripled. (虫 by itself used to be pronounced huǐ, but that rare word is now written 虺 huǐ, and 虫 is used as an abbreviation for 蟲 chóng).
虫 is the 142nd of the 214 radicals.
