2152 蠟(S蜡) [là] wax; (蠟燭 làzhú) candle
蠟[蜡] ³là {E} n. ①wax ②candle M:⁴zhī 支 ③polish ◆b.f. yellowish; sallow 蜡黄 làhuáng*
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 蜡:) From 虫 (chóng) 'insect' (bee: beeswax), and 巤 liè phonetic.
巤 is replaced by 昔 (xī) in the simple form 蜡.
Compare 猎(獵) liè 'hunt' and 腊(臘) là '12th moon'.
