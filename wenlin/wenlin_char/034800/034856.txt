xxxx 蠨(S蟏) [xiāo] 蟏蛸 xiāoshāo a kind of spider
蠨[蟏] ²²xiāo char. 蟏蛸 xiāoshāo
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 蟏:) From 虫 and 萧(蕭) xiāo phonetic.
A xiāoshāo is dark brown, with a long thin body and very long legs. They like to make webs indoors, and they are regarded as omens of good luck.
