2517 蠢 [chǔn] (愚蠢 yúchǔn) stupid
蠢 chǔn {D} s.v. stupid; foolish; dull; clumsy | Háiyǒu bǐ tā gèng ∼ de rén ma? 还有比他更∼的人吗? Is there anyone more stupid than him? ◆b.f. wriggle 蠢动 chǔndòng*
From 春 chūn phonetic over 虫 (chóng) 'worm' doubled.
The basic meaning is 'wriggle' (蠢动 chǔndòng), hence the 虫 worms.
