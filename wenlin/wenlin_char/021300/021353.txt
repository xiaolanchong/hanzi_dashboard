==== 卩 [jié] (radical 26, component: 'seal'; 单耳朵 dān ěrduo)
No single-syllable words currently in dictionary.	add new s.s.w.
Picture of a person kneeling. A symbol of authority.
Formerly thought to represent a sceptre or seal (for signing documents); only from archeological evidence uncovered in the 20th century is it clear that 卩 depicted a person kneeling.
Also written 㔾 or 巴 (but distinct in origin from 巴 bā).
Written  in 节爷疖卫.
