308  却(F卻) [què] however
却[卻] ¹què* {B} adv. ①but; yet ②indeed | Yuánlái ∼ shì tā. 原来∼是他。 It's him after all. ③now (in storytelling) | ∼shuō ∼说 Now the story goes ... ④just | ∼ gòu ∼够 just enough ◆b.f. ①step back; retreat 却步 quèbù ②drive back; repulse 却敌 quèdí ③reject; refuse 推却 tuīquè ◆conj. however; but ◆suf. (indicating completion/totality)
From (simple form:) 去 qù or (full form:) 谷 gǔ phonetic, and 卩 (jié), a person kneeling.
"Desist from, decline, reject; on the contrary, but; however, in fact" --Karlgren.
却(卻) què is phonetic in 脚(腳) jiǎo 'foot'.
