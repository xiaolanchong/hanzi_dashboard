1142 卷 [juǎn] roll; 春卷 spring roll (F捲)  [juàn] volume, book, exam paper  [quán] (=鬈)  [quān] [gǔn] [jùn]
卷[-/捲] juǎn(r)* {B} n. roll ◆v. ①roll up ②sweep off; carry along ◆m. roll; spool; reel
卷 ¹juàn {E} m. volume ◆b.f. ①examination paper 考卷 kǎojuàn(r) ②file; documents; dossier 卷宗 juànzōng ③roll; rolled document or picture 画卷 ¹huàjuàn* ④book
鬈/卷 ¹³quán b.f. curly (hair); beautiful hair 鬈发 quánfà* 鬈曲 ⁴quánqū
Two  hands rolling 米 (mǐ) rice into dumplings; 㔾=卩 (jié) 'seal' added to the bottom to mean 'document'.
Cognate with 拳 quán 'fist' (the hand rolled up).
Pronounced juǎn, the variant form 捲 is sometimes used among 繁体字.
