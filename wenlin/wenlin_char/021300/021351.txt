1905 卧(F臥) [wò] lie down; 卧室 wòshì bedroom
卧[臥] ²wò {C} v. ①lie down ②crouch; sit (of animals/birds) ③〈topo.〉 poach (eggs)
The left side 臣 (chén 'minister') depicts an eye sideways, the way a person's eye looks when they are lying down.
The right side is 人 (rén) 'person', written like 卜 (bǔ) in the simple form.
卧 occurs as a component in 监(監) jiān and 临(臨) lín.
