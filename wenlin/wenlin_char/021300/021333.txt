391  单(F單) [dān] alone; single; sheet   [chán] 单于  [Shàn] (surname)
单[單] ¹dān {B} b.f. ①single; sole 单身 ¹dānshēn ②odd (numbered) 单数 dānshù ③weak 单薄 dānbó ◆adv. ①singly ②alone
单[單] ¹⁴chán char. 单于 chányú
单[單] ¹Shàn b.f. county in Shandong ◆n. Surname ◆char. 单于 ³shànyú
"Single, alone; only; odd (in number); single sheet, a list, a bill, a slip" --Karlgren.
The origin of 单(單) is obscure. Lindqvist proposes it may have represented hunting stones tied with a rope. Consider 弹 dàn as in 子弹 zǐdàn 'bullet', and 兽(獸) shòu 'beast'.
The top is 吅 in the full form 單, reduced to 丷 in the simple form 单.
