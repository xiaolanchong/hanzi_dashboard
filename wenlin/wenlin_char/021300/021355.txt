749  卫(F衛) [wèi] 保卫 defend; 卫生 sanitary; 卫星 satellite
卫[衛] ⁸wèi {E} b.f. ①guard; defend 保卫 ¹bǎowèi 卫兵 wèibīng* ②protect (against disease) 卫生 wèishēng* ◆n. Surname
The full form 衛 is 韋 wéi phonetic inside of 行 (xíng) 'walk'.
Cognate with 围(圍) wéi 'surround', but 卫(衛) wèi is pronounced with the falling tone.
