2550 玫 [méi] 玫瑰 méigui rose; 黄刺玫 yellow rose
玫 ¹⁰méi {E} char. 玫瑰 méigui
From 王(玉 yù) 'jade' and 攵(文) wén phonetic (in this case, 攵 is from 文 wén, not from 攴 pū). Originally 玫瑰 méigui meant a kind of beautiful jade; later the word was borrowed for the name of a beautiful flower, the rose.
Compare 梅 méi 'plum' and 草莓 cǎoméi 'strawberry'.
