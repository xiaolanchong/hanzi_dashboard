2411 蝶 [dié] 蝴蝶 húdié butterfly; 蝶蛹 diéyǒng chrysalis  [tiē]
蝶 ⁴dié {E} b.f. ①butterfly 蝴蝶 húdié ②butterfly-shaped 蝶骨 diégǔ
From 虫 (chóng) 'insect' and 枼 yè phonetic.
The right side 枼 is the old form of 叶(葉) yè 'leaf'. An appropriate phonetic, since leaves are where butterflies like to spend their time.
Compare 碟谍堞揲喋牒蹀鲽 dié.
