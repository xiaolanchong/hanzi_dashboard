---- 鞠 [jū] 鞠躬 jūgōng to bow  [qū] [qiōng]
鞠 ⁵jū {E} v. 〈wr.〉 rear; bring up ◆b.f. make a bow 鞠躬 ¹jūgōng*
From 革 (gé) 'leather' and 匊 jū phonetic.
"Leathern football; child, young, bring up (children)" --Karlgren.
"To nourish... To inform... A ball... A child... To bend in submission" --Mathews.
鞠 has sometimes been used in place of 鞫 jū 'interrogate'.
