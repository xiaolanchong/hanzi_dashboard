418  音 [yīn] (声音 shēngyīn) sound; 音乐 yīnyuè music
音 ²yīn {C} n. 〈lg.〉 phone ◆b.f. ①sound 声音 ¹shēngyīn* ②tone 音律 yīnlǜ ③news; tidings 音信 yīnxìn* ④〈lg.〉 syllable 音节 yīnjié* ⑤phoneme 音位 ²yīnwèi ⑥pronunciation 发音 ¹fāyīn*
The bottom 曰 (yuē) is 口 (kǒu) 'mouth' with a horizontal line in it, representing the tongue, or sound coming out. The top may have depicted a flute; now it looks like 立 (lì 'stand'). The tops of 音 yīn 'sound' and 言 yán 'words' were originally identical. 音 yīn and 言 yán may be cognate.
