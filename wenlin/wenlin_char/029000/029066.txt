1662 熊 [xióng] bear; 熊猫 xióngmāo panda
熊 ²xióng {D} n. ①bear ②Surname ◆v. 〈coll.〉 ①scold | Wǒ ∼le tā yị̄ dùn. 我∼了他一顿。 I scolded him. ②oppress; tyrannize ◆s.v. ①mean; malicious; merciless ②loutish; oafish
From 能 (néng) 'bear' and 灬 (火 huǒ) 'fire'. 能 depicted a bear; now it is used for the word néng 'able'. Whether the added 灬 four dots depict the bear's feet, or 火 fire cooking bear meat, is uncertain.
