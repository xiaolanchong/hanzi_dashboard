---- 毓 [yù] to feed, to rear
毓 ³⁰yù b.f. (used especially in personal names) give birth; nurture 钟灵毓秀 zhōnglíngyùxiù
From 每 (měi) 'every' and 㐬 (liú) 'baby upside down with hair'.
According to Karlgren(1923), 每 as a component in 毓
means, 'give constant attention'; and 毓 yù 'rear and foster children' is the same word (etymologically) as 育 yù 'raise, educate'.
