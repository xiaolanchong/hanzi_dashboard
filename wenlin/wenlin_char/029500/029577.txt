988  玉 [yù] (玉石 yùshí) jade; 玉米 yùmǐ corn
玉 ⁵yù {D} n. jade ◆b.f. ①gems of all kinds 美玉 ¹měiyù* ②〈wr.〉 fair; beautiful (of women) 玉颜 ⁴yùyán ③〈court.〉 your 玉照 ²yùzhào
Originally 王: three beads of jade on a string. A 丶 dot was added to distinguish it from 王 wáng 'king'. Where 王 occurs as a component (as in 理 lǐ etc.), it usually derives from 玉 yù 'jade'.
