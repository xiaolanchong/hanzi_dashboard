2194 玄 [xuán] black, dark; profound, abstruse; 玄学 metaphysics  [xuàn]
玄 ³xuán {E} s.v. profound; abstruse ◆b.f. ①black; dark 玄青 xuánqīng ②〈coll.〉 unreliable; incredible 玄妙 xuánmiào
From 亠 and 幺 (亠 was originally 入 rù).
"To 入 put the 幺 thread in the dye; dyed thread; GREEN color (later on, BLACK, on account of certain Taoist theories)" --Wieger.
玄 is the 95th of the 214 radicals.
