723  園(S园) [yuán] (花園 huāyuán) garden; (公園 gōngyuán) park
園[园] ⁶yuán {D} b.f. ①garden 花园 huāyuán(r/zi)* ②place for public recreation 公园 ¹gōngyuán*
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 园:) From 囗 (wéi) 'surround', suggesting a garden fence, and (full form:) 袁 yuán phonetic or (simple form:) 元 yuán phonetic.
