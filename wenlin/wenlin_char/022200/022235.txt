129  四 [sì] four; 四周 sìzhōu all around
四 ¹sì* {A} num. four; 4 ◆cons. ∼ A ¹bā B used with two similar terms A and B to indicate combination | ∼ xiāng bā ²zhèn ∼乡八镇 all the towns and villages
"Four. Numerical sign. Even number, which is easily divided into two halves. The old form graphically represents the division of 四 into halves" --Wieger.
There are, of course, other explanations.
