1020 圓(S圆) [yuán] round; circle; (sometimes used for 元 dollar)
圓[圆] ⁴yuán {A} s.v. round ◆b.f. ①satisfactory; comprehensive 圆满 yuánmǎn ②make plausible; justify 圆场 yuánchǎng 自圆其说 zìyuánqíshuō ③circle 圆周 yuánzhōu*
元/圆[-/圓] ²yuán {A} m. ①Chinese monetary unit ②dollar
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 圆:) From 囗 (wéi) 'surround' and 员(員) yuán.
Etymologically the same word as 员 (〇 round 贝 money), which now means 'member'. Compare 元 'dollar', 原 'original', 源 'source', 园 'park', all pronounced yuán.
