1515 赤 [chì] red; bare; 赤道 chìdào equator; 赤脚 chìjiǎo barefoot
赤 ²chì {E} b.f. ①red 赤色 chìsè ②loyal; sincere; single-hearted 赤诚 chìchéng ③bare; naked 赤裸裸 chìluọ̌luǒ
Originally 大 (dà) 'big' (person with arms outstretched; now 土) over 火 (huǒ) 'fire'. A 大 big red 火 fire; or a 大 person with a 火 burning emotion, blushing red.
