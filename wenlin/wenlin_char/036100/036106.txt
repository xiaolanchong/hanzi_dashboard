1134 贊(S赞) [zàn] 贊成 approve of; 稱贊 praise
贊[赞] ²zàn {E} b.f. ①support; assist 赞助 ¹zànzhù ②agree with 赞成 zànchéng*
赞[贊/讚] ³zàn b.f. praise; commend 赞扬 zànyáng* ◆n. eulogy
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 赞:) From 先 (xiān) 'first' (doubled) and 贝(貝 bèi) 'money'.
"先 doubled: two persons coming forward with 貝 money" --Karlgren(1923).
A variant form 讚 with 言 added is sometimes used for the meanings 'praise' and 'eulogy'.
