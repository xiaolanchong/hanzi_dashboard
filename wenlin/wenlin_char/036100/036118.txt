2969 贖(S赎) [shú] redeem; ransom; atone; 贖買 shúmǎi redeem, buy out  [shù]
贖[赎] ²shú {E} v. redeem; ransom ◆b.f. atone for 赎罪 shúzuì*
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 赎:) The left side is 贝(貝 bèi) 'money'.
The right side was a phonetic component, an obsolete character that has come to look just like 卖(賣 mài 'sell').
Compare 续(續) xù 'continue' and 读(讀) dú 'read'.
