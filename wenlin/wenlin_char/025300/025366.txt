1803 挖 [wā] dig; excavate
挖 ¹wā* {B} v. dig; excavate | Gōngrén zài zhèr ∼le yī̠ tiáo gōu. 工人在这儿∼了一条沟。 The workers have dug a ditch here.
From 扌(手 shǒu) 'hand' and 穵 yà 'cave; hollow', which is 穴 (xué) 'hole' over 乙 yǐ phonetic. 穵 was formerly also used for wā 'dig', yà and wā being cognates;  the 'hand' radical was added for the verb.
