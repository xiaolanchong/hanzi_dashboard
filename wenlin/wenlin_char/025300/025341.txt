---- 拽 [zhuài] pull, haul  [zhuāi] fling, throw, hurl  [yè] (=曳)
拽 zhuài* {D} v. pull; drag; haul | Bié ∼ wǒ xiùzi. 别∼我袖子。 Don't pull my sleeve.
曳/拽 ⁸yè {E} b.f. drag 拉曳 lāyè 拖曳机 tuōyèjī
拽 zhuāi v. fling; throw; hurl
From 扌(手 shǒu) 'hand' and 曳 (yè) 'haul'.
Originally a variant of 曳 yè, then applied to a synonymous word zhuài.
