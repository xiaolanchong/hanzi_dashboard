2741 挟(F挾) [xié] hold under one's arm; coerce
挟[挾] ³xié {E} v. ①hold sth. under the arm ②embrace ③coerce; hold hostage ④harbor (resentment/etc.) ⑤〈wr.〉 rely on; depend on
From 扌(手 shǒu) 'hand' and 夹(夾) jiā 'squeeze'.
Etymologically the same word as 夾, which depicts a 大 big person squeezing 从 two little people under the arms.
The pronunciation xié was simply a variant of jiā.
