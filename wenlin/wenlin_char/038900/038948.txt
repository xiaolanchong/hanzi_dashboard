---- 頤(S颐) [yí] ䷚ The Corners of the Mouth; 頤和園
頤[颐] ¹¹yí {E} b.f. ①cheek/chin 颐辅 yífǔ ②nourish; care for 颐养 ²yíyǎng
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 颐:) From 𦣞 yí 'chin' and 页(頁 yè) 'head'.
The left side is not 臣 (chén), it is 𦣞, which was a sideways picture of a chin.
See also 熙 xī and 姬 jī.
