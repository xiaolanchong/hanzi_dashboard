2126 韵(F韻) [yùn] (押韵 yāyùn) rhyme; 韵文 yùnwén verse
韵[韻] ⁵yùn {E} n. 〈lg〉 rhyme; rhyme-class ◆b.f. ①tone; agreeable sound 韵调 yùndiào(r) ②charm; appeal 风韵 fēngyùn ③final; syllable final 韵母 yùnmǔ ④main vowel 韵腹 ²yùnfù ⑤allophone
From 音 (yīn) 'sound' and (full form:) 員 yuán or (simple form:) 匀 yún phonetic. The simple form 韵 dates back at least to the Song dynasty.
