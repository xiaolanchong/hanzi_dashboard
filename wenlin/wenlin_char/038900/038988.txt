233  題(S题) [tí] topic; 問題 question, problem; 題目 title  [dì]
題[题] ²tí {B} n. ①topic; subject; title ②problem; exam question ◆v. inscribe
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 题:) From 是 shì phonetic and 页(頁 yè) 'head'.
"Forehead; frontispiece, heading; theme, thesis, proposition; inscription; write, pronounce, mention" --Karlgren.
Compare 提 tí 'raise'.
