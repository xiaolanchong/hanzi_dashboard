2785 頃(S顷) [qǐng] 公頃 gōngqǐng hectare; 頃刻 qǐngkè in an instant  [qīng] [kuǐ]
頃[顷] ²qǐng {E} m. (of area equal to 100 mǔ or 6.667 hectares) ◆adv. 〈wr.〉 just; just now ◆b.f. a little while 有顷 ²yǒuqǐng
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 顷:) From 𠤎 (huà) 'change' and 页(頁 yè) 'head'.
"頁 head 𠤎 turned" --Karlgren.
"(An inclination of the head:) a moment, a short while; (also applied to a homophonous word:) an area of a little more than 6 hectares (100 亩)" --Karlgren(1923).
The original word is now written 倾(傾) qīng 'lean'.
