1250 頗(S颇) [pō] quite, rather  [pò] [pí]
頗[颇] ²pō {D} adv. quite; very; considerably ◆b.f. inclined to one side; slanting; tilting 偏颇 piānpō
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 颇:) From 皮 pí phonetic and 页(頁 yè) 'head'.
The primitive meaning was 'lean over, incline', etymologically the same word as 坡 pō (山坡 shānpō 'hillside').
