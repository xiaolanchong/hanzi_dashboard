946  額(S额) [é] (前額) forehead; 定額 quota
額[额] ³é {D} n. forehead ◆b.f. ①specified quantity 名额 ¹míng'é ②horizontal tablet 匾额 biǎn'é ③〈art〉 hanging scroll mounted as panel; tablet-like mountings 横额 héng'é*
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 额:) From 客 kè phonetic and 页(頁 yè) 'head'.
"Forehead; frontispiece, inscription; regulation; fixed number" --Karlgren.
