1130 桌 [zhuō] table; 一张桌子 one table
桌 ¹zhuō* {E} b.f. table; desk 桌子 zhuōzi* ◆m. (for tablefuls)
The bottom 木 (mù) 'wood' suggests the meaning 'table'.
The top,  over 日, comes from 卓 zhuó 'high', which is a cognate word. The bottom of 卓 is replaced by 木.
"High, elevated; (an 'elevated' stand:) table" --Karlgren(1923).
