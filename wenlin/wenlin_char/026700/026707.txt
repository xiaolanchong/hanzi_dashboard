---- 桓 [Huán] (surname)
桓 ⁸huán char. 桓拨 huánbō 盘桓 pánhuán* ◆n. Surname
From 木 (mù) 'wood' and 亘. 亘 may be the old form of 宣 xuān, phonetic in 桓; or 亘 may be the old form of 桓 itself. 亘 originally looked like a spiral; 桓 was formerly borrowed for 还(還) huán 'turn round'.
"Pillar, column, post; erect, martial" --Karlgren(1923).
