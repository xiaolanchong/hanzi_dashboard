310  條(S条) [tiáo] twig; strip; item; 條件 tiáojiàn condition  [tiāo]
條[条] ¹tiáo* {A} b.f. ①sth. long and narrow; strip; slip 柳条 liǔtiáo(r/zi) ②stripe; streak 线条 xiàntiáo ③item; article; clause 条文 ¹tiáowén* ④short note 条子 ¹tiáozi* ⑤〈lg.〉 string ⑥〈art〉 scroll suspension loop made of plain silk ◆m. (for long, narrow things)
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 条:) 条 could represent the 夂 twigs and other items on top of a 木 tree.
In reality, the full form 條 is from 攸 yōu phonetic and 木 (mù) 'tree' signific. 亻 and 丨 are omitted in the simple form 条.
Compare 修 xiū 'repair'.
