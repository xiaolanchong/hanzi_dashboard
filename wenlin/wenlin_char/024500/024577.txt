575  态(F態) [tài] form; 态度 tàidu manner; 状态 zhuàngtài state
态[態] ²tài {E} b.f. ①appearance; state; condition 形态 xíngtài* ②attitude; demeanor 态度 tàidu* ③〈lg.〉 voice; aspect 主动态 zhǔdòngtài ④〈comp.〉 mode
From 太 tài phonetic over 心 (xīn) 'heart'. The full form 態 has 能 néng on top, instead of 太 (the reason is obscure).
