819  忘 [wàng] (忘记 wàngjì) forget  [wáng] 忘八
忘 ²wàng {A} v. ①forget | Wǒ ∼le fāxìn. 我∼了发信。 I forgot to mail the letter. ②overlook; neglect
From 亡 wáng 'disappear' and 心 (xīn) 'heart'.
Cognate with 亡 wáng.
"(Mentally lost:) absent-minded, forget" --Karlgren.
Don't confuse 忘 wàng with 忙 máng 'busy', which is composed of the same two components.
