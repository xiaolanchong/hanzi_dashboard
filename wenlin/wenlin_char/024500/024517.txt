318  必 [bì] (必须) must; 不必 needn't
必 ³bì {C} adv. ①certainly; surely ②must; have to | Xiànzài nǐ bụ̀∼ lái le. 现在你不∼来了。 There's no longer any need for you to come.
Mnemonic: If you cross your 心 heart, then you 必 must. Originally, 必 had nothing to do with 心 xīn 'heart':
"From 弋 (arrow used as) tally, signific and 八 bā phonetic" --Karlgren.
