1034 孙(F孫) [sūn] grandchild; 子孙 descendants; 孙悟空; 孙中山
孙[孫] ¹sūn {E} b.f. ①grandson 孙子 sūnzi* 外孙 wàisūn(zi) ②generations below that of grandchild 曾孙 zēngsūn* ③second growth of plants 稻孙 dàosūn ◆n. Surname
From 子 (zǐ) 'child' and 系 (xì) 'connect' (in the full form 孫) or 小 (xiǎo) 'small' (in the simple form 孙).
"系 connection, succession of 子 children" --Karlgren.
