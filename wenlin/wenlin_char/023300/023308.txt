2345 嬌(S娇) [jiāo] graceful, delicate; 嬌嫩 jiāonèn tender and lovely
嬌[娇] ⁴jiāo {D} s.v. ①fragile; frail; delicate ②squeamish; finicky ◆b.f. tender; lovely; charming 娇嫩 jiāonen ◆v. pamper; spoil
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 娇:) From 女 (nǚ) 'woman' and 乔(喬) qiáo phonetic.
Compare 姣 jiāo, very similar in meaning; the pronunciations were different in ancient Chinese, and still are different in Cantonese.
