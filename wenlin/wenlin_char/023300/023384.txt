535  存 [cún] (存在 cúnzài) exist; 保存 bǎocún preserve
存 cún {B} v. ①store; keep; preserve ②accumulate; collect; gather | ∼ diǎnr qián yǐ ⁴bèi wànyī. ∼点儿钱以备万一。 Save some money for the unexpected. ③deposit (money) | Bǎ qián ∼ yínháng. 把钱∼银行。 Deposit money in the bank. ④leave with; check (luggage) ⑤reserve; retain ⑥remain on balance; be in stock ⑦cherish; harbor ⑧〈comp.〉 save ◆b.f. exist; live; survive 生存 shēngcún
The first three strokes (𠂇 plus 丨) were originally 才 cái 'talent'. The lower right part is 子 (zǐ) 'child'.
"To propogate, survive, continue to exist... 才 capacity of getting 子 children; 才 deformed as in 在" --Karlgren.
Compare 在 zài 'at'.
