==== 錕(S锟) [kūn] 錕鋙 name of a precious sword  [gǔn]
No single-syllable words currently in dictionary.	add new s.s.w.
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 锟:) From 钅(金 jīn) 'metal' and 昆 kūn phonetic.
Kūnwú was the name of a legendary mountain, whose metal was used to make fine swords.
The pronunciation kūnwú is given by 现代汉语规范字典.
