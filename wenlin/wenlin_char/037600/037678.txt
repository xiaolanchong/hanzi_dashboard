==== 錮(S锢) [gù] to plug with molten metal
錮[锢] ⁵gù {E} v. ①plug with molten metal; run metal into cracks ②〈wr.〉 hold in custody; imprison
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 锢:) From 钅(金 jīn) 'metal' and 固 gù 'solid'.
Etymologically the same word as 固.
"To strengthen by stopping up crevices with molten metal; to stop, obstruct; obstinate; chronic disease" --Karlgren.
