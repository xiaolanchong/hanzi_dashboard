==== 鋶(S锍) [liǔ] matte; 鋶冶煉 matte smelting
鋶[锍] ³liǔ n. matte | ∼ yěliàn ∼冶炼 matte smelting
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 锍:) From 钅(金 jīn) 'metal' and 㐬 liú phonetic.
Not found in Mathews, 李氏 or Karlgren. 汉语大字典 and 现代汉语词典 give technical definitions in Chinese. I finally found it in 现代科学技术词典: "matte".
