2580 陋 [lòu] 丑陋 chǒulòu ugly
陋 ³lòu {E} b.f. ①narrow and small 陋巷 ¹lòuxiàng* ②ugly 丑陋 chǒulòu ③vile; mean 陋习 lòuxí ④ignorant; crude; simple-minded 愚陋 yúlòu ⑤poor (of performances/etc.); inferior 简陋 ¹jiǎnlòu* ⑥stingy
"Narrow, confined; low, vile; ignorant, vulgar" --Karlgren.
Originally from 内 (nèi) 'inside' and 匚: "Shut up 内 inside a 匚 trunk" --Karlgren. The top line of 匚 joined with 内, making 丙 plus 𠃊, and 阝(阜 fù) 'hill' was added (it is not clear to me why, possibly in connection with the meaning 'low').
