---- 阜 [fù] mound, hill; abundant; (radical 170, 阝 on LEFT side) (cf. 邑)
阜 ²²fù 〈wr.〉 n. mound; hill ◆b.f. abundance 物阜民丰 wùfùmínfēng
Picture of a flight of stairs.
As a component, written 阝, always on the left side.
"Note the two derivatives, 陰 yīn, the shady side of a hill (North); and 陽 yáng, the sunny side of a hill (South). It is now used to mean the dual powers, day and night, life and death, male and female, etc." --Wieger.
