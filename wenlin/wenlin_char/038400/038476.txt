2337 陌 [mò] 陌生 mòshēng strange; 陌生人 stranger
陌 ⁸mò {E} b.f. ①path between fields 阡陌 qiānmò ②strange; not acquainted with 陌生 mòshēng*
From 阝(阜 fù) 'hill' and 百 bǎi phonetic.
"Elevated path between fields" --Karlgren.
From the original meaning 'path' (compare 阡 qiān), came the extended meaning 'stranger whom one passes in the street' (陌路 mòlù), and then 陌生 mòshēng 'strange'.
