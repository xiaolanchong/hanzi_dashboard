942  阵(F陣) [zhèn] battle array; (量): 一阵风
阵[陣] ¹zhèn {B} n. ①battle array ②position; front ③a period of time ◆v.m. (for passing phases/spells) ◆b.f. battle; fight 阵亡 zhènwáng*
From 阝(阜 fù) 'hill' and 车(車 chē) 'car'.
"The commentators consider 陣 as a corruption of 陳; it would then be a variant of the same stem. One could otherwise imagine: a 阜 (wall:) defending row of 車 war chariots" --Karlgren.
Compare 陈(陳) chén 'display'.
