---- 阡 [qiān] path between fields running north & south; 阡陌 crossing paths
阡 ⁹qiān {E} b.f. north-south path between fields 阡陌 qiānmò ◆char. 喀尔巴阡山 Kā'ěrbāqiān Shān
From 阝(阜 fù) 'hill' and 千 qiān phonetic.
阡 qiān means 'path between fields running north and south', while 陌 mò at one time meant 'path between fields running east and west'.
