2378 瓊(S琼) [qióng] beautiful jade; 瓊脂 agar  [xuán]
瓊[琼] ³qióng b.f. a high-quality jade 琼瑶 qióngyáo* ◆n. short name for Hainan
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 琼:) The simple form 琼 is 王(玉 yù) 'jade' plus 京 jīng phonetic.
In the full form 瓊, the phonetic part is 夐 xiòng, which is ⺈ over 冖 over 儿 over 目 over 夂.
