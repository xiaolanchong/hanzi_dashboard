786  環(S环) [huán] ring; 環境 huánjìng environment, surroundings  [huàn]
環[环] ²huán {B} b.f. ①ring; circle 耳环 ěrhuán ②link 铁环 tiěhuán* ③surround; encircle 环绕 huánrào
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 环:) From 王(玉 yù) 'jade' and 睘 huán phonetic; 睘 has been replaced by 不 in the simple form. The primitive meaning is 'bracelet', hence 玉 yù 'jade'. Etymologically the same word as 还(還) huán 'return' (turn around, round trip).
