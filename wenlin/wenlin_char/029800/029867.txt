xxxx 璫(S珰) [dāng] richly ornamented; pearls for pierced ears; ancient headgear
璫[珰] ⁵dāng b.f. earring; decorative ear pendant 璧珰 bìdāng 貂珰 diāodāng 耳珰 ěrdāng
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 珰:) From 王(玉 yù) 'jade' and 当(當) dāng phonetic.
The full form 璫 is sometimes used in place of 噹 for "ding-dong".
