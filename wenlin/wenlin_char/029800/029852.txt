---- 璜 [huáng] ancient semi-annular ceremonial jade pendant
璜 ¹³huáng n. semi-circular jade tablet ◆char. 装潢 zhuānghuáng
From 王(玉 yù) 'jade' and 黄(黃) huáng ('yellow') phonetic.
Possibly 黄 originally depicted a person wearing jade ornaments around the waist. Compare 珩 héng.
"黄實古玉佩之象也。" "後假為黄白字, 卒至假借義行而本義廢, 乃造珩若璜以代之, 或更假用衡字。" --郭沫若 «金文叢攷». (As quoted in 漢語大字典.)
