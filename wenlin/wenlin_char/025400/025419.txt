==== 捋 [lǚ] stroke, smooth out with the fingers   [luō] rub one's palm along
捋 ⁴lǚ {E} v. smooth out with the fingers; stroke
捋 ²luō v. rub one's palm along sth. | Tā ∼qǐ xiùzi jiù gànkāi le. 他∼起袖子就干开了。 He rolled up his sleeves and started to work.
Originally 寽, from two hands 爫 and 寸; another hand 扌(手 shǒu) was added.
