---- 掐 [qiā] pinch; dig nails into; pick (flowers); cut off, nip; choke
掐 ¹qiā {D} v. ①pinch; clutch; strangle; choke ②〈slang〉 fight ◆m. 〈topo〉 handful/bunch/pinch/etc.
From 扌(手 shǒu) 'hand' and 臽 xiàn phonetic.
"To pinch; to dig the nails into; to pick (flowers)" --Chao & Yang.
Not to be confused with 搯 tāo (variant of 掏).
