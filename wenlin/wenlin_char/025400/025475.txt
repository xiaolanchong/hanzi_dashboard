1386 掃(S扫) [sǎo] sweep; 打掃 clean up  [sào] 掃帚 broom
掃[扫] ²sǎo {B} v. ①sweep ②glance at ◆b.f. ①sweep away; eliminate 扫除 sǎochú 扫盲 sǎománg ②put all together 扫数 sǎoshù ③paint (the eyebrows/etc.)
扫[掃] ²sào {E} b.f. broom 扫帚 sàozhou 扫把 sàobǎ
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 扫:) From 扌(手 shǒu) 'hand' and 帚 (zhǒu) broom.
In the simple form 扫, 帚 is abbreviated to 彐.
Compare 妇(婦) fù 'woman'.
