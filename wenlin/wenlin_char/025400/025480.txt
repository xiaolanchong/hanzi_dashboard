1183 授 [shòu] award, give; teach; 教授 jiàoshòu professor
授 ⁵shòu {D} b.f. ①confer; give 授权 ¹shòuquán* ②instruct 授课 ¹shòukè*
授 shòu 'give' is etymologically the same word as 受 shòu 'receive', the character being enlarged by 扌(手 shǒu) 'hand' (受 already has two hands in it: 爫 and 又). Giving and receiving are the same event construed two different ways.
