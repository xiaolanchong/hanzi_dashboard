1131 损(F損) [sǔn] decrease; damage; ䷨; 损失 sǔnshī lose, loss
损[損] ¹sǔn* {D} b.f. ①decrease; lose 损失 sǔnshī* ②harm; damage 损害 sǔnhài ◆s.v. ①be mean/shabby ②〈topo.〉 be sarcastic/caustic
From 扌(手 shǒu) 'hand' and 员(員) yuán 'member' (the function of 员 in 损 is uncertain, not likely phonetic; 员 originally meant 'round' and 'money' like modern 圆 and 元).
