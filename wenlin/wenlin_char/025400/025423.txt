2427 捏 [niē] hold between the fingers; knead; fabricate
捏 niē* {C} v. ①hold between the fingers; pinch ②knead with the fingers; mold ◆b.f. fabricate; trump up 捏造 niēzào
From 扌(手 shǒu) 'hand' and 圼 niè 'to work in clay'.
Cognate with 圼 niè, which is composed of 日, representing a lump of clay, and 土 (tǔ) 'earth'.
"To work in clay, knead; to make, concoct" --Karlgren.
Also cognate with 涅 niè 'alunite (black clay)'.
A variant form is 揑.
