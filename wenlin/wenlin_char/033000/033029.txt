1683 脅(S胁) [xié] upper part of side of person's body; coerce  [xiàn] [xī]
脅[胁] ⁷xié {E} b.f. ①upper part of the human body 胁下 xiéxià ②coerce; force 胁迫 xiépò*
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 胁:) The full form 脅 is 劦 xié phonetic over 月(肉 ròu) 'flesh'.
The simple form 胁 is 月 plus 办 (bàn).
劦 is the old form of 协(協) xié 'cooperate'.
