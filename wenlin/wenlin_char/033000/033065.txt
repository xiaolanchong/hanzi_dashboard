---- 脩 [xiū] dried meat; tuition; teacher's salary  [yǒu] [tiáo] [xiāo]
脩 ⁶xiū b.f. ①dried meat ②student's gift to a teacher as tuition 脩脯 xiūfǔ 束脩 ²shùxiū
From 攸 yōu phonetic and 月(肉 ròu) 'flesh'.
The original meaning is 'dried meat'.
Confucius accepted a bundle of dried meat from a student too poor to pay any other tuition. Hence the extended meaning 'tuition'.
This character 脩 has also been used as a variant of 修 xiū.
