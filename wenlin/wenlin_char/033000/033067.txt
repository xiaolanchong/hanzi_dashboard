956  脫(S脱) [tuō] take off; 脫離 tuōlí break away
脫[脱] ²tuō {A} v. ①cut meat off the bones ②cast/come off; shed; take off ③leave out (words) | Zhè háng ∼le yị̄ ge zì. 这行∼了一个字。 One word is missing in this line. ◆b.f. ①become disconnected 脱离 tuōlí* ②escape from 逃脱 táotuō 脱险 tuōxiǎn* ③neglect; slight ◆conj. 〈wr.〉 if; in case
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 脱:) From 月(肉 ròu) 'flesh' and 兑(兌) duì (☱ 'joy') phonetic. 兑 is purely phonetic, the meaning 'joy' having nothing to do with taking off clothes and exposing 肉 flesh.
