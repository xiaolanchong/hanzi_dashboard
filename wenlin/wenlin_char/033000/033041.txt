648  脑(F腦) [nǎo] (脑子 nǎozi) brain; 脑袋 head; 脑壳 skull
脑[腦] ¹nǎo {E} n. brain ◆b.f. brain-like material 豆腐脑 dòufunǎo(r)
Originally 𡿺: 巛 hair over 囟 head; then 月(肉 ròu) 'flesh' was added, giving the full form 腦. The right side is 㐫 in the simple form 脑. (A variant form is 匘.) Compare 恼(惱) nǎo (as in 恼火 nǎohuǒ 'irritated').
