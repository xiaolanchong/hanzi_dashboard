1092 训(F訓) [xùn] lesson
训[訓] ²xùn {D} v. ①lecture; teach; train; explain ②scold; admonish ◆b.f. ①teachings; instruction; a “lesson” 教训 jiàoxun* ②〈trad.〉 standard; model; example 家训 jiāxùn ③train 训练 xùnliàn ④critical explanation of a text 训诂 xùngǔ*
From 讠(言 yán) 'words' and 川 chuān 'river'.
"Instruct, teach; admonish; (take lesson from:) imitate, follow, approve -- a 川 flow of 言 words; or is 川 phonetic in spite of the initial?" --Karlgren.
