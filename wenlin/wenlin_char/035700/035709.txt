1622 譽(S誉) [yù] praise; 榮譽 róngyù honor; 名譽 míngyù fame
譽[誉] ⁷yù {E} b.f. ①(good) reputation; fame 名誉 ¹míngyù* 声誉 ¹shēngyù* ②praise; eulogize 称誉 chēngyù
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 誉:) From 與 yǔ phonetic over 言 (yán) 'words'.
與 on top is abbreviated to 兴 (xìng) in the simple form 誉.
Compare 举(舉) jǔ.
