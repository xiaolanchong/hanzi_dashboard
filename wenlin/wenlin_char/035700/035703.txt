598  護(S护) [hù] (保護) guard, protect; 護士 hùshi nurse
護[护] ²hù {C} b.f. protect; guard 保护 ¹bǎohù ◆v. be partial to; shield; cover
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 护:) From 扌(手 shǒu) 'hand' and 户 hù ('door') phonetic.
The full form 護 is completely different: from 言 (yán) 'words' and 蒦 huò phonetic.
