171  位 [wèi] position; (量) (polite measure word for people)  [lì]
位 ²wèi {A} b.f. ①place; location 位置 wèizhi ②status; condition 地位 ¹dìwèi* ◆m. 〈court.〉 (for persons)
From 亻(人 rén) 'person' and 立 (lì) 'stand'.
"Position, place, office; seat, throne; respected person -- a 人 man 立 standing: position (at court)." --Karlgren(1923).
