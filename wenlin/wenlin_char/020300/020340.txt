==== 佴 [Nài] (surname)    [èr] second; assistant
No single-syllable words currently in dictionary.	add new s.s.w.
From 亻(人 rén) 'person' and 耳 ěr phonetic.
"Second; assistant" --Karlgren.
Karlgren says 佴 èr is cognate with 二 èr 'two'. He does not mention the surname Nài, but he gives the ancient pronunciation as *nzi, so it seems plausible that nài and èr are modifications of the same original pronunciation.
