2039 佩 [pèi] (ornament); 佩服 pèifu admire; 敬佩 jìngpèi esteem
佩 ²pèi {E} v. wear (at the waist, etc.) ◆b.f. admire 佩服 pèifu ◆n. 〈trad.〉 pendant waist ornament
From 亻(人 rén) 'person', 凡 (fán) 'every', and 巾 (jīn) 'cloth'.
"Ornaments worn at the waist...凡 all kinds of things carried in 巾 the girdle by 人 a man..." --Karlgren.
Note how the dot in 凡 became a horizontal line 一 over 巾.
