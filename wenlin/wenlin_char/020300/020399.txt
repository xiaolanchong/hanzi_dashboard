2068 侯 [hóu] marquis; (surname)  [hòu]
侯 ³hóu {E} n. Surname ◆b.f. ①marquis 侯爵 hóujué ②nobleman or high official 侯门似海 hóuménsìhǎi ③archery target 射侯 shèhóu
A marquis: a 亻(人 rén) person who shoots 矢 (shǐ) arrows at a  target.
“...The shooting at a target was used in antiquity, for the election of feudatories and officials...” --Wieger.
Compare 候 hòu as in 时候 and 等候, which has one more stroke.
