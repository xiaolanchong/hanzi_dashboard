2587 佣(F傭) [yōng] 佣人 servant; 雇佣 hire  [yòng] 佣金 commission
佣[傭] ³yōng {E} b.f. ①hire 雇佣 gùyōng ②servant 佣妇 yōngfù
佣[傭] ²yòng {E} b.f. commission; brokerage fee 佣金 yòngjīn*
From 亻(人 rén) 'person' and (full form:) 庸 yōng 'usual' or (simple form:) 用 yòng 'use'.
Cognate with both 庸 yōng 'usual' and 用 yòng 'use'.
"(Use a person:) engage for hire; ordinary" --Karlgren.
