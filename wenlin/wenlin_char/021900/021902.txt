xxxx 喎(S㖞) [wāi] crooked (mouth); 喎僻不遂 facial paralysis
喎[㖞] ²wāi b.f. crooked (of facial features) 㖞僻不遂 wāipìbùsuí
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 㖞:) From 口 (kǒu) 'mouth' and 呙(咼) guō phonetic.
Same word as 歪 wāi 'crooked', used to refer to the mouth/face in particular, as in 口眼㖞斜, where 㖞斜 wāixié is the same as 歪斜.
