579  喜 [xǐ] happiness; 喜欢 xǐhuan like  [xī] [chì]
喜 ²xǐ {D} b.f. ①like; be fond of 喜欢 xǐhuan ②happy; delighted; pleased 欢喜 huānxǐ* ③happy event (esp. wedding) 喜事 ¹xǐshì* ④〈coll.〉 pregnancy 有喜 yọ̌uxǐ
From 壴 (zhù) 'drum' (as in 鼓 gǔ 'drum') over 口 (kǒu) 'mouth'.
"To beat the 壴 drum and 口 sing" --Karlgren.
The resemblance to 吉 jí 'lucky' is coincidental.
The symbol 囍 is called 双喜 shuāngxǐ 'double happiness'.
