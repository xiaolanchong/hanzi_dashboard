xxxx 鷃(S) [yàn] small quail-like birds
鷃/鴳[] ²⁰yàn b.f. small quail-like bird 雀 ²yànquè 鹏 péngyàn
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form :) From 晏 yàn phonetic and 鸟(鳥 niǎo) 'bird'.
The simplified form  is not in Unicode 6.0. It appears in the ABC Chinese-English Comprehensive Dictionary (DeFrancis 2003, print edition), in 《现代汉语规范字典》 and 《现代汉语词典》.
A variant form is 鴳.
