==== 鶉(S鹑) [chún] 鵪鶉 ānchun quail; 鶉衣 chúnyī ragged clothes  [tuán]
鶉[鹑] ⁵chún {E} b.f. ①quail 鹌鹑 ānchun* ②ragged, patched pattern 鹑服 chúnfú
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 鹑:) From 享(𦎧) chún phonetic and鸟(鳥 niǎo) 'bird'.
The phonetic component 𦎧 chún now looks like 享 (xiǎng 'feast').
