xxxx 鷇(S) [kòu] fledgling
鷇[] ⁶kòu b.f. newly hatched bird; fledgling 食 kòushí 音 kòuyīn
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form :) From 殻 ké phonetic (slightly distorted), and 鸟(鳥 niǎo) 'bird'.
The simplified form  is not in Unicode 6.0. It appears in the ABC Chinese-English Comprehensive Dictionary (DeFrancis 2003, print edition), in 《形音義規範字典》, and in 《现代汉语词典》.
