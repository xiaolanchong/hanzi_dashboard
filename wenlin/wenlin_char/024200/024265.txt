1977 廉 [lián] honest, clean; low-priced
廉 ⁶lián {E} b.f. ①honest and upright 廉洁 ³liánjié ②low-priced; cheap 廉价 liánjià*
From 广 'house' and 兼 jiān phonetic.
"There was an Archaic kl- or gl- somewhere" says Karlgren, explaining how 兼 jiān could be phonetic in 廉 lián. The old meaning was 'corner' of a 广 house; corner --> square --> honest.
