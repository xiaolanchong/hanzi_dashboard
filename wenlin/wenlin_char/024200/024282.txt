1798 廚(S厨) [chú] 廚房 chúfáng kitchen
廚[厨] ⁴chú {E} b.f. ①kitchen 厨房 chúfáng* ②a cook 厨师 ¹chúshī
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 厨:) A 厂 room for cooking 豆 beans with your 寸 hands? The full form 廚 has 壴 instead of 豆, and 广 instead of 厂. The inside part, 尌 shù, is really phonetic, the same as in 树(樹) shù 'tree'.
