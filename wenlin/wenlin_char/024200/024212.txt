应[應] ³yìng {C} b.f. ①respond 响应 xiǎngyìng* 相应 ¹xiāngyìng ②consent; comply 答应 dāying* 应诺 yìngnuò ③adapt to 应景 yìngjǐng(r)* ④cope/deal with 应付 yìngfu* ⑤apply; applied 应用 yìngyòng* 应用科学 yìngyòng kēxué
The full form 應 is composed of 鷹 yīng ('eagle') phonetic (minus 鳥) and 心 (xīn) 'heart' signific. The simple form 应 has 倠 and 心 replaced by a four-stroke rough outline.
