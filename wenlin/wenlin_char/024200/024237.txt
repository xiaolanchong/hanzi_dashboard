796  庭 [tíng] 家庭 jiātíng family; 法庭 court; 庭院 courtyard
庭 ³tíng {E} b.f. ①hall ②front (court)yard 庭院 ¹tíngyuàn* ③law court 法庭 fǎtíng*
From 广 'house' and 廷 tíng 'royal court'.
"Court, palace hall, audience chamber; mansion, house family -- etymologically the same word as (廷); character enlarged by 广" --Karlgren.
