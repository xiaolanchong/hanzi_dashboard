2986 鹼(S碱) [jiǎn] alkali; soda
鹼/礆[碱/硷] ⁴jiǎn {C} n. ①alkali ②soda
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 碱:) The full form 鹼 is composed of 鹵 (lǔ) 'halogen' and 僉 qiān phonetic.
The simple form 碱 is composed of 石 (shí) 'stone' and 咸 xián phonetic.
There are various variant forms: 硷, 礆, 鹻, 堿...
Not to be confused with 鹹(咸) xián 'salty'.
