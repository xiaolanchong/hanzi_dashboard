2959 炫 [xuàn] dazzle; show off, brag, boast
炫/眩/衒 ²xuàn {E} b.f. display; dazzle; show off 炫耀 ¹xuànyào* 炫露 xuànlù 炫鬻 xuànyù 自炫 zìxuàn
炫 ⁸xuàn b.f. bright; dazzling light 炫目 ¹xuànmù*
From 火 (huǒ) 'fire' and 玄 xuán phonetic.
"Bright, brilliant" --Karlgren(1923).
Compare 眩 'dazzling, dizzy', and 衒 'peddle; expose, recommend, praise'. 炫 is mostly used now instead of 衒 for the meaning 'show off', but 炫/眩/衒 are often interchangeable.
