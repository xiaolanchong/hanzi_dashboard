==== 烝 [zhēng] (烝民, 烝黎) people, masses
烝 ¹²zhēng b.f. multitudinous 烝黎 zhēnglí 烝民 zhēngmín
From 丞 chéng phonetic and 灬(火 huǒ) 'fire'.
"Rising steam, to steam, distil; rise, advance, progress, forcible, capable; voluminous, multitudinous, many, all" --Karlgren(1923).
'Steam' is now written 蒸, with 艹(草 cǎo) 'grass' added. 烝 means 'multitudinous' in 烝黎, 烝民.
