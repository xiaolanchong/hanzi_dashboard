1541 烏(S乌) [wū] (烏鴉) crow; (烏黑) black; 烏龜 tortoise  [wù] [yā]
烏[乌] ⁴wū {E} b.f. ①crow 乌鸦 ¹wūyā* ②black; dark 乌云 wūyún* ◆n. Surname
乌[烏] ²⁰wù char. 乌拉 wùla 乌拉草 wùlacǎo
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 乌:) Like 鸟(鳥 niǎo) 'bird', but missing the dot in the head; the eye is invisible because a crow's eye is black like the feathers.
