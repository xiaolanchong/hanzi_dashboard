1411 炼(F煉) [liàn] smelt, refine; temper metal; 锻炼 exercise
炼[煉] ¹liàn {C} v. ①smelt; refine ②temper ③train ④〈Ch. med.〉 stew herbs/etc.
From 火 (huǒ) 'fire' and 柬 jiǎn phonetic; in the simple form 炼, 柬 is replaced by ; compare 拣(揀) jiǎn. Notice that the component (柬 jiǎn) is not the same as 东(東 dōng 'east').
Etymologically, 炼 liàn is the same word as 练(練) liàn 'practice'.
