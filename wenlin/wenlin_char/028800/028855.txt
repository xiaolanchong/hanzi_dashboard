==== 炷 [zhù] (书) wick of oil lamp; burn; measure for joss sticks
炷 ¹⁴zhù b.f. ①wick (of an oil lamp) 灯炷 ²dēngzhù ②burn (joss sticks) 炷香 zhùxiāng ◆m. (for joss sticks)
From 火 (huǒ) 'fire' and 主 zhǔ phonetic.
"Wick; candle; incense stick; to light" --Karlgren.
If 主 zhǔ really depicted a lamp with a flame on top, then there might be some etymological relationship between 主 zhǔ and 炷 zhù.
