1687 炒 [chǎo] stir fry, saute; 炒饭 fried rice; 炒面 chow mein
炒 ²chǎo {C} v. ①stir-fry; fry; sauté | Zhè cài néng ∼ ma? 这菜能∼吗? Can this vegetable be stir-fried? ②〈coll.〉 ⓐbuy and sell at a profit ⓑselect ⓒdismiss; fire; sack | Lạ̌obǎn bǎ tā gẹ̌i ∼ le. 老板把他给∼了。 His boss fired him.
From 火 (huǒ) 'fire' and 少 shǎo.
Compare 烧 shāo roast, cook.
