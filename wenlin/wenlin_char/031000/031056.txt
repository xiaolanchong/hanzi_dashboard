---- 祐 [yòu] (same as 佑 in 保佑 bless and protect)
佑/祐 ⁶yòu {E} b.f. protect 佑护 yòuhù 保佑 bǎoyòu
From 礻(示 shì) 'altar' and 右 yòu 'right'.
Etymologically the same word as 右 and 佑 yòu 'assist'. 祐 has 礻(示) added to indicate the specific meaning of assistence from heaven, blessing.
"Aid, protect, especially heaven's protection" --Karlgren.
