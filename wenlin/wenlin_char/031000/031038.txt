168  社 [shè] (社会 shèhuì) society
社 ¹shè* {D} b.f. ①organized body; agency; society; club 社团 shètuán ②people's commune 人民公社 rénmín gōngshè ③god of the land 社稷 ²shèjì ④sacrifices to the god of the land 赛社 sàishè
From 礻(示 shì) 'altar' and 土 (tǔ) 'earth'.
"Altar to the spirits of the land; sacrifice to the spirits of the land; tutelary deity, village, society -- the 示 religious rites for 土 the land" --Karlgren.
