2553 樞(S枢) [shū] 樞紐 shūniǔ pivot, axis  [ōu]
樞[枢] ¹⁰shū {E} b.f. pivot; hub; center 枢纽 shūniǔ* 户枢 ²hùshū
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 枢:) From 木 (mù) 'wood' and 区(區) qū. An obsolete synonymous word was written with this character, and 區 qū was phonetic. The character was then applied to the word shū.
