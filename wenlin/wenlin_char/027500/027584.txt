1337 毀(S毁) [huǐ] destroy; 毀滅 huǐmiè exterminate
毀[毁] ¹huǐ {C} v. ①destroy; ruin | Tā bǎ jīyào wénjiàn gẹ̌i ∼ le. 他把机要文件给∼了。 He destroyed confidential documents. ②〈topo.〉 refashion; make over ◆b.f. ①burn up ②defame; slander 毁谤 huǐbàng
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 毁:) From 臼 (jiù) 'mortar', 土 (tǔ) 'dirt' or 工 (gōng), and 殳 (shū) 'beat'.
"To 殳 beat to 土 powder (mostly corrupted: 工) in 臼 a mortar" --Karlgren(1923).
