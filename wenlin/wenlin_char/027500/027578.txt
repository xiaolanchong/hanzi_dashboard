821  殺(S杀) [shā] kill  [shài] [sà] [xiè] [shì]
殺[杀] ¹shā* {B} v. ①kill ②fight ③smart; burn (of medicine) ④weaken; reduce; abate ⑤take off; counteract ◆suf. exceedingly
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 杀:) From 乂 (yì) 'shears' and 木 (mù) 'tree'.
To 乂 cut a 木 plant: decapitate.
The full form 殺 has 殳 (shū) 'beat' added.
