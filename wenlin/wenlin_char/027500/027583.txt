2290 殿 [diàn] hall, palace
殿 ¹⁰diàn {D} n. hall; palace; temple ◆b.f. the rear 殿军 diànjūn
From 尸 (shī) 'body', 共 (gòng 'share'), and 殳 (shū) 'beat'.
"In the seal 共 is a picture of a chair, and 尸 being a 'sitting man' the left part as a whole is said to mean 'buttock' and 殿 the place where the buttock is 殳 beaten: hall of justice etc. Interpretation doubtful" --Karlgren.
