2073 毅 [yì] 毅力 willpower; 坚毅 jiānyì determined
毅 ¹⁷yì {E} b.f. resolute 意志 ¹yìzhì 坚毅 jiānyì
The left side 豙 is from 辛 (xīn) 'criminal' over 豕 (shǐ) 'pig', 辛 being contracted into 立 (sharing its bottom stroke with 豕). The primitive meaning, says Karlgren, was "enraged pig". To this was added 殳 (shū) 'beat', to emphasize the violent determination of the wild boar.
