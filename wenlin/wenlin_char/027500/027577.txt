From 医 yì phonetic and 殳 (shū) 'hit'.
"Sound of hitting; echo" --Karlgren(1923). "A particle" --Karlgren(1940).
医 was originally used for a word yì meaning 'quiver':
"A 匚 case for 矢 arrows; sometimes used for 醫" --Karlgren(1923).
殹 yì is phonetic in 醫; the modern simple form of 醫 is 医.
