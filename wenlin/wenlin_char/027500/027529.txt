---- 殉 [xùn] be buried alive with dead; sacrifice oneself
殉 ³xùn {E} b.f. ①be buried alive with the dead 殉葬 xùnzàng ②die for 殉国 ¹xùnguó
From 歹 'death' and 旬 xún phonetic.
"Follow in the grave, be buried (alive) with somebody; to die for, sacrifice oneself; wholly devoted to, bent on, greedy of, desire" --Karlgren(1923).
