==== 毄 [jī] (variant of 擊击)
No single-syllable words currently in dictionary.	add new s.s.w.
From 軎, end of a 車 war chariot's axle, and 殳 beat. See 击 jī for picture and explanation. 毄 is simplified slightly to 𣪠 when it occurs as a component in 擊, 繋, and 墼: notice how the 口 in 毄 is reduced to 凵 in 𣪠.
