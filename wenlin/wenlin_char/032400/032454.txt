676  细(F細) [xì] thin; 仔细 zǐxì careful; 细胞 xìbāo cell; 详细 detailed
细[細] ¹xì {A} s.v. ①thin; slender ②fine; in small particles ③thin and soft ④exquisite; delicate ⑤careful; meticulous; detailed ⑥minute; trifling
From 纟(糸 sī) 'thread' and 田 (tián 'field').
"The 糸 (thread:) hair of the 田 head, see 思" --Karlgren.
田 by itself means 'field', but here it represents a head.
