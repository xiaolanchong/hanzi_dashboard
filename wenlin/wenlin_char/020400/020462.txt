642  修 [xiū] repair, decorate; 修改 xiūgǎi revise
修 ¹xiū* {B} v. ①embellish; decorate ②repair; mend; overhaul ③write; compile ④study; cultivate ⑤build; construct ⑥trim; prune ◆s.v. 〈wr.〉 long; tall and slender ◆ab. xiūzhèngzhǔyì
The meaning 'adorn, decorate' (修饰 xiūshì) is suggested by 彡 'feathers'. 攸 yōu is phonetic, as it is in 悠 yōu and 條(条) tiáo.
