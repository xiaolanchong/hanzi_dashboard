---- 俎 [zǔ] vessel or cutting board for meat
俎 ⁵zǔ b.f. ①〈trad.〉 receptacle for sacrificial meat 俎豆 zǔdòu ②cutting board; butcher block 刀俎 dāozǔ ◆n. Surname
"A utensil for holding meat at feasts and sacrifices. A board for cutting meat. -- Two pieces of 肉 meat offered in sacrifiece on or before an altar or, possibly, an ancestral tablet (cf. 祖)" --Creel.
