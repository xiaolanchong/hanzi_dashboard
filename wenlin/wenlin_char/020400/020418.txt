259  係(S系) [xì] 聯係 liánxì connect; 關係 guānxi connection
係[系] ⁵xì v. 〈wr.〉 be ◆b.f. relate to; bear on 关系 guānxi*
From 亻(人 rén) 'person' and 系 xì.
Etymologically the same word as 系 xì. Enlarged by 亻(人 rén) 'person'. 係 does not occur among simple form characters, 系 is always used instead.
Compare 繫 jì, xì.
