個[个] ¹gè* {A} pref. individual ◆m. (reading/citation pronunciation for ge)
個[个] ⁴gě char. 各个儿 gègěr 自个 zìgě(r)
From 亻(人 rén) 'person' and 固 gù phonetic. 個 gè is the measure word for 人 rén. The simple form 个 is not actually a simplification of 個, but a very old character representing a twig of bamboo (half of 竹 zhú). A variant form is 箇.
