1200 俗 [sú] common, rustic, vulgar; custom; 俗话 proverb, saying
俗 sú {D} b.f. ①vulgar 俗气 súqi* ②popular; common; current 通俗 tōngsú* ③custom; convention 风俗 fēngsú* ④secular; lay 僧俗 sēng-sú
From 亻(人 rén) 'person' and 谷 gǔ 'valley'.
"Was 谷 phonetic in spite of the initial, or is the meaning: a 人 man from 谷 the mountains, a rustic?" --Karlgren.
