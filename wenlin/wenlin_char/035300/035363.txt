2901 訣(S诀) [jué] rhymed formula; (訣竅) knack; tricks of the trade
訣[诀] ⁷jué {E} b.f. ①rhymed mnemonic formula 口诀 ¹kǒujué ②knack; tricks of the trade 秘诀 mìjué ③bid farewell; part 永诀 ¹yǒngjué
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 诀:) From 讠(言 yán) 'words' and 夬 guài 'break-through'. Cognate with 夬 and 决 jué 'decide'.
"Finger trick; give signs with the fingers; secret, occult, mystery; secret formula; parting words at death; take leave" --Karlgren.
