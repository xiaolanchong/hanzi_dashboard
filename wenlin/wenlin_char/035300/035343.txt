xxxx 訏(S) [xū] mislead; brag; large  [xǔ] 訏訏 vast
訏[] ¹⁵xū b.f. brag; grand 谟 xūmó
[訏] ⁵xǔ char.  ²xǔxǔ
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form :) From 讠(言) and 于.
"To brag; grand, vast" --Karlgren(1923).
"To boast. Great, important... Great, large" --Mathews.
The simplified form is not in Unicode 6.0. It appears in the ABC Chinese-English Comprehensive Dictionary (DeFrancis 2003, print edition), in 《现代汉语词典》, and in 《形音義規範字典》.
Compare 訐 jié.
