1193 触(F觸) [chù] touch; 接触 jiēchù get in touch; 触角 antenna
触[觸] ²chù {D} v./b.f. ①touch; contact 接触 jiēchù* ②strike; hit 触礁 chùjiāo* ③move sb.; stir up sb.'s feelings 触动 ¹chùdòng
角 (jiǎo) 'horn, antenna' suggests the meaning 'touch'. The full form 觸 contains 蜀 shǔ ('silkworm') phonetic. The simple form 触 has 虫 (chóng) 'worm' in place of 蜀.
Compre 独(獨) dú 'alone'.
