訴[诉] ⁴sù {E} b.f. ①tell; relate; inform 告诉 gàosu* ②complain; accuse 诉苦 sùkǔ ③sue; appeal 起诉 ¹qǐsù* 上诉 ¹shàngsù
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 诉:) From 讠(言 yán) 'words' and 斥 (chì) 'denounce'.
"斥 blaming 言 words" --Karlgren.
斥 has one more stroke than 斤 (jīn 'axe').
