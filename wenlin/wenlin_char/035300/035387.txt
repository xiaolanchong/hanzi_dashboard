525  註(S注) [zhù] notes; 註釋 zhùshì comment; 註册 zhùcè register
註[注] ⁶zhù {E} v. annotate ◆b.f. record; register 注册 zhùcè ◆n. notes; annotation
From 言 (yán) 'words' and 主 zhǔ phonetic.
The character 注 zhù is often used instead of 註, so for example 注 釋 is written instead of 註釋. Among simple form characters, only 注 occurs.
