1193 觸(S触) [chù] touch
觸[触] ²chù {D} v./b.f. ①touch; contact 接触 jiēchù* ②strike; hit 触礁 chùjiāo* ③move sb.; stir up sb.'s feelings 触动 ¹chùdòng
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 触:) 角 (jiǎo) 'horn, antenna' suggests the meaning 'touch'. The full form 觸 contains 蜀 shǔ ('silkworm') phonetic. The simple form 触 has 虫 (chóng) 'worm' in place of 蜀.
Compre 独(獨) dú 'alone'.
