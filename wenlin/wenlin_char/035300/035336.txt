240  計(S计) [jì] (計算) count, calculate; 統計 statistics
計[计] ⁵jì {C} v. count; compute; calculate ◆v./n. plot; plan ◆b.f. ①meter; gauge 温度计 wēndùjì ②idea ◆n. Surname
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 计:) From 讠(言 yán) 'words' and 十 (shí) 'ten'.
The 十 ten 言 words used for counting are 一二三四五六七八九十.
