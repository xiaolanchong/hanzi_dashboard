==== 訥(S讷) [nè] (訥口) to stammer
訥[讷] nè b.f. stammer; speak haltingly 讷讷 nènè 木讷 mùnè
(The glyphs in the Unicode 2.0 and 3.0 books have 内. The Unicode 1.0 book had 內.)
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 讷:) From 讠(言 yán) 'words' and 内(內) nèi phonetic.
Compare 呐 nà; the characters 呐 and 訥 are sometimes interchangeable (see Mathews 4609).
