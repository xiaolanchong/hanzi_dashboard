---- 邑 [yì] city; 采邑 càiyì fief; 通都大邑 tōngdū-dàyì metropolis  [è]
邑 ⁴³yì b.f. city; town 城邑 ³chéngyì ◆char. 邑邑 ¹⁹yìyì
"Seat 囗 of the government's 卩 authority" --Wieger.
囗 (wéi) represents a wall around a city; 巴(卩 jié) 'seal' represents government authority. As a component, abbreviated to 阝, always on the RIGHT side; compare 阜 fù.
邑(阝) is the 163rd of the 214 radicals.
