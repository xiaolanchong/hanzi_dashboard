邊[边] ¹biān(r)* {A} n./suf. ①side ②margin; edge; rim ③limit | Tā de huà tài méi ∼ le. 他的话太没∼了。 What he said is inflated. ④Surname ◆b.f. border; boundary 边界 biānjiè* ◆cons. ∼ V1 ∼ V2 V1 and V2 simultaneously | Tā ∼ gàn ∼ xué. 他∼干∼学。 He's learning on the job.
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 边:) The full form 邊 is 臱 mián phonetic (自 over 穴 over 方), over 辶(辵 chuò) 'go'. The simple form 边 replaces 臱 with 力 (lì), an arbitrary abbreviation.
