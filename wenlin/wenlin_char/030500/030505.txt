---- 眩 [xuàn] (昏眩 hūnxuàn) dizzy; 眩晕 xuànyùn vertigo  [huàn] [juàn]
炫/眩/衒 ²xuàn {E} b.f. display; dazzle; show off 炫耀 ¹xuànyào* 炫露 xuànlù 炫鬻 xuànyù 自炫 zìxuàn
眩 ⁹xuàn b.f. dizzy; dazzled; bewildered 眩晕 xuànyùn* 目眩 mùxuàn
From 目 (mù) 'eye' and 玄 xuán ('dark') phonetic.
"Dim sight, confused; mistaken, deceived" --Karlgren(1923).
Compare 炫 'dazzle', which is often interchangeable with 眩.
