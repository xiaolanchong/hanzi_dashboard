---- 睽 [kuí] ䷥ Opposition (38); 睽睽 kuíkuí stare; gaze  [kuì] [jì]
睽/暌 ⁴kuí {E} b.f. separated; isolated 睽隔 kuígé
From 目 'eye' and 癸 guǐ.
Kerson Huang translated the hexagram name as 'Abandoned'. Wilhelm/Baynes translated it 'Opposition'. In the Mawangdui manuscript of the 易经, ䷥ was named 乖 guāi, translated by Edward Shaughnessy as 'Perverse'.
