==== 眦(F眥) [zì] corner of the eye
眦[眥] ⁵zì b.f. corners of the eye; where the upper and lower eyelids come together; the eye 眦目 ²zìmù 睚眦 yázì 目眦欲裂 mùzìyùliè
From 目 (mù) 'eye' and 此 cǐ phonetic.
The form 眥 is 此 over 目. (We call 眦 the "simple" form and 眥 the "full" form, only in accordance with the GB and Big5 codes).
