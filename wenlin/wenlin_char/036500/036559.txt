xxxx 軏(S𫐄) [yuè] crossbar on neap of carriage; 輗軏 carriage alignment
軏[𫐄] ¹⁴yuè b.f. point where the crossbar is attached to the tongue of a carriage 𫐐𫐄 níyuè
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 𫐄:) From 车(車 chē) 'car' and 兀 wù phonetic.
"Crossbar on neap of carriage" --Karlgren(1923).
The simplified form is in XHC and Guifan Zidian, and was added to Unicode 5.2.
