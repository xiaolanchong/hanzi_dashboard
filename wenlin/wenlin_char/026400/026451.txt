---- 杓(=􁀣𣏐) [sháo] [biāo] [shuó] [dí] [zhuó] (Unihan:) handle of cup, ladle, spoon; name of a constellation
勺/杓[-/-] ¹sháo(r) {E} n. spoon; ladle; dipper ◆m. 〈hist.〉 (of capacity equal to one centiliter)
杓 ⁷biāo n. 〈trad.〉 the three stars at the end of the handle of the Big Dipper
From 木 (mù) 'tree' and 勺.
