---- 杳 [yǎo] (杳渺 yǎomiǎo, 杳冥 yǎomíng) dim; obscure; 杳然 yǎorán quiet, lonely
杳/窈/窅 ³yǎo {E} b.f. obscure; distant; deep; secluded 杳霭 yǎo'ǎi 窈窈 ²yǎoyǎo 杳然 ¹yǎorán
From 木 (mù) 'tree' and 日 (rì) 'sun'.
"Sundown, darkness: mysterious, vague, solitary; the 日 sun below the 木 trees" --Karlgren(1923).
Compare 杲 gǎo.
