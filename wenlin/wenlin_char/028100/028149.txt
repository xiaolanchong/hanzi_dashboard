2632 淵(S渊) [yuān] 深淵 shēnyuān abyss
淵[渊] ²yuān {E} b.f. deep 渊博 yuānbó* 深渊 ²shēnyuān
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 渊:) The old form was 𣶒:
"Picture of whirling water at the bottom of an abyss" --Karlgren.
氵(水 shuǐ) 'water' was added later to make the full form 淵.
In the simple form 渊, the right side is 𫂱.
