2661 涵 [hán] (涵洞) culvert; 涵养 hányǎng self-control; conserve  [hàn]
涵 ⁴hán {E} b.f. ①contain (implicitly) 含义 ¹hányì* ②tolerance; forbearance 包涵 bāohan ③culvert 涵洞 hándòng
From 氵(水 shuǐ) 'water' and 函 hán 'container'.
"Submerge; vast, capacious; magnanimous; sometimes used for 函; etymologically the same word as 函; character specifyingly enlarged by 水" --Karlgren(1923)
