==== 浣 [huàn] to wash; 10 days; 浣熊 huànxióng raccoon
浣 ⁹huàn {E} b.f. ①wash 浣熊 huànxióng 浣衣 ²huànyī ②〈hist.〉 any of the three ten-day divisions of a month 上浣 shànghuàn
From 氵(水 shuǐ) 'water' and 完 wán phonetic.
A raccoon is called a 浣熊, literally "wash bear", probably because raccoons wash their food in water (if available) before eating.
