1355 鍋(S锅) [guō] pot; wok, frying pan; 鍋貼 guōtiē pot sticker  [guǒ]
鍋[锅] ¹guō {B} n. ①pot; wok; pan; boiler ②bowl (of a pipe/etc.) M:kǒu 口
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 锅:) From 钅(金 jīn) 'metal' and 呙(咼) guō phonetic.
The Cantonese pronunciation is "wok", which has become the English word for a particular kind of Chinese-style deep round-bottomed frying pan.
