2903 粤(F粵) [Yuè] 粤语 Yuèyǔ Cantonese; 粤剧 yuèjù Guangdong opera
粤[粵] Yuè {E} n. short name for Guangdong/Guangxi
The top was 奥(奧) ào, 'place of the house gods, mystery', now contracted into 丿 plus 囗 plus 米. The bottom is 丂 (qiǎo) 'exclamation'. The character 粤 was used for a variant of the word 曰 yuē 'speak', then was borrowed for Yuè, the name of 广东(廣東) Guǎngdōng and 广西(廣西) Guǎngxī provinces.
