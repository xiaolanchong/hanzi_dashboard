2980 糠 [kāng] chaff
糠 ¹kāng {D} n. chaff; bran; husk ◆s.v. dry and spongy (usu. of vegetables) ◆v. shiver
Originally written 康, a 庚 gēng threshing tool with 米 (mǐ) grains of rice falling around it. 康 was borrowed for kāng as in 健康 jiànkāng 'health', and another 米 was added for the original meaning 糠 kāng 'chaff'.
