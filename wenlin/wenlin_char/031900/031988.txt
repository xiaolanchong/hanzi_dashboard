==== 糴(S籴) [dí] buy rice
糴[籴] ⁸dí v. buy in (grain/rice)
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 籴:) From 入 (rù) 'enter' and 米 (mǐ) 'rice'. The full form 糴 also contains 翟 dí phonetic.
"Lay in grain, buy rice -- the signific part is: to 入 lay in 米 rice" --Karlgren.
Compare 粜(糶) tiào 'sell grain'.
