1226 徒 [tú] on foot; follower; 徒弟 apprentice; 匪徒 gangster
徒 ⁴tú {E} b.f. ①disciple; pupil; apprentice 徒弟 túdi ②follower 信徒 xìntú* ③〈derog.〉 person; fellow 酒徒 jiǔtú ④(prison) sentence; imprisonment 徒刑 ²túxíng ⑤go on foot 徒步 túbù* ⑥bare; empty 徒手 ¹túshǒu 徒有虚名 túyǒuxūmíng ◆adv. ①merely; only ②in vain; to no purpose
Originally from 彳 (chì) 'footstep', 土 tǔ phonetic, and (止 zhǐ) 'foot'. 土 and  combined happen to look like 走 (zǒu) 'walk'.
