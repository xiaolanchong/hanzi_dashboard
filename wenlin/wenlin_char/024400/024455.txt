---- 徇 [xùn] (書) give in to, submit to; 徇私情 practice favoritism
徇 ⁸xùn {E} b.f. ①practice favoritism 徇私 xùnsī* ②die for a cause 徇义 xùnyì
From 彳 'conduct', 旬 xún, 勹 and 日.
From 彳 footstep and 旬 xún phonetic.
"Go everywhere, perambulate; pervading; follow" --Karlgren.
Acording to Karlgren, 徇 xùn is etymologically the same word as 循 xún and 巡 xún, but note the different tones in modern Mandarin.
