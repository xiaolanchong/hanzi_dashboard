961  彩 [cǎi] (色彩 sècǎi) color; 彩色 cǎisè multicolor
彩 ³cǎi {E} b.f. ①color; colors in combination 五彩 wụ̌cǎi* ②brilliance; splendor 精彩 jīngcǎi ③prize 头彩 tóucǎi ④blood from a wound 挂彩 guàcǎi
From 采 cǎi 'pick' and 彡 (shān) 'feathers'.
"Etymologically the same word as 采; character specifyingly enlarged by 彡" --Karlgren.
Compare 形 xíng 'shape' and 影 yǐng 'shadow'.
