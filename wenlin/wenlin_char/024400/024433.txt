358  影 [yǐng] shadow; image; 电影 movie
影 ¹yǐng {E} b.f. ①shadow; reflection; image 影子 yǐngzi* ②trace; vague impression 影子 yǐngzi* ③photograph; picture 合影 héyǐng* ④film; movie 电影 diànyǐng(r) ⑤copy; trace | ∼ Sòngběn ∼宋本 a facsimile edition of a Song book ◆v. 〈topo.〉 hide; conceal
From 景 jǐng ('scenery') phonetic and 彡 (shān).
彡 sometimes means 'hair' or 'feather'. In 影 it seems to represent a shadow, compare 形 xíng 'shape' and 彩 cǎi 'color'.
