2605 彬 [bīn] 彬彬 bīnbīn (书) refined, urbane  [bān]
彬/斌/邠 ⁴bīn {E} b.f. of literary talent and simple style 彬彬 ¹bīnbīn 文质彬彬 wénzhìbīnbīn 邠如 bīnrú
彬/邠/豳 Bīn b.f. name of a xiàn in Shaanxi; ancient home of the founder of the Zhou dynasty
From 林 (lín) 'grove' and 彡 (shān) 'form' (as in 形 xíng 'shape', 影 yǐng 'shadow').
"Of the same size and quality, symmetrical; elegant -- similar in 彡 form, like the 林 trees in a grove" --Karlgren.
彬 bīn appears to have no direct relation to 杉 (shān 'fir').
