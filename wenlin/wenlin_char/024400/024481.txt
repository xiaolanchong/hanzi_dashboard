2069 御 [yù] imperial; (F禦) 防御 fángyù defense; 抵御 dǐyù resist  [yà]
驭/御[馭/-] ¹²yù {E} b.f. 〈wr.〉 drive (a carriage/etc.) 驾御 jiàyù 御夫 yùfū 驭手 ¹yùshǒu
御 ¹³yù {E} b.f. ①〈wr.〉 control; manage 御事 ⁸yùshì ②imperial 御花园 yùhuāyuán 御赐 ¹yùcì*
御[禦] ¹⁴yù {E} b.f. resist; ward off 防御 fángyù* 抵御 dǐyù
From 彳 (chì) 'footstep', and 卸 (xiè) 'unload'.
"Drive a carriage; to direct, govern, to reign; the sovereign, imperial; serve the emperor, wait on, attendant -- to make the horse 彳 go to 卸 the unloading place" --Karlgren.
The character 驭(馭) yù also means 'drive a carriage'.
御 now mostly occurs as the simple form of 禦 yù 'defend'.
