1014 彙(S汇) [huì] gather together; 彙報 to report; 詞彙 vocabulary
彙[汇] ³huì {E} b.f. ①gather together; assemble 汇编 huìbiān* ②collection; list 词汇 ¹cíhuì
彙 depicts a hedgehog. Borrowed as a phonetic loan character for the word huì 'collection'.
Both 彙 huì and 匯 huì mean 'gather together', and are simplified to 汇, but they are sometimes distinguished among full-form characters. See 汇 for further description.
