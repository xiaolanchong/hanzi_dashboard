---- 弁 [biàn] ancient man's cap  [pán]
弁 ⁹biàn n. 〈trad.〉 ①man's cap ②low-ranking military officer
厶 represents a cap, and 廾 is  two hands.
"Conical cap, military cap, casque; excitement of battle, hurry, quick march -- picture of two hands and a cap" --Karlgren(1923).
卞 biàn 'rash, impetuous' was originally the same word and the same character as 弁.
