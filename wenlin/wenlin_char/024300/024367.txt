1629 弯(F彎) [wān] curve, bend
弯[彎] ¹wān {B} s.v. bent; curved; crooked | Chē bǎ lángān zhuàng ∼ le. 车把栏杆撞∼了。 The vehicle bumped into the railing and bent it. ◆v. ①bend; flex ②draw (a bow) ◆n. turn; curve
From (䜌 luán) 'contraction' over 弓 (gōng) 'bow'.
