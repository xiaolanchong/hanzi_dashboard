1629 彎(S弯) [wān] curve
彎[弯] ¹wān {B} s.v. bent; curved; crooked | Chē bǎ lángān zhuàng ∼ le. 车把栏杆撞∼了。 The vehicle bumped into the railing and bent it. ◆v. ①bend; flex ②draw (a bow) ◆n. turn; curve
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 弯:) From (䜌 luán) 'contraction' over 弓 (gōng) 'bow'.
"To 䜌 fasten (the string) on to 弓 the bow (which then is bent)" --Karlgren.
