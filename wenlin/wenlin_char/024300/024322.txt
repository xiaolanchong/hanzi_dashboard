857  异(F異) [yì] different; 异常 yìcháng unusual  [yí]
异[異] ⁸yì {D} b.f. ①different 异同 yì-tóng 差异 chāyì* ②strange; unusual 奇异 ¹qíyì* ③other; another 异味 ²yìwèi(r) ④foreign (place) 异国 yìguó* 异乡 ¹yìxiāng ⑤disloyal 异心 yìxīn ⑥separate 离异 líyì ⑦feel surprised 诧异 chàyì ⑧treat specially
The full form 異 is 田 over 共. It depicted a person wearing a scary mask (the head is like that of 鬼 guǐ 'demon'), waving hands in the air. Strange; different.
The simple form 异 is 巳(已) yǐ phonetic over 廾 'two hands'. It is over two thousand years old (说文).
異 is a component in 戴翼冀瀵 (in these characters 異 is not simplified to 异).
