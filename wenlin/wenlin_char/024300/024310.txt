1105 延 [yán] (延长 yáncháng) prolong
延 ⁹yán {E} b.f. ①prolong; extend; protract 延长 yáncháng* ②postpone; delay 延期 yánqī* ③engage; send for; invite 延聘 yánpìn
From 丿 (a stroke falling to the left), 止 (zhǐ) 'foot' (with the last two strokes combined into one: 𠃊), and 廴 (yǐn) 'journey'.
"廴 walking 止 foot; meaning of 丿 above foot uncertain" --Karlgren.
