---- 闌(S阑) [lán] late; railing, balustrade  [làn]
闌[阑] ⁸lán {E} b.f. railing; balustrade; fence 阑入 lánrù 夜阑 yèlán 夜阑珊 yè lánshān
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 阑:) From 门(門 mén) 'door' and 柬 jiǎn phonetic.
"Door screen, railings; to bar, shut off; late, finished; reckless" --Karlgren.
