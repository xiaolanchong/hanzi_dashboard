2111 闖(S闯) [chuǎng] rush, charge  [chèn]
闖[闯] chuǎng {B} v. ①rush; dash; charge ②temper oneself (by battling difficulties) | Nǐ de qiántú kào zìjǐ qù ∼. 你的前途靠自己去∼。 Your future depends on your own initiative.
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 闯:) From 门(門 mén) 'gate' and 马(馬 mǎ) 'horse'.
A 马 horse rushing through a 门 gate.
