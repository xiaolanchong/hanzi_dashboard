==== 闔(S阖) [hé] (書) entire; shut
闔[阖] ¹⁴hé attr./b.f. entire; whole 合家 héjiā ◆v. shut; close
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 阖:) From 门(門 mén) 'door' and 盍 hé 'cover'.
"Door-leaf, shut, cover; the whole, all -- etymologically the same word as 盍 in its original sense; character enlarged by 門" --Karlgren.
