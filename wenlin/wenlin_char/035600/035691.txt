==== 譫(S谵) [zhān] rant, rave, talk wildly
譫[谵] ⁶zhān b.f. delirious 谵语 zhānyǔ* 谵妄 ²zhānwàng
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 谵:) From 讠(言 yán) 'words' and 詹 zhān.
"Talkative" --Karlgren(1923).
Etymologically the same word as 詹 zhān, the character being redundantly enlarged by another 言.
