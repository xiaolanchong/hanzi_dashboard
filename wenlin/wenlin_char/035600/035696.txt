319  議(S议) [yì] (議論 yìlùn) discuss; 會議 conference; 建議 suggest
議[议] ¹⁰yì {E} v. confer; discuss | Zhège wèntí wǒmen ²zài ∼ yị̄ ∼. 这个问题我们再∼一∼。 We will discuss this problem again. ◆b.f. ①criticize; suggest 建议 jiànyì* ②opinion; view 异议 ²yìyì ③treatise; argumentation 议论 yìlùn*
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 议:) From 讠(言 yán) 'words' and 义(義) yì 'justice'.
议 is cognate with 义.
"(Discuss what is right:) deliberate upon, criticise" --Karlgren.
