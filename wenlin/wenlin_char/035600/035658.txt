xxxx 譊(S𫍢) [náo] 譊譊 náonáo hubbub, quarrel  [xiāo]
譊[𫍢] ⁵náo b.f. raise a hubbub; quarrel 𫍢𫍢 ²náonáo
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 𫍢:) From 讠(言 yán) 'words' and 尧(堯) yáo phonetic.
"恚呼也" --說文解字.
The simplified form is in 辞海 and in Unicode 5.2.
Compare 呶呶 and 怓怓 'babble'. 譊譊 is used specifically for sounds of quarreling.
