---- 證 [zhèng] (S证) prove; 圖書證 library card; (S症) disease
證/証[证] ⁶zhèng {D} n. license; certificate ◆b.f. ①prove; testify; demonstrate 证明 ¹zhèngmíng* ②evidence; proof; testimony 证据 ¹zhèngjù* ③certificate; card; credentials 证书 ¹zhèngshū* 工作证 gōngzuòzhèng ④disease; illness 证候 zhènghòu
症[-/證] ⁵zhèng {D} b.f. ①disease; illness 病症 bìngzhèng* ②symptoms 症状 zhèngzhuàng*
From 言 yán 'words' and 登 dēng phonetic.
The simple form is 证, except when the meaning is "disease", in which case the simple form is 症 (which is also used to some extent among full form characters).
