xxxx 謰(S) [lián] 謰謱 talk on and on; 謰語 compound word
謰[] ¹⁸lián char. 语 ²liányǔ
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form :) From 讠(言 yán) 'words' and 连(連) lián 'on and on'.
The simplified form  is not in Unicode 6.0. It appears in the ABC Chinese-English Comprehensive Dictionary (DeFrancis 2003, print edition), and in 《规范字典》 (1998:731).
謰謱 is also written 嗹嘍. 謰語 is also written 连语.
