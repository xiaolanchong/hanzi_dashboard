==== 誒(S诶) [éi] [ěi] [èi] (=欸)  [āi] (=唉)  [xī] [yì]
唉/欸/誒[-/-/诶] ³āi {C} intj. (mild exclamation/sigh) Gosh!; My!
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 诶:) From 讠(言 yán) 'words' and 矣 yǐ phonetic.
Most dictionaries use 欸 as the standard form.
(诶 is Guojia Biaozhun number DAC0, there is no GB2312 code for 欸.)
