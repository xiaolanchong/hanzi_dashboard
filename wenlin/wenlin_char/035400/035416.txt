==== 詘(S诎) [qū] to contract; wrinkle; stutter, stammer  [chù]
詘[诎] ¹⁶qū b.f. shorten ◆char. 充诎 chōngqū
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 诎:) From 讠(言 yán) 'words' and 出 chū phonetic.
"Bend, stoop; subdue, oppress; to stutter" --Karlgren.
诎 is etymologically the same word as 屈 qū 'bend'.
