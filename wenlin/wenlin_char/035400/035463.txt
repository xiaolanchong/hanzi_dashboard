1755 誇(S夸) [kuā] brag; 誇大 kuādà exaggerate  [qù]
誇[夸] ¹kuā {C} v. ①exaggerate; overstate; boast ②praise ◆s.v. ①big ②lavish
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 夸:) The top is 大 (dà) 'big'. The bottom 亏 (kuī) is a variant of 于 yú, meaning an exclamation:
"A 大 great 亏 exclamation" --Karlgren.
The full form 誇 has 言 (yán) 'words' added.
The similarity in sound between 夸 kuā and 亏(虧) kuī is coincidental.
