1821 幽 [yōu] 幽静 secluded; 幽默 humor; 幽暗 gloomy; 幽灵 ghost
幽 ⁵yōu {E} b.f. ①remote; secluded 幽静 ¹yōujìng 清幽 qīngyōu ②secretive 幽会 ²yōuhuì ◆char. 幽默 yōumò*
From 山 (shān) 'mountain' and 幺 yāo phonetic (doubled). A quiet, dark retreat in the 山 mountains.
The word 幽默 yōumò is a phonetic loan from English 'humor'.
