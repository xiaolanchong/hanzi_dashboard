---- 幂(F冪) [mì] cloth cover; (math:) power; 幂级数
幂[冪] ⁶mì n. ①〈wr.〉 cloth cover ②〈math.〉 power ◆v. 〈wr.〉 cover with cloth
From 冖 mì 'cover' and 幕 (mù) 'curtain'.
幂 mì is the same word as 冖 mì. Since 冖 was too simple to be a character all by itself, 幕 was added to make 冪.
The top three strokes of 幕 are omitted in the simple form 幂.
