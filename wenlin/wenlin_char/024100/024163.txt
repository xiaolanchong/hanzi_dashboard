1219 幣(S币) [bì] money, currency; 硬幣 coin; 人民幣 rénmínbì RMB.
幣[币] ¹bì {D} b.f. money; currency 硬币 ¹yìngbì*
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 币:) From 敝 bì phonetic over 巾 (jīn) 'cloth'. In the simple form 币, 敝 is replaced by 丿 a single stroke.
巾 represents a "piece of silk; a present, tribute, recompense" --Karlgren.
