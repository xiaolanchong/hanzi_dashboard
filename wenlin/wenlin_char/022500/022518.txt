==== 埶 [yì] (old form of 艺藝 yì; component in 熱勢褻)  [shì]
No single-syllable words currently in dictionary.	add new s.s.w.
From 坴 (lù) 'heap of earth', and 丸 (not wǎn but:)  'sieze'.
"Agriculture, cultivate -- from 坴 heap of earth and  to handle" --Karlgren(1923).
Not to be confused with 执(執) zhí although historically it often has been.
