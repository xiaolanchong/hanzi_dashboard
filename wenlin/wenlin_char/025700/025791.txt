2423 撿(S捡) [jiǎn] pick up, gather, collect  [liàn]
撿[捡] ⁵jiǎn {B} v. ①pick up ②collect; gather
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 捡:) From 扌(手 shǒu) 'hand' and 佥(僉) qiān phonetic.
Compare 拣(揀) jiǎn 'choose; gather', which is unrelated but has some overlap in meaning, and happens to have the same pronunciation in modern Mandarin.
