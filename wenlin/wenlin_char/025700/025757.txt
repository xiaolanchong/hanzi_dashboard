xxxx 撝(S㧑) [huī] direct; manage; 撝挹 modest; 撝損 humble  [wéi]
撝[㧑] ⁹huī b.f. direct; manage 㧑挹 huīyì 㧑损 huīsǔn
According to the glyph printed in the Unicode Standard, Version 2.0 (and 3.0), and also according to the stroke count, the right side is 爲, not 為. In some fonts this character is (wrongly?) displayed with 為.
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 㧑:) From 扌(手 shǒu) 'hand' and 为(爲) wéi.
