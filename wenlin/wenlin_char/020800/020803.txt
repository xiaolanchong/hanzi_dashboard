271  元 [yuán] primary; dollar
元 ³yuán {E} b.f. ①first; primary 元旦 Yuándàn* ②basic; fundamental 元素 ¹yuánsù* 元音 ²yuányīn ③chief; principal 元首 ¹yuánshǒu ④old; eminent 元老 yuánlǎo ⑤vital state; vitality 元气 ¹yuánqì* ⑥〈phil.〉 origin ◆n. ①Yuan dynasty ②Surname
元/圆[-/圓] ²yuán {A} m. ①Chinese monetary unit ②dollar
The top 二 is an old form of 上 (shàng) 'superior', the bottom is 儿 (人 rén) 'person'.
"Probably etymologically the same word as 原" --Karlgren.
Compare 原 yuán 'originally' and 源 yuán 'source'.
The meaning 'dollar' may derive from 圆 yuán 'round' (coin).
