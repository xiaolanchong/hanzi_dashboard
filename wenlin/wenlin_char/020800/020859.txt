养[養] ¹yǎng {B} v. ①support; provide for | Quánjiā rén kào tā yị̄ ge rén ∼. 全家人靠他一个人∼。 The whole family relies on him alone. ②raise; maintain | Wọ̌ ∼le yī̠ tiáo xiạ̌o gǒu. 我∼了一条小狗。 I have a little dog. ③give birth to ④form; cultivate ◆b.f. ①rest; recuperate 养病 yǎngbìng* ②foster; adoptive 养子 yạ̌ngzǐ 养女 yạ̌ngnǚ
From 𦍌(羊) yáng phonetic and 食 (shí) 'food'.
In the simple form 养 the bottom is like 介 (jiè). 养 is only nine strokes: the 丨 of 𦍌 turns into the 丿 on top of 介.
