184  入 [rù] (进入 jìnrù) enter; 收入 shōurù income
入 ¹rù {B} b.f. ①enter 进入 ¹jìnrù ②receive; take in; income 收入 shōurù* ③conform with 入味 rùwèi(r) ④join 入党 rùdǎng* ⑤descend; set (of sun) ⑥〈lg.〉 “entering” tone 入声 rùshēng
Possibly roots entering the ground; or an arrow-head; or an upside-down V shaped entrance to a tent or hut. 入 is part of 内 nèi 'inside'.
Eleventh of the 214 radicals.
入 rù 'enter' and 人 rén 'person' are almost mirror images in their modern forms.
