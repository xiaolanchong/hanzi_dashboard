113  兩(S两) [liǎng] two; 50 grams  [liàng]
兩[两] ¹liǎng* {A} num. ①two; 2 ②both (sides); either (side) ③a few; some ◆m. ①ounce ②tael
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 两:) "Picture of a balance, two arms with a weight on each" --Karlgren.
Interestingly, the picture fits both meanings ('two' and 'fifty grams'), even though the meanings seem totally unrelated.
