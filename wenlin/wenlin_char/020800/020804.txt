1245 兄 [xiōng] (elder) brother; 兄弟 xiōngdì brothers  [kuàng]
兄 ³xiōng {D} b.f. ①elder brother 兄弟 xiōngdi ②courteous address between men 老兄 lǎoxiōng ③elder male relative of the same generation 表兄 biǎoxiōng
From 口 (kǒu) 'mouth' and 儿(人 rén) 'person'.
"The 儿 person with 口 speaking authority (towards the younger)" --Karlgren.
The more common word for older brother is 哥哥 gēge, while 弟弟 dìdi means 'younger brother'.
