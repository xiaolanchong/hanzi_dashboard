180  关(F關) [guān] shut; (关系 guānxi) connection; 关于 concerning
关[關] ¹guān {A} v. ①shut; close ②turn off ③lock up ④close down ⑤issue/receive (payment) ⑥concern; implicate ◆n. ①pass ②barrier ③crux; critical juncture; turning/key point ④key part
The full form 關 has 門 (mén) 'door' to indicate the meaning and numerous strokes inside (threads being woven -- once a phonetic component).
关 appears as a part of 联 lián and 送 sòng, although none of the characters containing 关 has any historical relationship with 关(關) guān.
Compare 关系 guānxi with 联系 liánxì.
