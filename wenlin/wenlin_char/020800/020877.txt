211  再 [zài] again; 再见 zàijiàn goodbye
再 ²zài {A} adv. ①again; once more; further(more) | ∼ chī yī̠diǎnr. ∼吃一点儿。 Eat some more. ②in a higher degree ③and then; not (do sth.) before | Chīwán fàn ∼ kàn diànshì. 吃完饭∼看电视。 Finish your food before you watch TV. ◆v. 〈wr.〉 come back; return | Qīngchūn bụ̀ ∼. 青春不∼。 One's youth does not return.
Supposed to represent a 巾 balance or scales, hanging from a 一 beam, with the number 二 two (2) superimposed (the lower two horizontal strokes).
"A 二 second 巾 weighing, equal to the first one" --Wieger.
Compare 两 liǎng. No relation to 冉 rǎn.
