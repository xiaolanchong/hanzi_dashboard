866  鬆(S松) [sōng] loose; free; careless  [sòng] [sóng]
鬆[松] ¹sōng {B} s.v. ①loose; slack; lax | Zhè ²gēn shéngzi tài ∼ le. 这根绳子太∼了。 The rope is too slack. ②not hard up ③light and flaky; soft ◆b.f. dried minced meat/fish/etc. 肉松 ròusōng ◆v. ①loosen; slacken ②let go; untie
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 松:) From 木 (mù) 'tree' and 公 gōng phonetic; Karlgren speculates an Archaic *ks- or *gz-.
The second meaning 'dishevelled; slack, lax, loose; free, careless', in the full form is written 鬆, with 髟 (biāo) 'long hair' signific on top of 松 sōng phonetic. The simple form for both meanings is 松.
