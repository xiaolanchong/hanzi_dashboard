xxxx 揾(F搵) [wèn] wipe; 揾泪 wipe tears; 揾钱 make money  [wù]
揾[搵] ³wèn b.f. ①press with the finger ②wipe 揾泪 wènlèi ◆char. 揾钱 wènqián
From 扌(手 shǒu) 'hand' and 昷(𥁕) wēn phonetic.
揾 also has the meaning "press down", which appears to be cognate with 摁 èn; 揾 may even be pronounced èn with this meaning (acc. to Mathews).
