1276 援 [yuán] hold; help; 支援 support; 援助 help  [huàn]
援 ⁹yuán {E} b.f. ①assist; support 支援 zhīyuán* ②rescue; help 援助 ¹yuánzhù* ③cite; quote 援用 yuányòng
From 扌(手 shǒu) 'hand' and 爰 yuán 'hence'.
According to Karlgren, 援 yuán is etymologically the same word as 爰 yuán, which originally meant 'drag'.
Compare 暖 nuǎn 'warm' and 缓 huǎn 'slow'.
