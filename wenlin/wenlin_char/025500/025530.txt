---- 掺(F摻) [chān] mix (put sth. into sth. else)  [shǎn] (书) grasp  [càn]
掺/搀[摻/攙] ¹chān {D} v. mix | Niúnǎi li ∼le hěn duō shuǐ. 牛奶里∼了很多水。 The milk is mixed with a lot of water.
From 扌(手 shǒu) 'hand' and 参 cān phonetic.
In standard modern Chinese, the character 搀 chān is used instead of 掺.
The word 掺 shǎn "grasp" is bookish and rare.
The word 掺 càn described an ancient drum beat.
