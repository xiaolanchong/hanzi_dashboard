662  宣 [xuān] (宣布) declare; 宣传 propaganda
宣 ¹xuān {E} b.f. ①declare; proclaim; announce 宣布 xuānbù ②drain off (liquids) 宣泄 xuānxiè ◆n. Surname
From 宀 (mián) 'roof' and 亘, which was once 回 (now 日) between 二.
"Revolve, go everywhere, in all directions -- the seal has 二 'above and below' and 回 a turn, a round: in all directions" --Karlgren.
The similarity to 亘 gèn 'extend' is coincidental.
