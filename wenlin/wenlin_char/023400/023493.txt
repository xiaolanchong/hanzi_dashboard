2826 寅 [yín] the third 地支 Earthly Branch
寅 ⁶yín n. 3rd of the 12 Earthly Branches
"Behaviour, gait, ritual politeness; 居敬也。In a 宀 house, a man [一 over 人] (a special primitive, stiff bearing, on the top, a cap) pays salutations with both hands. The modern character is a contraction" --Wieger.
The oldest forms appear to depict two hands holding an arrow; according to Henshall the meaning was "straighten an arrow"; it was borrowed for the third Earthly Branch.
Phonetic in 演 yǎn.
