宋 Sòng {E} n. ①Song dynasty (960-1279) ②Liu-Song dynasty (420-479) ③Surname
From 宀 (mián) 'roof' over 木 (mù) 'wood'.
"House, dwell; noun proper of state and dynasty -- 木 wooden 宀 house" --Karlgren.
There was also a 宋 Sòng dynasty in the years (420-479).
