1856 宴 [yàn] (宴会 yànhuì) banquet
宴/晏/燕/醼/䜩[-/-/-/-/讌] ⁶yàn {E} b.f. ①banquet; dinner party 宴会 ¹yànhuì* ②give a dinner 宴客 yànkè ③pleasure; joy 宴乐 yànlè ④leisure 宴安 yàn'ān
From 宀 (mián) 'roof' and an old character 妟 yàn:
"Rest, peace -- from 日 and 女 (daily siesta in the harem?)" --Karlgren.
宴 yàn is cognate with 妟 yàn and 安 ān 'peaceful'.
"Quiet, rest; amusement; banquet, feast" --Karlgren.
