---- 箕 [jī] [ji] (簸箕 bòji) dustpan; winnowing basket
箕 ²⁰jī {E} b.f. dustpan; winnowing basket 簸箕 bòji 箕赋 ²jīfù 粪箕子 fènjīzi
From 竹 (zhú) 'bamboo' and 其 qí 'basket'.
Originally written 其, which was a picture of a winnowing basket. Then 其 was borrowed for various similarly pronounced abstract words, now qí. The original word jī 'winnowing basket' came to be written with 竹 added.
