==== 篋(S箧) [qiè] (書) small suitcase
篋[箧] ⁹qiè b.f. small chest/box/suitcase 箧笥 qièsì 鼓箧 gǔqiè
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 箧:) Originally 匧, from 匚 (picture of a box) and 夾 jiā 'squeeze'. 竹 (zhú) 'bamboo' was then added. Cognate to 夹(夾) jiā and 狭 xiá 'narrow', etc.
"Long and narrow box; trunk; portfolio" --Karlgren.
