---- 箴 [zhēn] (variant of 針针) needle; admonish, warn  [jiǎn]
箴 ¹²zhēn b.f. admonish 箴规 zhēnguī 箴言 ¹zhēnyán* 规箴 ²guīzhēn
From 竹 (zhú) 'bamboo' and 咸 (xián) 'bite'.
"Needle, probe; criticise, admonish -- the 咸 'biter', piercer made of 竹 bamboo" --Karlgren.
"A probe; a needle. To pierce. To warn, to exhort. Style of writing" --Mathews.
Etymologically the same word as 针 zhēn 'needle'.
