2325 簾(S帘) [lián] curtain
簾[帘] ⁵lián {D} b.f. curtain 窗帘 chuānglián(r/zi)*
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 帘:) There are two characters, 帘 and 簾. Among simple form characters only 帘 is used. 帘 is composed of 穴 (xué) 'hole' (window) and 巾 (jīn) 'cloth'.
The full form character 簾 is from 竹 (zhú) 'bamboo' and 廉 lián phonetic.
