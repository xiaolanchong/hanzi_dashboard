424  类(F類) [lèi] category, kind, type; 类似 lèisì similar
类[類] ¹lèi* {B} n./m. ①kind; type; class; category; genus | Nà shì nǎ yị̄ ∼ dòngwù? 那是哪一∼动物? What species of animal is that? ②〈lg.〉 form class ◆b.f. resemble; be similar to 类似 lèisì* ◆adv. generally
The full form 類 contains 米 (mǐ) 'rice', 犬 (quǎn) 'dog', and 頁 (yě) 'head'.
"Categories: 米 rice, examplifying the vegetable kingdom, 犬 dog, the animal kingdom, and 頁 head, the human world" --Karlgren.
The simple form 类 is 米 'rice' over 大 (dà) 'big'.
