379  研 [yán] grind; (研究 yánjiū) study  [yàn] [xíng]
研 ⁸yán {E} v. grind ◆b.f. study; research 研究 yánjiū*
From 石 (shí) 'stone' and 开(幵) qiān phonetic. The right side is now written like 开 kāi 'open', but it was a originally a character 幵 qiān 'level', composed of 干 (gān) doubled. 幵 is obsolete. In 研 yán the horizontal strokes were joined to look like 开 kāi. The lower part of 并 bìng has the same origin.
