==== 諭(S谕) [yù] (書) tell; 上諭 shàngyù imperial edict  [tǒu]
諭[谕] ²⁶yù b.f. inform (an inferior); order 谕告 ²yùgào 密谕 ¹mìyù
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 谕:) From 讠(言 yán) 'words' and 俞 yù phonetic.
"Instruct, inform; make known, publish, proclaim, issue orders" --Karlgren(1923).
Etymologically the same word as 喻, but not related to 语.
