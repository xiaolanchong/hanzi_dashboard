---- 諄(S谆) [zhūn] 諄諄 zhūnzhūn earnestly and tirelessly
諄[谆] ¹zhūn {E} b.f. ①untiring; patient; earnest; sincere 谆谆 ¹zhūnzhūn 谆切 zhūnqiè ②reiterate; (say) repeatedly 谆嘱 zhūnzhǔ
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 谆:) From 讠(言 yán) 'words' and 享(𦎧) chún phonetic.
The phonetic component 𦎧 chún now looks like 享 (xiǎng 'feast').
"Inculcate, repeat; patiently" --Karlgren(1923).
Compare 肫 zhūn which has similar meaning.
