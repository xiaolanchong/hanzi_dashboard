2621 謎(S谜) [mí] (謎語 míyǔ) riddle  [mèi] 謎兒 riddle
謎[谜] ²mí {E} n. ①riddle; conundrum ②enigma; mystery | Zhège rén shì ge ∼. 这个人是个∼。 This man is a mystery.
謎[谜] ⁷mèi char. 谜儿 mèir 破谜儿 pòmèir 猜谜 cāimèi(r)
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 谜:) From 讠(言 yán) 'words' and 迷 mí 'lost'.
Etymologically the same word as 迷 mí.
