1404 災(S灾) [zāi] (災難 zāinàn, 災害 zāihài) disaster
災[灾] ¹zāi {B} b.f. ①disaster; calamity 灾害 zāihài 水灾 shuǐzāi ②misfortune
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 灾:) The bottom is 火 (huǒ) 'fire'.
The top is 宀 (mián) 'roof', meaning a house on fire; or 巛 (川 chuān) 'river', meaning floods and fires.
