1284 灰 [huī] dust; grey
灰 ¹huī {B} n. ①ash ②dust | Zhuōzi shang yǒu yī̠ céng ∼. 桌子上有一层∼。 There's a layer of dust on the desk. ③lime ◆s.v. ①gray ②disheartened | Tā biàn de hěn ∼. 他变得很∼。 He's become very pessimistic.
From 火 huǒ 'fire' and 𠂇, which derives from 又 yòu 'hand'.
"Ashes; dust; lime -- explained as 火 fire which you can 又 take with the hand; yet 又 ... might be phonetic ..." --Karlgren(1923).
Compare 炭 tàn 'charcoal'.
