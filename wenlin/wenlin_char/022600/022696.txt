1680 墨 [mò] (墨水 mòshuǐ) ink; 墨子 Mòzǐ (a philosopher)  [mèi]
墨 ²mò {C} n. ①ink stick; ink cake ②Surname ◆b.f. ①ink 墨水 mòshuǐ(r) ②handwriting 遗墨 yímò ③black; dark 墨镜 mòjìng* ④Mohism 墨家 Mòjiā ⑤corrupt; venal ◆ab. Mòxīgē ◆v. 〈hist.〉 tattoo the face (as punishment)
From 黑 hēi 'black' over 土 (tǔ) 'earth'.
According to Karlgren, 墨 mò 'black; ink' and 默 mò 'dark; silent; secret' are both cognate with 黑 hēi 'black' ("formed with a deriv. suffix m-").
