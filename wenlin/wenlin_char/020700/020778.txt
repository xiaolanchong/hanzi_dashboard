766  優(S优) [yōu] excellent; 優良 fine; 優秀 outstanding; 優越 superior
優[优] ¹yōu {D} b.f. ①excellent; superior 优良 yōuliáng* ②ample; abundant 优裕 ²yōuyù ③free; leisurely 优游 yōuyóu ④give preferential treatment 优待 yōudài* ⑤〈trad.〉 actor; actress 优伶 ²yōulíng
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 优:) From 亻(人 rén) 'person' and 尤 yóu phonetic. In the full form 優, the phonetic is 憂 yōu (which is the full form of 忧 yōu 'worry').
