==== 儐(S傧) [bīn] 女儐相 nǚbīnxiàng bridesmaid; 男儐相 best man  [bìn]
儐[傧] ⁵bīn {E} b.f. bridesmaid or best man at wedding 傧相 bīnxiàng* 男傧相 nánbīnxiàng 女傧 nǚbīn
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 傧:) From 亻(人 rén) 'person' and 宾(賓) bīn 'guest'.
Presumably the same word as 宾, enlarged by 亻 person for the special meaning: an attendant of the bride or groom at a wedding.
