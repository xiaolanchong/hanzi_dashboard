---- 僮 [tóng] (=童) boy servant  [Zhuàng] (=壯)  [chòng]
童/僮 ³tóng {E} b.f. ①child 儿童 értóng* 童年 ²tóngnián ②boy servant 童儿 tóngr 童仆 tóngpú
壮[壯/僮] Zhuàng {E} b.f. (a member of) the Zhuang ethnic minority 壮族 Zhuàngzú
From 亻(人 rén) 'person' and 童 tóng.
Same morpheme as 童, enlarged by 亻 especially for meaning 'boy servant'.
