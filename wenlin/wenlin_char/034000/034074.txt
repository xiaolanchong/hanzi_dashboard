2948 蔚 [wèi] 蔚蓝 wèilán azure, sky-blue; 茺蔚 chōngwèi motherwort  [Yù]
蔚 ¹¹wèi {E} b.f. ①luxuriant 蔚蓝 wèilán* ②having literary talent 彬蔚 ²bīnwèi
From 艹(草 cǎo) 'grass' and 尉 Wèi phonetic.
"Artemisia; beautiful, elegant . . . N. pr of a place" --Karlgren.
There is a county in Hebei called 蔚县 Yù Xiàn. In this name, 蔚 is pronounced Yù instead of wèi.
