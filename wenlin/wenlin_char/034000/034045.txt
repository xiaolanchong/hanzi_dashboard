==== 蓽(S荜) [bì] 蓽撥 long pepper; (=篳)
蓽[荜] ³⁶bì b.f. (Indian) long pepper (Piper longum) 荜拨 ²bìbō 荜茇 bìbá
筚/荜[篳/蓽] ³⁵bì b.f. ①bamboo/wicker material 筚路 ⁴bìlù ②wicker door (implying a humble/poor household) 蓬筚 péngbì 蓬门筚户 péngménbìhù
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 荜:) From 艹(草 cǎo) 'grass' and 毕(畢) bì phonetic.
