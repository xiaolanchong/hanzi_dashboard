2348 蔑 [miè] 轻蔑 qīngmiè scornful
蔑 ²miè {E} b.f. disdain; scorn 蔑视 mièshì* 诬蔑 wūmiè*
From 苜 miè ('squint') phonetic over 戍 (shù) 'defend'.
The top, 艹 over 罒, is not 苜 mù 'alfalfa', but 苜 miè 'squint', same as the top of 夢 mèng 'dream' and 瞢 méng 'dim sight'.
"Be without; not exist; nothing, not; insignificant, trifling" --Karlgren.
A cognate word is 篾 miè 'thin bamboo strip'.
