==== 蹺(S跷) [qiāo] lift a leg or finger; on tiptoe; stilts  [qiào]
蹺/蹻[跷/𫏋] ⁷qiāo {E} v. ①raise the feet/fingers ②walk on tiptoe ◆b.f. stilts 高跷 gāoqiāo
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 跷:) From ⻊(足 zú) 'foot' and 尧(堯) xiǎo phonetic.
"To raise the feet, as when sitting; to cross the legs" --Mathews.
A variant form is 蹻.
