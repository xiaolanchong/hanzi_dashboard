(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 娄:) "The seal has: 毋中女: 'a woman in seclusion', says the commentator" --Karlgren.
"Woman 女 confined, enclosed 中 in the 毋 prison of the gynecium; for ever idle; useless, etc; 毋中女, 空虚之意。" --Wieger.
