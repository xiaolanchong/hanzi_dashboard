2933 貯(S贮) [zhù] (貯存) store, save
貯[贮] ¹²zhù {E} b.f. store; save; lay aside 贮存 zhùcún*
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 贮:) The signific part is 贝(貝 bèi) 'money'.
In the full form 貯, the right side is 宁, which is 宀 over 丁. 宁 was a phonetic component, same as in 佇(伫) zhù, unrelated to 寧 níng.
In the simple form 贮, the right side is 宀 over 一.
Compare 苎(苧) zhù.
