832  貴(S贵) [guì] expensive; valuable
貴[贵] ²guì {A} s.v. expensive; costly | Jīpiào tài ∼ le. 机票太∼了。 The plane ticket is too expensive. ◆b.f. ①highly valued 宝贵 bǎoguì* ②noble; of high rank 贵族 guìzú ③〈court.〉 your 贵姓 ¹guìxìng* ◆pref. 〈court.〉 esteemed 贵国 guìguó ◆n. short for Guizhou
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 贵:) Originally two hands over 土 (tǔ) 'earth', soil being the most valuable thing in an agricultural society. Later 贝(貝 bèi) 'money' was added to the bottom.
