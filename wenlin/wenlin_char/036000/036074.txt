647  質(S质) [zhì] (物質 wùzhì) matter, substance; 質量 zhìliàng quality; mass
質[质] ⁵zhì {C} n. quality ◆b.f. ①nature; character; temperament 性质 ¹xìngzhì* ②matter; substance 物质 ¹wùzhì* ③question 质问 zhìwèn ④pawn; mortgage; pledge ⑤hostage 人质 ¹rénzhì* ⑥simple; plain 质朴 ¹zhìpǔ* ⑦sincere; frank
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 质:) The full form 質 has 斦 (斤 jīn 'axe' doubled) on top of 貝 (bèi) 'money': a kilogram of money.
The simple form 质, like the variant 貭, is altered to resemble 盾 dùn 'shield'.
