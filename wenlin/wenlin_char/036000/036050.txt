---- 賒(S赊) [shē] buy or sell on credit  [shā]
賒[赊] ¹shē {E} v. buy/sell on credit | Méi ²qián xiān ∼ zhe. 没钱先∼着。 You can have it on credit if you don't have money right now.
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 赊:) From 贝(貝 bèi) 'money' and 佘 shé phonetic.
A variant form 賖 has 余 yú instead of 佘 shé.
