2430 賈(S贾) [gǔ] merchant  [Jiǎ] (surname)  [jià]
賈[贾] ⁷gǔ {E} 〈wr.〉 n. merchant ◆b.f. ①engage in trade 贾人 ²gǔrén ②sell ③buy 贾买 gǔmǎi*
贾[賈] Jiǎ n. Surname
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 贾:) From 覀(襾) xià phonetic over 贝(貝 bèi) 'money'.
Cognate with 价(價) jià 'price'. The sounds gǔ, jiǎ and jià are all related (ancient *ga).
