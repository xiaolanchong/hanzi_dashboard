---- 貳(S贰) [èr] two (used for 二 èr on checks, financial records)
貳[贰/弍] ²èr {D} num. used for 2 on checks/etc. ◆b.f. betray 贰臣 èrchén
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 贰:) From 二 èr 'two', enlarged by 弋 (yì) 'tally', and again by 贝(貝 bèi) 'money'.
贰 is a phonetic component in 腻(膩) nì 'oily'.
