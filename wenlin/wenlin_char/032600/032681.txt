258  義(S义) [yì] justice; 主義 zhǔyì -ism; 意義 yìyì meaning  [yí] [xī]
義[义] ¹²yì {E} b.f. ①justice; righteousness 正义 ¹zhèngyì* ②chivalry; sense of honor 义气 yìqi ③meaning; significance 意义 ¹yìyì* 字义 ³zìyì ④human ties; relationship 情义 ³qíngyì ⑤adopted; adoptive 义子 yìzǐ 义女 yìnǚ ⑥artificial; false 义齿 yìchǐ ⑦volunteer 义务 ¹yìwù*
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 义:) From 𦍌(羊 yáng) 'sheep' over 我 wǒ phonetic. The simple form is 义.
羊 yáng 'sheep' conveys the meaning 'good-natured', because that's how sheep are; compare 美 měi 'beautiful', 善 shàn 'good', etc.
