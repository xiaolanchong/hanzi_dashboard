==== 羁(F羈) [jī] bridle; restrain; 羁绊 jībàn yoke; 羁旅 travel
羁[羈/羇/覊/覉] ²¹jī {E} b.f. ①bridle 无羁之马 wújīzhīmǎ ②restrain 羁绊 ¹jībàn* 不羁 ¹bùjī ③an inn; to lodge 羁旅 ²jīlǚ 羁心 ⁴jīxīn
From 罒(网 wǎng) 'net', 革 (gé) 'leather', and 马(馬 mǎ) 'horse'.
"A halter; subdue, restrain -- a 革 leather 网 (net:) halter on a 馬 horse" --Karlgren.
Variant forms are 覊 (with 覀 in place of 罒), and 羇/覉 (with 奇 in place of 马).
