114  政 [zhèng] 政府 zhèngfǔ government; 政治 zhèngzhì politics  [zhēng]
政 ²zhèng {E} b.f. ①government 政府 zhèngfǔ* 宪政 xiànzhèng* ②politics; political 政治 ¹zhèngzhì* ③affairs of a family or an organization 家政 jiāzhèng*
From 正 zhèng 'correct' and 攵(攴 pū) 'strike, beat'.
政 zhèng is etymologically the same word as 正 zhèng.
