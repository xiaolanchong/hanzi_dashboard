1119 敵(S敌) [dí] (敵人 dírén) enemy; 敵軍 enemy troops
敵[敌] ¹dí {D} b.f. ①enemy; foe 敌人 dírén ②match; equal 敌手 ¹díshǒu*
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 敌:) The full form 敵 is 啇 dī phonetic plus 攵(攴 pū) 'beat'. In the simple form 敌, 啇 is replaced by 舌 (shé) 'tongue': stick 舌 tongue out at enemies, then 攵 beat them?
