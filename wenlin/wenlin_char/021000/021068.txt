---- 剌 [là] to cut, slash; cruel  [lá] [lā]
剌 ⁷là char. 剌子 ³làzi 粗剌剌 cūlàlà 跋剌 bálà
剌 ⁷lā char. 热辣辣 rèlālā 大剌剌的 dàlāla de
The left side is 束 (shù) 'bundle'. Don't confuse 剌 là with 刺 cì!
The right side is 刂(刀 dāo) 'knife'.
"To cut, slash; cruel -- to 刀 cut asunder 束 something which is tied up" --Karlgren.
