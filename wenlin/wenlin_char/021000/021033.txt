210  利 [lì] (利益 lìyì) benefit, profit; 利用 utilize; 胜利 triumph
利 ⁴lì {C} n. ①profit; interest ②advantage; benefit ③Surname ◆b.f. ①sharp 锋利 ²fēnglì ②favorable 顺利 shùnlì* ③do good (to); be beneficial (for) 利于 lìyú
From 禾 (hé) 'grain' and 刂(刀 dāo) 'knife'. Harvest the 禾 grain with a 刀 knife: benefit, profit.
