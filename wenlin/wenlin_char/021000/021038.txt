1926 刮 [guā] scrape; 刮胡子 shave; (F颳) (wind) blow  [kā]
刮 ¹guā {A} v. ①scrape; shave ②daub/smear (with paste/etc.) ③plunder; extort
刮[颳] ²guā {E} v. blow (of wind)
刮 ⁵kā char. 刮吃 ¹kāchi
From 舌 (shé 'tongue') and 刂(刀 dāo) 'knife'.
舌 isn't really 'tongue' here; it's the same old phonetic component as in 话 huà, 活 huó, etc.
