779  草 [cǎo] grass; herb; 草莓 cǎoméi strawberry  [zào]
草 ¹cǎo* {A} n. grass; straw; herb ◆s.v. careless; hasty; rough ◆b.f. ①draft 草稿 cạ̌ogǎo* ②〈coll.〉 female (of animals)
Originally 艸, a picture of growing plants. 早 zǎo phonetic was added, and 艸 became abbreviated to 艹, which occurs as a component in many characters denoting plants.
