==== 荅 [dā] (a kind of plant; obsolete, phonetic in 搭 dā etc.); (=答)  [dá] [tà]
No single-syllable words currently in dictionary.	add new s.s.w.
From 艹(草 cǎo) 'grass' and 合 (hé) 'together'.
"The 合 tangled 艹 plant" --Karlgren(1923).
Cognate with, and sometimes used instead of, 答 dā which has 竹 (zhú) 'bamboo' not 艹 'grass'.
