1019 荣(F榮) [róng] (光荣, 荣誉) glory, honor
荣[榮] ⁶róng {E} b.f. ①honor 荣誉 róngyù* ②honorable; glorious 光荣 guāngróng ③thriving 繁荣 fánróng* ◆n. Surname
The top is 荧(熒) yíng phonetic, abbreviated to 𤇾 in the full form, and 𫇦 in the simple form. The bottom is 木 (mù) 'tree'.
Compare 劳(勞) láo 'labor', 营(營) yíng 'camp'.
