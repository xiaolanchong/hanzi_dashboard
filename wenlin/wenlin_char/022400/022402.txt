1525 垂 [chuí] hang down, droop; 垂直 chúizhí vertical, perpendicular  [zhuì]
垂 ¹chuí {C} v./b.f. ①hang down; droop; let fall ②bequeath to posterity; hand down ③near; approach ④condescend
"Tree with drooping branches?" --Karlgren.
The ancient form 𠂹 was like the tops of 華(华) huá and 差 chà.
"A bough loaded with leaves and drooping flowers; 草木华叶下垂..." --Wieger.
