1373 津 [jīn] (ferry crossing; saliva); 津津有味 with pleasure
津 ⁸jīn {E} n. 〈wr.〉 ①ferry crossing; ford ②short for Tianjin ◆b.f. ①saliva 津液 ²jīnyè ②sweat ③moist; damp
From 氵(水 shuǐ) 'water' and 聿.
According to Karlgren(1923), the phonetic was originally not 聿 yù, but a now-obsolete character composed of 聿 and 彡. The same character was also phonetic in 盡(尽) jìn.
