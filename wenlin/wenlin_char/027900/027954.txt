798  洲 [zhōu] continent; 亚洲 Asia; 欧洲 Europe; 非洲 Africa
洲 ¹zhōu* {D} n. continent ◆b.f. ①river islet; sand bar; shoal 沙洲 shāzhōu ②(river) delta 三角洲 sānjiǎozhōu
From 氵(水 shuǐ) 'water' and 州 zhōu 'prefecture'.
"Islet; continent -- etymologically the same word as 州; character tautologically enlarged by 水" --Karlgren.
