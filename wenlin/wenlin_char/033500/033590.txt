1041 茶 [chá] tea; 茶叶 cháyè tea leaves; 红茶 hóngchá black tea
茶 ¹chá* {A} n. tea
The bottom is 余 yú phonetic, minus one horizontal stroke (see the rare related character 荼 tú 'bitter plant').
The English word 'tea' is from the Chinese word chá, or, to be exact, from 茶 as it is pronounced in Fujian province.
