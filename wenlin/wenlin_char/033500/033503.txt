2829 苟 [gǒu] herb, greens; if; (Surname); 不苟 bùgǒu not lax; conscientious  [gōu]
苟 ²gǒu {E} b.f. ①careless; negligent ②indifferent (to right or wrong) 苟同 gǒutóng ◆conj. 〈wr.〉 if
Composed of 艹(草 cǎo 'grass') over 句 jù phonetic.
In classical Chinese, 苟 means "if indeed".
Not to be confused with 茍 jì in which the top is divided in the middle.
