1620 茫 [máng] 茫茫 vast, boundless & indistinct  [huǎng]
茫 ⁴máng {E} b.f. vast; borderless (as an expanse of water) 茫茫 ¹mángmáng* 渺茫 miǎománg
"High waters; vast and vague, chaos" --Wieger (page 404).
From 氵(水 shuǐ) 'water' and 芒 máng phonetic. There is also an old character 汒 máng for the same word ("Inundation; sudden; vast" --Wieger).
Don't confuse 茫 máng with 范 fàn (范围 'range').
