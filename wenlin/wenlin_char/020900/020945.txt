1988 凑(F湊) [còu] gather together; happen by chance
凑[湊] ¹còu {C} v. ①gather together; pool; collect ②happen by chance ③take advantage of ④move close to; press near
From 冫 or 氵 (水 shuǐ) 'water' and 奏 zòu phonetic.
"Streams joining into one; join, collect, bring together; hit upon, occur" --Karlgren(1923).
