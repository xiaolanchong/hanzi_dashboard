2551 凹 [āo] concave; 凹透镜 concave lens; 凹凸 āotū uneven  [wā] (=洼)
凹 ¹āo {D} v. concave; sunken; dented
洼/凹[窪/-] ⁴wā {E} v. be hollowed-out; low-lying ◆n. low-lying area; depression M:piàn/²kuài 片/块
A picture, opposite of 凸 tū 'convex'.
Only the pronunciation āo is standard, though wā is still common. Properly, wā should be written 洼.
