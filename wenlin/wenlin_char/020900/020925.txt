---- 冽 [liè] cold; clear
冽 ⁶liè {E} b.f. cold 凛冽 lǐnliè 栗冽 ¹lìliè
洌/冽 ⁸liè b.f. clear (of water or wine) 寒洌 hánliè 泠洌 língliè 洌泉 lièquán
From 冫 (bīng) 'ice' and 列 liè phonetic.
Compare 洌, which is sometimes interchangeable with the meaning 'clear'; 冽 (with 'ice') is used more for the meaning 'cold'.
