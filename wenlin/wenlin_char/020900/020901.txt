---- 冥 [míng] 幽冥 yōumíng shadowy; the nether world  [mián] [miàn]
冥 ⁵míng {E} b.f. ①netherworld 冥府 Míngfǔ* ②dark; obscure 幽冥 ¹yōumíng ③deep; abstruse 冥思出神 míngsīchūshén ④dull; stupid 冥顽 míngwán
From 冖 (mì) 'cover', 日 (rì) 'sun' and 六 (liù) 'six'.
"Dark, obscure; clouded, misty; mysterious; ignorant; Hades --- the 六 (Chinese double-) hours when the 日 sun is 冖 covered, not to be seen" --Karlgren.
