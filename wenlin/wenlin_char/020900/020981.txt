==== 凵 [qiǎn] [kǎn] (radical 17, component: 'receptacle'; 山字底 shānzìdǐ)
No single-syllable words currently in dictionary.	add new s.s.w.
凵 depicts a pit. It may have been the original character for the word 坎 kǎn.
Seventeenth of the 214 radicals.
汉语大字典 gives two pronunciations, qiǎn and kǎn, the former corresponding to the radical in 说文.
