2251 恥(S耻) [chǐ] 恥辱 chǐrǔ shame, humiliation; 可恥 shameful; 無恥 shameless
恥[耻] ³chǐ {E} b.f. shame; disgrace; humiliation 耻辱 chị̌rǔ*
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 耻:) From 耳 (ěr) 'ear' and (simple form:) 止 zhǐ phonetic or (full form:) 心 (xīn) 'heart'.
Does 耳 'ear' mean you are ashamed when people HEAR about your misbehavior, or that your ears turn red?
