1828 叢(S丛) [cóng] crowd; 叢書 series of books; 叢林 jungle
叢[丛] ²cóng {C} b.f. ①crowd together 丛集 ¹cóngjí ②a crowd 人丛 réncóng* ③collection 丛书 ¹cóngshū ④clump; thicket; grove 丛林 cónglín ◆n. Surname
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 丛:) The full form 叢 is 丵 'bush' over 取 (qǔ) 'gather'.
"To 取 gather 丵 bushes. A bushy place, crowded; a collection, to collect" --Wieger.
The simple form 丛 is 从 cóng phonetic over 一 a line.
