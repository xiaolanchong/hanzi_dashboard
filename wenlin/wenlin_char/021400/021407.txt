243  原 [yuán] originally; 原来 it turns out; original; 原因 cause
原 ⁵yuán {D} b.f. ①primary; original 原始 ¹yuánshǐ* ②unprocessed; raw 原料 yuánliào ③simple ④pardon; forgive 原谅 yuánliàng* ⑤plain; plateau 草原 cǎoyuán 高原 gāoyuán* ⑥origin; source 原地 ¹yuándì* ⑦element; atom 原子 yuánzǐ* ⑧graveyard ◆n. Surname
A 泉 (quán) spring gushing out from a 厂 cliff, the origin of the water. Now 源 yuán with an added 氵(水 shuǐ 'water') means 'origin', and 原 means 'originally'. In 原, the bottom of 泉 quán has become abbreviated from 水 to 小 (xiǎo 'small').
