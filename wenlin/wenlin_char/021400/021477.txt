604  句 [jù] sentence  [gōu] [gòu] [qú]
句 ²jù {A} b.f. sentence 句子 ¹jùzi ◆m. sentence; line of poetry/song
"Hook; to hook; seduce; mark off by a hook, punctuate. -- See 勾 gōu, the now current form in this sense... (something marked off:) sentence, passage, vocable -- the seal has 丩 (now corrupted 勹) 'hook' signific and 口 kǒu phonetic" --Karlgren.
The word 'hook' is now written 钩(鉤) gōu.
