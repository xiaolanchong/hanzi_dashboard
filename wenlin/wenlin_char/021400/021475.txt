200  口 [kǒu] mouth; 门口 ménkǒu doorway; 人口 rénkǒu population
口 kǒu(r)* {A} n. ①mouth ②opening; entrance ③Great Wall gateway ④hole; cut ⑤knife edge ⑥age of draft animal ◆m. (for mouthfuls/people/wells/etc.) | Wǒmen jiā jiù yǒu liạ̌ng ∼ rén. 我们家就有两∼人。 There are only two people in our family. ◆attr. 〈lg.〉 oral; buccal
Picture of a mouth. 口 is a common component in other characters (such as 和 hé 'harmony' and 名 míng 'name'). It should be distinguished from the component 囗 wéi 'surround' (as in 国國 guó 'country' and 园園 yuán 'garden').
