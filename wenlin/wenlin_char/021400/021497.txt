1145 叹(F嘆) [tàn] sigh  [yǐ] [yòu]
叹[嘆] ¹tàn {D} b.f. ①sigh 叹气 ¹tànqì* ②exclaim (with admiration/etc.) 赞叹 zàntàn ③chant; recite; intone 咏叹 yǒngtàn
From 口 (kǒu) 'mouth' and  (jiān) 'distress'.
 is abbreviated to 又 in the simple form 叹.
A variant form is 歎, with 欠 (qiàn) 'yawn' instead of 口.
Note: according to Karlgren(1940), 叹(嘆) tàn 'sigh' and 难(難) nán 'difficult' are cognates, and  is the old form of 艰(艱) jiān 'difficult'. Wieger, following 說文, treats  as short for 暵 hàn.
