---- 厩(F廄) [jiù] stable, pen (for horses etc.); to gather; (surname)
厩[廄] ¹⁰jiù {E} b.f. stable; cattle shed; pen 马厩 mǎjiù
The simple form 厩 is from 厂 (chǎng) 'shack' (a roof) and 既 jì phonetic.
The full form 廄 has 广 (guǎng) in place of 厂, and 殳 (shū) in place of 旡 (jì).
Variant forms are 廐 and 廏, with 皀 (xiāng) in place of .
