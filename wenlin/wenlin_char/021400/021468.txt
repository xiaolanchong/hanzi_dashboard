From 宀 'roof', 火 'fire', and 又 'hand'.
"A hand holding a torch to search a house." --王宏源.
This was the original form of 搜 sōu 'search'. It was modified to 叟, and borrowed as a phonetic loan for 叟 sǒu 'old man'.
