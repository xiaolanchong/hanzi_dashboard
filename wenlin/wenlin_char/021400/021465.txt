1790 叙(F敘) [xù] chat; (叙述 xùshù, 叙事 xùshì) narrate, relate
叙[敘] ⁴xù {E} v. ①talk; chat ②narrate; recount; relate ③assess; appraise ④arrange in order ◆n. preface
From 余 yú phonetic and 攵(攴 pū) 'beat' or 又 (yòu) 'hand'.
"Arrange in order; order, series, rank, degree; seriatim; to expose in order, narrate, preface --- etymologically the same word as 序" --Karlgren(1923).
