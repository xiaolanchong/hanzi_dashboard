---- 眇 [miǎo] (rare) make the eye small; delicate  [miào]
渺/藐/眇 ²miǎo {E} b.f. tiny; insignificant 渺小 miạ̌oxiǎo
眇 ⁸miǎo b.f. blind
From 目 (mù) 'eye' and 少 (shǎo) 'small'.
"Make the eye(s) small, peer; one-eyed; small, delicate, insignificant" --Karlgren.
Etymologically related to 秒杪妙. Component in 渺 and 缈.
