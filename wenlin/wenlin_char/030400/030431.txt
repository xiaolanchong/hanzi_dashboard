1595 盟 [méng] (联盟 liánméng) alliance, pact  [míng] 盟誓  [mèng]
盟 ²méng {E} b.f. alliance; pact 联盟 liánméng ◆n. (Mongol) league
盟 ⁷míng b.f. (old pronunciation for ²méng in certain compounds) 盟誓 ³míngshì
From 明 míng 'bright, clear' over 皿 (mǐn) 'sacrificial cup'.
"Solemn declaration before the gods with sacrifice, covenant, pact -- etymologically the same word as 明 míng (declaration); character specifyingly enlarged by 皿 sacrificial cup..." --Karlgren(1923).
Except in the phrase 盟誓 míngshì 'take an oath', the pronunciation is méng rather than míng.
