1247 盪(S荡) [dàng] (=蕩)  [tàng] (=燙); (=趟)
No single-syllable words currently in dictionary.	add new s.s.w.
From 湯 tāng phonetic and 皿 (mǐn) 'dish, utensil'.
"Bath tub; purify, wash off; eliminate, do away with; move, shake, disturb" --Karlgren(1923).
Often interchangeable with 蕩 dàng among full form characters. Among simple full form characters, only 荡 is used.
When pronounced tàng, 盪 is a variant of 燙 or 趟.
