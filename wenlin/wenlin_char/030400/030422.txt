1068 盖(F蓋) [gài] cover, shell; 盖子 lid
盖[蓋] ¹gài {B} n./v. cover ◆v. ①seal; affix a seal ②surpass; top; prevail over ③build; construct ◆adv. 〈wr.〉 about; around | ∼ qiān rén ∼千人 about a thousand people ◆s.v. 〈slang〉 super; marvelous; excellent | Déguó ²duì zhēn ∼. 德国队真∼。 The German team is excellent!
The bottom is 皿 (mǐn) 'container'. The top represents a lid; in the full form 蓋 it is 艹 plus 去 (qù 'go'); in the simple form 盖 it is 𦍌 (compare 羊 yáng 'sheep').
