1113 監(S监) [jiān] 監獄 prison; 監督 supervise  [jiàn] [kàn]
監[监] ¹⁰jiān {E} b.f. ①supervise; inspect; watch 监督 ¹jiāndū* 监工 jiāngōng* ②inspector; supervisor 监守 ²jiānshǒu ③prison; jail 监狱 jiānyù*
监[監] ¹⁷jiàn {E} b.f. ①eunuch 太监 tàijiàn* ②〈trad.〉 government establishment 国子监 Guózǐjiàn
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 监:) Originally from 卧 (wò) 'bend down' over 血 (xuè) 'blood'.
"The seal has: to 卧 bend over a 血 sacrificial bowl with blood: examine the omen" --Karlgren.
The dot on top of 血 got moved up under 卜.
In the simple form 监, 臣 got abbreviated to two strokes.
监 is a phonetic component in 槛 jiàn 'cage', and 监 is simplified to  in 鉴(鑒) jiàn 'examine; mirror'.
Also see 蓝篮兰 lán, 览 lǎn, 盐 yán, and 临 lín.
