==== 懨(S恹) [yān] 懨懨 weak and weary through illness; peaceful  [chú]
懨/懕/厭[恹/厌] ¹¹yān char. 恹恹 yānyān
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 恹:) From 忄(心 xīn) 'heart' and 厌(厭) yàn phonetic.
A variant form is 懕. Sometimes written 厭. Probably cognate with 厌(厭) yàn: fed up; weary; weak; sleepy; peaceful -- or satiated; content.
