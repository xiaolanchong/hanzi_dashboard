1978 晒(F曬) [shài] roast in sun
晒[曬] shài* {B} v. ①shine on ②sun-dry; soak up the sun | Wọ̌ xǐhuan zài hǎitān shang ∼ tàiyáng. 我喜欢在海滩上∼太阳。 I like soaking up the sun on the beach. ③〈slang〉 cold-shoulder ◆s.v. sun-drenched
From 日 (rì) 'sun' and (full form:) 麗 lì phonetic or (simple form:) 西 xī phonetic.
It's remarkable for 麗 lì to be phonetic in 曬 shài. Compare 灑 sǎ and 釃 shī. Also compare the relation between 史 shǐ and 吏 lì.
