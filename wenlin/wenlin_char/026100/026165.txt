==== 昵 [nì] intimate; 狎昵 xiánì improperly familiar with  [nǐ] [zhì]
昵 ⁶nì {E} b.f. intimate 昵爱 ²nì'ài 亲昵词语 qīnnì cíyǔ
Originally 尼 (ní), depicting two people (尸 shī and 匕 bǐ) close together. 日 (rì) 'sun' was added (meaning that the sun is close, according to 说文). The original character 尼 was borrowed for various loan-words from Sanskrit and other languages.
