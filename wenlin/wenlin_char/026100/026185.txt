2352 晉(S晋) [jìn] promote; a dynasty (265-420); ䷢ Progress
晉[晋] Jìn {E} n. ①Jin dynasty (265-420) ②short name for Shanxi province ③Surname ◆v. ①enter; advance ②promote
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 晋:) The top 亚 was originally two arrows pointing downwards, like 至 (zhì) 'arrive' doubled. The bottom is 日 (rì) 'sun'.
Etymologically, 晋 jìn appears to be the same word as 进(進) jìn.
"进也。日出, 万物进。易曰明出, 地上晋。从日, 从二至, 会意。" --说文解字 (易 here means 易经 The Book of Changes).
"The 至 arriving, advancing 日 sun?" --Karlgren(1923).
Compare 普 pǔ.
