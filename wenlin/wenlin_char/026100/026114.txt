1833 昂 [áng] (昂首 ángshǒu) hold one's head high; high, soaring  [yàng]
昂 ¹áng* {E} v. hold (one's head) high ◆b.f. high; soaring 高昂 gāo'áng
From 日 (rì) 'sun' and 卬, a person standing by 卩 (jié) a person kneeling; cognate to 仰 yǎng 'face upward'.
Compare 迎 yíng 'welcome'.
Don't confuse 昂 áng with 昴 Mǎo 'The Pleiades'.
