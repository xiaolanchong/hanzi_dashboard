569  显(F顯) [xiǎn] appear; 显然 xiǎnrán obvious
显[顯] ¹xiǎn {D} v. ①appear; be obvious ②demonstrate; show; display ◆b.f. illustrious and powerful 显要 ¹xiǎnyào*
The old form 㬎 derived from 日 (rì) 'sun' shining over 絲 (sī) 'silk threads' (but with only four dots at the bottom). The full form 顯 has 頁 (yè) 'head' added. In the simple form 显 the bottom is abbreviated to 业 (yè).
