327  候 [hòu] (时候 shíhou) time; wait; situation
候 ⁴hòu {E} v. wait; await ◆b.f. ①inquire after 问候 wènhòu* ②pay (bills) 候账 ²hòuzhàng ③time; season 时候 shíhou* ④condition; state; symptoms 气候 qìhòu*
From 侯 hóu ('marquis') phonetic, plus an extra vertical 丨 stroke. 候 hòu was formed by adding an extra 亻(人 rén) to 侯 hóu; subsequently one of the two 亻 was reduced to 丨.
