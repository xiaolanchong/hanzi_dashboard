1028 雞(S鸡) [jī] chicken; 宮保雞丁 Kung Pao Chicken
雞[鸡] ²jī {A} n. ①chicken ②〈slang〉 prostitute M:²zhī 只
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 鸡:) The full form 雞 is composed of 奚 xī phonetic and 隹 (zhuī) 'bird'.
In the simple form 鸡, the left side is abbreviated to 又 (yòu), and the right side is 鸟(鳥 niǎo) 'bird'.
A variant form is 鷄.
