==== 雋(S隽) [juàn] 雋永 juànyǒng meaningful  [jùn] [zuì]
雋[隽] ⁷juàn b.f. handsome; talented 隽永 juànyǒng
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 隽:) The top is 隹 (zhuī) 'bird'. The bottom is from 弓 (gōng) 'bow' turned sideways, pointing straight up at the bird. In the simple form, the bow has become altered to 乃 (nǎi).
"To shoot a bird on the wing; 从弓所以射隹, 会意。" --Wieger.
