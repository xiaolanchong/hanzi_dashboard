935  零 [líng] zero; 零件 língjiàn spare part; 零钱 spare change  [līng] [lián]
零 ²líng {A} num. ①zero ②naught; nil ◆b.f. ①odd; a little extra 零头 língtóu(r) ②fractional; part 零数 língshù(r)* ③small change 零钱 ¹língqián ④(mechanical) part 零件 ¹língjiàn* ⑤wither and fall 零落 ¹língluò
零 līng {E} char. 孤零零 gūlīnglīng 丁零零 dīnglīnglīng
From 雨 (yǔ) 'rain' and 令 lìng phonetic.
"To drip, a drop; fraction, trifle; odds; nought" --Karlgren.
雨 'rain' indicated the original meaning 'raindrop'. An old form 霝 is 口口口 three drops of water (instead of 令) under 雨; this 霝 survives in 靈(灵) líng.
Meaning 'zero', 零 is also written 〇 líng in numbers.
