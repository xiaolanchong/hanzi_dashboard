810  雪 [xuě] snow; 雪花 xuěhuā snowflake; 滑雪 huáxuě to ski
雪 ¹xuě {A} n. snow | ∼ xià de zhēn dà. ∼下得真大。 It's snowing heavily. ◆b.f. ①snow-white 雪白 xuěbái ②wipe out (a humiliation); avenge (a wrong) 雪耻 xuěchǐ 雪恨 xuěhèn
From 雨 (yǔ) 'rain' and 彐, picture of a hand (), originally from 彗 (huì) 'broom': 雪 snow is frozen 雨 rain which can be swept with a 彗 broom.
