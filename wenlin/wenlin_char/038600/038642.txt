637  雲(S云) [yún] (雲彩 yúncai) cloud
雲[云] ¹yún* {A} n. ①cloud ②short name for Yunnan ③Surname
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 云:) Picture of clouds. The character 云 was borrowed for the word yún 'say' (now rare), which happened to sound the same, and the full form 雲 was created with 雨 (yǔ) 'rain' added to the top.
