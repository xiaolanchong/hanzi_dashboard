1265 雷 [léi] thunder; 雷雨 thunderstorm; 雷达 radar  [lèi]
雷 ¹léi {B} n. ①thunder ②〈slang〉 disaster; calamity ③Surname ◆b.f. mine 地雷 dìléi
From 雨 (yǔ) 'rain' and 田 (tián) 'field'. Originally there were many fields, with a lightning-bolt moving between them. Apparently 畾 léi ('space between fields') was phonetic. Compare 累 lèi 'tired'.
