---- 犀 [xī] 犀牛 xīniú rhinoceros
犀 ²⁴xī {E} b.f. rhinoceros 犀牛 ¹xīniú 犀鸟 xīniǎo ◆char. 木犀饭 mùxifàn
From 尸 and 氺 (equivalent to 尾 wěi 'tail') and 牛 (niú) 'ox'.
"The Tibetan yak ox; rhinoceros -- the 牛 ox remarkable for its 尾 tail" --Karlgren(1923).
12 strokes according to 汉字正字手册, but only 11 strokes according to 康熙字典. (The vertical strokes can be connected?)
