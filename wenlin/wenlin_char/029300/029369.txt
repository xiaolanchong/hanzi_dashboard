1408 犹(F猶) [yóu] still, yet; 犹豫 yóuyù hesitate; 犹如 yóurú as if  [yòu]
犹[猶] ⁶yóu {E} 〈wr.〉 adv. still; yet ◆v./conj. just as; like; as if
From 犭(犬 quǎn) 'dog' and (full form:) 酋 qiú or (simple form:) 尤 yóu phonetic.
"Monkey; (character applied to a homophonous abstract word:) resemble, like, as, as if, equal to; yet, still, still more" --Karlgren.
