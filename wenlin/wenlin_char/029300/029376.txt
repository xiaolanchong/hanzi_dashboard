813  狀(S状) [zhuàng] (狀態, 狀况) condition, state of affairs
狀[状] ²zhuàng {E} b.f. ①form; shape 形状 ¹xíngzhuàng* ②state; condition 状况 zhuàngkuàng* ③account; record 供状 gòngzhuàng ④written complaint; plaint 告状 gàozhuàng ⑤certificate 奖状 jiǎngzhuàng* ⑥describe; narrate 描状 miáozhuàng ⑦〈lg.〉 (adverbial) modifier 状语 zhuàngyǔ*
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 状:) From 丬(爿) qiáng phonetic and 犬 (quǎn) 'dog'.
"A dog that blusters, to feign," says Wieger, who seems to imply that 状 is etymologically related to 妆 zhuāng 'makeup' and 装 zhuāng 'disguise'.
丬(爿) qiáng is phonetic, compare 壮 zhuàng 'strong' and 将 jiāng 'future'.
