---- 狎 [xiá] (狎昵 xiánì) be improperly familiar with
狎 ¹⁰xiá b.f. improperly familiar with 狎妓 ¹xiájì 狎昵 xiánì
From 犭(犬 quǎn) 'dog' and 甲 jiǎ phonetic. An ancient meaning was 'domesticate dogs'.
"Close relations, intimate with; disrespectul to" --Karlgren.
Etymologically the same word as 狭 xiá 'narrow'.
