575  態(S态) [tài] form; 態度 tàidu manner; 狀態 zhuàngtài state
態[态] ²tài {E} b.f. ①appearance; state; condition 形态 xíngtài* ②attitude; demeanor 态度 tàidu* ③〈lg.〉 voice; aspect 主动态 zhǔdòngtài ④〈comp.〉 mode
From 能 (néng) over 心 (xīn) 'heart'.
According to Karlgren, 能 was used for a character pronounced tái as well as for néng 'able', and that character was phonetic in 態 tài.
The simple form 态 has 太 tài phonetic.
