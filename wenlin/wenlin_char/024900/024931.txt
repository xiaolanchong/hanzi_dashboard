1187 慣(S惯) [guàn] be used to; 習慣 xíguàn be used to; habit; custom
慣[惯] ¹guàn {C} v. ①be used to | Wǒ gàn ∼ le. 我干∼了。 I'm used to working. ②indulge; spoil | Háizi bùnéng tài ∼. 孩子不能太∼。 Children should not be overindulged. ◆b.f. habitual; customary 习惯 xíguàn* ◆adv. in the habit of
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 惯:) From 忄(心 xīn) 'heart' and 贯(貫) guàn. The top of 贯 depicts a coin with a string through a hole in its middle, to convey the meaning 'penetrate' (贯穿 guànchuān).
"Know thoroughly, familiar with, used to, practised in" --Karlgren.
