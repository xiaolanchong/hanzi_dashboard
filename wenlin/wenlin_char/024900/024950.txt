1060 慶(S庆) [qìng] 慶祝 celebrate; 慶賀 congratulate; 慶幸 rejoice  [qīng] [qiāng]
慶[庆] ¹qìng {E} b.f. ①celebrate; congratulate 庆祝 qìngzhù* ②occasion for celebration 国庆 Guóqìng* ◆n. Surname
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 庆:) The full form 慶 is composed of 鹿 (lù) 'deer' (abbreviated), 心 xīn 'heart', and 夂 (zhǐ) 'walk slowly'.
"To 夂 come and present a 鹿 deer to express one's 心 good wishes" --Karlgren.
The simple form 庆 is 广 over 大.
