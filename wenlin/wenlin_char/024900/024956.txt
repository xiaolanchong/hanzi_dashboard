---- 慼 [qī] grief, sorrow, sad
No single-syllable words currently in dictionary.	add new s.s.w.
From 戚 qī (as in 亲戚 qīnqī 'relative') and 心 (xīn) 'heart'.
According to Karlgren, 慼 is etymologically the same word as 戚, the meaning being "grief, sorrow, sad". (戚 formerly meant 'battle-axe', and still sometimes has the meaning 'sorrow').
