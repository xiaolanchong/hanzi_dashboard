713  源 [yuán] (来源 láiyuán) source, origin; 资源 zīyuán resources
源 ⁸yuán {D} b.f. ①source (of a river); fountainhead 水源 shuǐyuán* ②source; cause; origin 来源 ¹láiyuán 根源 gēnyuán
From 氵(水 shuǐ) 'water' and 原 yuán 'original'.
Etymologically the same word as 原, which shows a 泉 (quán) spring coming out of a 厂 cliff, the original source of 水 water.
