1653 溝(S沟) [gōu] gulley; trench  [gǎng] [kòu]
溝[沟] ¹gōu {C} n. ①ditch; channel; trench ②groove; rut; furrow ③gully; ravine M:²dào/tiáo 道/条
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 沟:) From 氵(水 shuǐ) 'water' and (full form:) 冓 gòu or (simple form:) 勾 gōu phonetic.
冓 is the old form of 构(構) gòu 'structure'. 沟 gōu is cognate to 构 gòu:
"The 'trellis-work' on the field: ditches, drains" --Karlgren.
