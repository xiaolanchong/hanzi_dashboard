2233 溢 [yì] overflow; 洋溢 yángyì be permeated with
溢 ¹¹yì {E} v. 〈wr.〉 overflow; brim over ◆b.f. excessive; undue 溢美 yìměi*
From 氵(水 shuǐ) 'water' and 益 yì 'overflow, profit'.
Etymologically, 溢 is the same word as 益, which is a picture of ☵ water spilling out of a 皿 dish. 溢 has an extra 氵(水) added, to emphasize the fundamental meaning, while 益 is used more for the abstract
notion.
