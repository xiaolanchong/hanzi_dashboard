---- 溥 [pǔ] broad; vast; common; universal  [fū] [bù] [bó] [pò]
溥 ⁷pǔ b.f. broad; extensive; universal 溥博 pǔbó ◆n. Surname ◆char. 溥漠 pǔmò
From 氵(水 shuǐ) 'water' and 尃 fū ('spread') phonetic.
"Vast, universal" --Karlgren(1923).
Compare 普 pǔ which has the same meanings; perhaps 溥 and 普 are etymologically the same word. Their ancient pronunciations (as reconstructed by Karlgren) were the same.
