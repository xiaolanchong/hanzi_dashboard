==== 滎(S荥) [Xíng] 滎陽 Xíngyáng (place in Henan)  [Yíng] 滎經 Yíngjīng (place in Sichuan)  [yīng]

(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 荥:) From 𫇦(𤇾) yíng phonetic over 水 (shuǐ) 'water'.
An ancient meaning was "rivulet"; the character is used in place names.
