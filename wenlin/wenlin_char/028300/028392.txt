1995 滨(F濱) [bīn] 海滨 seashore; 滨临 be close to (body of water)
滨[濱] ²bīn {E} b.f. ①bank; brink; shore 湖滨 húbīn ②waterfront 海滨 hǎibīn ③be close to; border on (water) 滨江 bīnjiāng
From 氵(水 shuǐ) 'water' and 宾(賓) bīn phonetic.
The character 濒(瀕) bīn is sometimes used instead of 滨(濱) for the meaning 'be close to' (滨临 bīnlín).
