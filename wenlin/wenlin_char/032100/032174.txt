==== 綮 [qǐng] 肯綮 kěnqǐng vital juncture  [qǐ] (=棨) embroidered streamer  [qìng]
綮 ⁴qǐng char. 肯綮 ²kěnqǐng
From 啓 qǐ abbreviated phonetic and 糸 (sī) 'silk'.
'Embroidered streamer' is also written 棨, with 木 instead of 糸.
In 肯綮 kěnqǐng, 綮 is pronounced qǐng. This is standard according to 现代汉语词典 and 现代汉语规范字典 (which says, "不读 qìng").
