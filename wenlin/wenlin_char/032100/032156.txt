1553 綜(S综) [zōng] 綜合 zōnghé synthesize   ([zèng] heddle)  [zòng]
綜[综] ⁵zōng {E} b.f. comprehensive; aggregate; composite 综合 ¹zōnghé*
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 综:) From 纟(糸 sī) 'silk thread' and 宗 zōng phonetic.
"Arrange the threads in weaving; collect, arrange" --Karlgren.
(A 综 zèng 'heddle' must be some kind of weaving apparatus.)
