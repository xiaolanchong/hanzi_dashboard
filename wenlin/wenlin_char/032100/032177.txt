1519 綱(S纲) [gāng] fishing net rope; guiding principle; 綱領 program
綱[纲] ⁵gāng {D} n. ①headrope of a fishing net ②key link; guiding principle ③outline; program ④〈bio.〉 class
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 纲:) From 纟(糸 sī) 'silk' (rope) and 冈(岡) gāng phonetic.
Compare 网 wǎng 'net' (冈 is from 岡 which is from 网 plus 山).
