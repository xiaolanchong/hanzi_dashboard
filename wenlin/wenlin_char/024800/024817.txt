1909 惱(S恼) [nǎo] 惱火 angry, annoyed
惱[恼] ²nǎo {E} v. ①become angry ②exasperate; anger ◆b.f. ①unhappy; worried 懊恼 àonǎo ②irritating 烦恼 fánnǎo
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 恼:) From 忄(心 xīn) 'heart' and 㐫(𡿺) nǎo 'brain'.
When angry (恼火 nǎohuǒ), the 心 heart and 脑 brain seem to be on 火 fire.
