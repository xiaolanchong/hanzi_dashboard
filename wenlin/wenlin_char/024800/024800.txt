1480 惠 [huì] 恩惠 ēnhuì favor, kindness; 贤惠 xiánhuì virtuous (女)
惠 ⁶huì {E} b.f. ①kind; benevolent; gentle 恩惠 ēnhuì ②benefit; profit; favor 实惠 ¹shíhuì*
The bottom is 心 (xīn) 'heart'. The top  depicts a yoked ox.  is the old form of 专(專 zhuān).
" attached 心 heart" --Karlgren.
惠 huì is a component in 穗 suì, 蕙 huì, and 蟪 huì.
