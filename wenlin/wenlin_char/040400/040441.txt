2274 鷹(S鹰) [yīng] hawk, eagle
鷹[鹰] ²yīng {D} n. hawk; eagle; falcon
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 鹰:) From 广 (guǎng) 'house', 亻(人 rén) 'person', 隹 (zhuī) 'bird', and 鸟(鳥 niǎo) 'bird'.
"The 隹 bird used (for hunting) by 人 man and kept in his 广 house... character tautologically enlarged by 鳥" --Karlgren.
