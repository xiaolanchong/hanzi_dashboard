==== 鷳(S鹇) [xián] 白鷳 báixián silver pheasant
鷳/鷴[鹇] ¹³xián char. 白鹇 báixián
Note: From 閒 (not 閑) and 鳥. Wenlin formerly had 閑 plus 鳥 here, but that is another form: 鷴.
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 鹇:) From 闲(閑 or 閒) xián phonetic and 鸟(鳥 niǎo) 'bird'.
There are two full forms: 鷳 and 鷴.
