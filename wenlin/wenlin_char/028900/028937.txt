2611 焉 [yān] 心不在焉 absent-minded  [yí]
焉 ⁶yān {E} pr. 〈wr.〉 where; how? ◆b.f. thus; thereupon 于焉 yúyān 焉博 ²yānbó
Picture of a kind of bird (pheasant?). The head looks like 正 zhèng, and the body is like the bottom of 鳥(鸟 niǎo) in its full form. This character was borrowed as a phonetic loan for an adverbial suffix and a final particle in classical Chinese.
