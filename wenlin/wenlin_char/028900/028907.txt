2451 烫(F燙) [tàng] hot (burning hot to the touch, cf. 热 rè)
烫[燙] ²tàng {B} v. ①scald; burn | Wǒ de shǒu ràng kāishuǐ ∼ le. 我的手让开水∼了。 My hand was scalded by boiling water. ②warm in hot water ③iron; press ④perm (the hair) ◆s.v. scalding; boiling hot | Zhè tāng zhēn ∼. 这汤真∼。 The soup is boiling hot.
From 汤(湯) tāng 'soup' over 火 (huǒ) 'fire'.
Presumably 烫 tàng 'hot' and 汤 tāng 'soup' are cognates, but note the difference in tone.
(燙 is not listed in Karlgren.)
