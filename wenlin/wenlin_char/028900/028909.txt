413  热(F熱) [rè] hot
热[熱] rè* {A} s.v. ①hot | ∼ sị̌ wǒ le. ∼死我了。 I feel extremely hot. ②warm | Shuǐ ∼ ma? 水∼吗? Is the water warm? ◆b.f. ①thermal 热能 rènéng ②ardent; warmhearted 热心 rèxīn ③fever; temperature 发热 fārè ④craze; fad 热门 rèmén(r) ◆n. 〈phy.〉 heat ◆v. heat up; warm up; warm | Fàn liángle, náqù ∼ yị̄ ∼. 饭凉了, 拿去∼一∼。 The food is getting cold. Go and warm it up.
The full form 熱 is 埶 yì phonetic over 灬(火 huǒ) 'fire'.
In the simple form 热, 坴 is replaced by 扌(手 shǒu), so that the top becomes 执 (zhí) 'hold in the hand'. Compare 势(勢) shì in which the same substitution has occurred.
