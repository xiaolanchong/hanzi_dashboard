---- 覃 [Tán] (surname)  [Qín] (surname)  [yǎn]
覃 ²Qín n. Surname
覃 ¹¹tán b.f. deep 覃思 tánsī 覃第 tándì ◆n. Surname
The top of the modern character is 覀 (襾 xià or 西 xī) and the bottom is 早 (zǎo).
The explanation is obscure. The top was once 鹵 (lǔ), supposedly short for 鹹 xián phonetic. The bottom may have been related to 享 (xiǎng) 'enjoy'.
