303  觉(F覺) [jué] perceive  [jiào] (睡觉) sleep; 午觉 nap
觉[覺] ³jué {C} v. ①sense; feel ②wake (up) ③become aware/awakened; discover ◆b.f. feeling; sensation 感觉 gǎnjué*
觉[覺] ⁴jiào {C} b.f. sleep 睡觉 shuìjiào*
The top is (𦥯), the same as 学(學) xué 'learn'. 𦥯 was the old form of 学, before 子 (zǐ) 'child' was added.
The bottom is 见(見 jiàn) 'see'.
The two words 觉 jué and 学 xué are cognates (that is, they are etymologically related, and therefore similar in both sound and meaning).
