---- 磬 [qìng] chime stone; Buddhist inverted bell  [qǐng]
磬 ⁴qìng n. ①〈hist.〉 L-shaped musical stone ②〈Budd.〉 metal piece struck as a signal for dinner/etc. ③brass gong
声 was a picture of a chime stone, a musical instrument made with hanging stones. 殳 (shū) 'beat' (a hand holding a mallet) and 石 (shí) 'stone' have been added to clarify the meaning. Now 声 is used as the simple form of 声(聲) shēng 'sound'.
