931  礎(S础) [chǔ] 基礎 jīchǔ foundation, base, basis
礎[础] ⁴chǔ {E} b.f. base 基础 ¹jīchǔ*
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 础:) From 石 (shí) 'stone' and 出 chū phonetic (in the full form 礎, the phonetic part is 楚 chǔ).
础石 chǔshí means 'plinth'; a plinth is a stone block base in classical architecture. The only important common word that contains 础 is 基础 jīchǔ.
