522  確(S确) [què] true; 正確 correct; 確實 indeed; 明確 explicit
確[确] ²què {E} adv. firmly; indeed; truly ◆b.f. true; reliable; authentic 确实 quèshí*
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 确:) 石 (shí) 'stone' indicates the meaning: solid, firm, certain.
In the full form 確 the phonetic part is 隺 hè. In the simple form 确 the phonetic part is 角 jiǎo.
