1189 緩(S缓) [huǎn] (緩慢 huǎnmàn) slow
緩[缓] ¹huǎn {D} v. ①delay; postpone ②recuperate; revive ◆b.f. ①late; tardy; slow 缓慢 huǎnmàn ②relaxed 缓和 huǎnhé*
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 缓:) From 纟(糸 sī) 'silk' and 爰 yuán ('drag') phonetic.
"Slack; easy-going, remiss, slow, late; neglect, delay, postpone" --Karlgren.
