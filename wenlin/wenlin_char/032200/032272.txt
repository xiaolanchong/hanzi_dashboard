==== 縐(S绉) [zhòu] crape, crepe  [zhōu] 文縐縐 genteel  [chào] [cù]
縐[绉] ⁷zhōu {E} char. 文绉绉 wénzhōuzhōu
绉[縐] ⁶zhòu {E} b.f. crêpe 皱纹 ¹zhòuwén(r) 绉纱 zhòushā 湖绉 Húzhòu
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 绉:) From 纟(糸 sī) 'silk thread' and 刍(芻) chú phonetic.
"Crape; folds; wrinkled; shrivelled" --Karlgren(1923).
Etymologically the same word as 皱 zhòu 'wrinkle'.
