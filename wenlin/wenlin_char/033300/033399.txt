1871 艷(S艳) [yàn] gorgeous; amorous; 鮮艷 xiānyàn bright (color); 艷麗
艷[艳] ⁸yàn {E} b.f. ①beautiful; gorgeous; beauteous 艳丽 yànlì ②romantic 艳情 yànqíng ③〈wr.〉 admire 艳羡 yànxiàn
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 艳:) From 丰(豐 fēng) 'abundant' and 色 (sè) 'color'.
A variant form is 艶, where 豐 appears half-simplified so that it looks like 豊 lǐ.
