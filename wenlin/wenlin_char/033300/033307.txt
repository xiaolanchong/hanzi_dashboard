---- 舛 [chuǎn] (书) error; oppose
舛 ²chuǎn b.f. error 舛驳 chuǎnbó 舛错 chuǎncuò 讹舛 échuǎn
夕 and 㐄 were originally two feet, facing away from each other.
The left side 夕 is from 夂 zhǐ or 夊 suī here, depicting a foot (not 夕 xī 'evening', which depicted a crescent moon). The right side 㐄 was the mirror-image of 夊.
舛 is part of 舞 wǔ 'dance'. Compare 降 jiàng 'descend'.
