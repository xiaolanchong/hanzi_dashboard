---- 艮 [gèn] ☶ ䷳ Keeping Still, Mountain  [gěn] tough  [hén]
艮/哏 gěn 〈coll.〉 s.v. ①tough; hard to chew ②straightforward; blunt; forthright
艮 ²gèn n. one of the eight trigrams ◆v. extend; stretch
Originally just the mirror-image of 見(见 jiàn) 'see', a person with a big eye.
Compare 良 liáng 'good'.
艮 is the 138th of the 214 radicals.
