般 ⁹bō char. 般若 bōrě
From 舟 (zhōu) 'boat' and 殳 (shū) 'beat'.
To 殳 (beat:) propel a 舟 boat --> distribute --> category. The old meaning 'transport' is now 搬 bān, with 扌(手 shǒu) 'hand' added. Don't confuse with 船 chuán 'boat', which has  not 殳.
