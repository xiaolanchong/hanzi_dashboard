890  舞 [wǔ] (跳舞 tiàowǔ) dance
舞 ⁴wǔ {D} v. ①dance ②flourish; brandish ③play tricks with; juggle ④get ◆n. dance
Picture of a dancer holding some sort of ornaments in the hands. When this character was borrowed for the word wú 'without' (now 無 or 无), 舛 (chuǎn) two big feet were added at the bottom for the original meaning 'dance'.
Compare 降 jiàng.
