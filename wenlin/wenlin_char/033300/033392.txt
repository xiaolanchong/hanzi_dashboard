1368 艰(F艱) [jiān] 艰苦 jiānkǔ arduous, 艰难 jiānnán difficult
艰[艱] ¹³jiān {E} b.f. difficult; arduous 艰难 jiānnán 艰苦 ¹jiānkǔ
According to Karlgren(1940),  depicted a person with back-bound hands, in distress. 艮 gèn was added later (perhaps phonetic, though Karlgren says it was 壴 zhù 'drum' corrupted).
In the simple form 艰, the left side  is abbreviated to 又 (yòu), the same as in 汉(漢) hàn, 难(難) nán, and 叹(嘆) tàn.
