---- 舜 [Shùn] name of a legendary Chinese monarch
舜 Shùn {E} n. mythical successor of Emperor Yao and like him a paragon of regal virtue
From 爫 (zhǎo) 'hand', 冖 (mì) 'lid', and 舛 chuǎn ('two feet'), phonetic.
This character originally named a kind of plant, which it may have depicted, but it has changed beyond recognition. (See also 蕣.) The bottom was reshaped into 舛 chuǎn as a phonetic component. One old form is 䑞.
The legendary wise and benevolent Shùn is said to have ruled from 2255-2205 B.C.
