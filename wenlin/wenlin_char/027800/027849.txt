1624 泉 [quán] spring; (喷泉 pēnquán) fountain; 源泉 yuánquán source
泉 ⁴quán {D} b.f. ①spring; fountain 温泉 wēnquán* 泉水 quánshuǐ ②〈hist.〉 coin 泉币 quánbì ◆n. Surname
泉 is 白 (bái) 'white' over 水 (shuǐ) 'water'.
"Originally a picture of a basin from which water flows; remade into: 白 (white:) pure 水 water" --Karlgren.
As a component in the characters 原 and 源 yuán, 泉 is slightly simplified by replacing 水 with 小.
