531  油 [yóu] oil; 汽油 qìyóu gasoline; 酱油 jiàngyóu soy sauce [yōu] [yòu]
油 ²yóu {B} n. oil; fat; grease ◆v. ①apply paint/oil/varnish ②be stained with oil/grease ◆s.v. ①oily; greasy | Zhè cài tài ∼ le. 这菜太∼了。 This dish is too greasy. ②glib; slick | Zhè rén tè ∼. 这人特∼。 This person is very glib. ◆b.f. ①petroleum; petroleum products 石油 ¹shíyóu* 汽油 ¹qìyóu* 煤油 méiyóu ②spontaneously 油然 ¹yóurán
From 氵(水 shuǐ) 'water' (liquid) and 由 yóu phonetic.
Sometimes pronounced yōu (first tone) when doubled (as in 油油, 绿油油, 碧油油, 光油油, 黑油油, 黄油油, 乌油油), but the modern standard pronunciation is always rising tone in all these words, according to 现代汉语规范词典.
