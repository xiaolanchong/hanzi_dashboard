771  沙 [shā] sand; 沙漠 shāmò desert; 沙丘 shāqiū dune  [shà] [suō]
沙 ²shā {D} n. ①sand ②Surname ◆b.f. sth. granulated; sand-like 豆沙 dòushā* ◆s.v. ①hoarse; husky ②gritty
From 氵(水 shuǐ) 'water' and 少 shǎo 'small'. Compare 砂, with 石 (shí) 'stone' instead of 氵 water, and with the same sound and meaning as 沙. According to Karlgren, 少 is signific, not phonetic.
