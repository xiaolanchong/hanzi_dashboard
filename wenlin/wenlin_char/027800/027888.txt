1542 泰 [tài] ䷊ Peace; 泰国 Thailand; 泰山
泰 ³tài {E} b.f. peace, quiet 泰然 tàirán 康泰 kāngtài*
"The seal shows the upper part to be a fusion of 大 and 廾 () two hands, the lower is 水 water; the word is etymologically the same as 太 tài, and as 大 in one of its readings" --Karlgren.
Notice that the bottom 氺 is five strokes.
