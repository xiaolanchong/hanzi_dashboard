蒙 Měng {E} b.f. Mongol 蒙古 Mẹ̌nggǔ
The signific part of 蒙 is 冖 (mì) 'cover'. Together with the 一 line under it, it is like 冃 (mào), a cover. The lower 一 line of 冃 is replaced by 豕 (shǐ) 'boar', and on top of it all is 艹(草 cǎo) 'grass'. The significance of these last two elements is unclear. Cognate with 梦(夢) mèng 'dream', and also with 瞢朦矇 méng.
Compare 劳(勞) láo and 营(營) yíng, where the top 𫇦(𤇾) has a different origin.
