2103 葬 [zàng] (埋葬 máizàng) bury (the dead); 葬礼 funeral
葬 ¹zàng {D} v. bury; inter | ²Tā de fùmǔ ∼ zài zhèlǐ. 她的父母∼在这里。 Her parents were buried here.
From 艹(草 cǎo) 'herbs' (top and bottom) and 死 (sǐ) 'death'.
"To bury; to hide -- to cover a 死 dead person's body with 艸 herbs (doubled: the lower 艸 now deformed into 廾); the word is cognate to 藏 to hide" --Karlgren.
