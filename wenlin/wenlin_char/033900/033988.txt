1789 蓄 [xù] store, save; grow; 积蓄 jīxù save; 储蓄 chǔxù deposit
蓄 ¹xù {D} b.f. ①store/save up 储蓄 chǔxù* ②grow 蓄发 xùfà ③entertain (an idea); harbor 含蓄 ¹hánxù 蓄意 xùyì*
From 艹(草 cǎo) 'grass' and 畜 xù 'raise, nourish'.
"Rear; cultivate, store up, collect -- etymologically the same word as 畜 but more especially bearing upon the crop, and character hence enlarged by 艸" --Karlgren(1923).
