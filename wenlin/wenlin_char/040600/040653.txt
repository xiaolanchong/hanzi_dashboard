---- 黍 [shǔ] broomcorn millet (Panicum miliaceum)
黍 ⁸shǔ b.f. millet 黍稷 ³shǔjì 禾黍 héshǔ
From 禾 (hé) 'grain', 入 (rù) 'enter' and 水 (shuǐ) 'water'.
黍可为酒, 禾入水也。A 禾 grain which can be 入 put into 水 water and fermented to make wine (the bottom was not really 入 over 水 in the oracle-bone character; the whole thing was a picture of the plant).
黍 is the 202nd of the 214 radicals.
