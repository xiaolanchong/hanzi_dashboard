1013 默 [mò] silent; 默默 mòmò silently; 沉默 reticent; 幽默 humor
默 ⁶mò {E} b.f. keep silent 静默 jìngmò ◆v. write from memory
From 黑 hēi 'black' and 犬 (quǎn) 'dog'.
According to Karlgren, 默 mò 'dark; silent; secret' and 墨 mò 'black; ink' are both cognate with 黑 hēi 'black' ("formed with a deriv. suffix m-").
