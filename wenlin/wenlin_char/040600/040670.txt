(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 点:) The full form 點 is 黑 (hēi) 'black' plus 占 zhān phonetic. The simple form 点 is 占 over 灬(火 huǒ) 'fire'. (点 is also the Japanese form).
占 zhān is also phonetic in 店 diàn 'store'.
