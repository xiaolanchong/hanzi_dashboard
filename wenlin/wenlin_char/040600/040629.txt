86   麵(S面) [miàn] wheat flour; (麵条 miàntiáo) noodle
麵[面] ²miàn {E} n. ①noodles (made with wheat flour) ②(wheat) flour ◆b.f. ①foods made with wheat flour 面包 miànbāo* ②powder-like 胡椒面 hújiāomiàn(r) ◆s.v. 〈topo.〉 ①soft and floury ②weak; lax; slow
From 麥 (mài) 'wheat' and 面 miàn phonetic.
Among simple form characters, 面 is used in place of 麵.
