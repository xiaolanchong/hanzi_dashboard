1414 黎 [lí] mulititude; (surname); 黎明 límíng daybreak
黎 ⁷lí {E} b.f. black; dark 黎明 límíng* ◆char. 波多黎各 Bōduōlígè 巴黎 Bālí*
The phonetic is 利 lì, but with a three-stroke variant of 刀 knife, instead of 刂.
"Black, blackhaired; the black-haired ones: the people --- the signific is not, as generally stated, 黍 millet but 桼 varnish abbreviated" --Karlgren.
Compare 黧 lí.
