1133 魯(S鲁) [lǔ] (surname); (魯莽 lǔmǎng) rude; dull  [lǚ]
魯[鲁] ¹lǔ {E} b.f. ①stupid; dull 愚鲁 yúlǔ ②vulgar 粗鲁 cūlǔ ③rash; impetuous; blunt 鲁莽 lụ̌mǎng ◆n. ①short name for Shandong ②Surname
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 鲁:) From 鱼(魚 yú) 'fish' over 曰 (yuē) 'speak'.
"Unable, stupid; vulgar, simple -- 魚 fish (unable to) 曰 speak..." --Karlgren.
