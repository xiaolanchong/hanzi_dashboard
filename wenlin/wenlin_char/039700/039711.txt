==== 鬟 [huán] to wind the hair into a knot on the top of the head
鬟 ³huán {E} b.f. bun-type hairdo 鬟髻 huánjì 丫鬟 yāhuan 风鬟雾鬓 fēnghuánwùbìn
From 髟 (biāo) 'hair' and 睘 huán.
Cognate with 還(还) huán 'return' and 環(环) huán 'ring'.
"To wind the hair into a knot on the top of the head" --Karlgren(1923).
"A slave-girl's hair done up" --Wieger.
