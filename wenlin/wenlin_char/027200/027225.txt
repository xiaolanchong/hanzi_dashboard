---- 橙 [chéng] orange (color); 橙子 chéngzi orange (fruit)  [dīng] [dèng]
橙 ¹¹chéng {E} n. orange (tree/fruit) ◆b.f. orange (color) 橙色 ¹chéngsè
橙 ⁸dīng b.f. orange (fruit) 柳丁 liǔdīng 柳丁汁 liǔdīngzhī
From 木 (mù) 'tree' and 登 dēng phonetic.
Formerly the standard pronunciation of 橙子 was chénzi, but now it's chéngzi.
