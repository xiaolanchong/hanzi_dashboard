==== 歆 [xīn] 歆慕 xīnmù admire; 歆羡 xīnxiàn adore
歆 ¹⁰xīn b.f. admire 欣慕 ²xīnmù 欣羡 xīnxiàn
From 音 yīn phonetic and 欠 (qiàn) 'short of breath'.
"Accept the fumes of sacrifice; enjoy" --Karlgren(1923).
Interchanged with 欣 in 欣/歆慕 and 欣/歆羡, but etymologically distinct. In Cantonese the pronunciations are still different: 歆 ends in -m, 欣 ends in -n.
