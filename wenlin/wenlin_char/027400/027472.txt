967  歐(S欧) [ōu] 歐洲 Ōuzhōu Europe; 歐姆 ōumǔ ohm  [ǒu]
歐[欧] ¹Ōu {E} n. ①Surname ②short name for Europe
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 欧:) From 区(區) ōu phonetic and 欠 (qiàn) 'yawn'.
Formerly a variant of 呕 as in 呕吐, now used only as a surname and for transcribing sounds from foreign languages.
