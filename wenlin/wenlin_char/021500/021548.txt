300  听(F聽) [tīng] listen; 听见 hear; 听不懂 not understand  [yǐn] [yí]
听[聽] ¹tīng* {A} v. ①listen; hear ②heed; obey | Tā duì wǒ de yìjian ∼ bu jìnqu. 他对我的意见∼不进去。 He turned a deaf ear to my suggestions. ③〈wr.〉 administer ④allow; let ◆m. 〈topo.〉 tin; can
The full form 聽 is composed of 耳 (ěr) 'ear', 㥁(德 dé) 'virtue', and 壬 tǐng phonetic (lower left corner).
The simple form 听 is 口 (kǒu) 'mouth' plus 斤 jīn phonetic. (听 long ago stood for a word yǐn 'smile'; it was already being used instead of 聽 tīng by the 17th century).
