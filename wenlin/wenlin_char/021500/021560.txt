714  吸 [xī] inhale; 呼吸 breathe; 吸收 absorb; 吸引 attract
吸 ²xī {B} v. absorb; suck up ◆b.f. ①inhale; breathe in 呼吸 hūxī* ②attract; draw to oneself 吸引 xīyǐn*
From 口 (kǒu) 'mouth' and 及 jí phonetic.
Compare 息 xī 'breath'. The similar meanings and identical sounds of 吸 xī and 息 xī in modern Mandarin are coincidental; the two words are not etymologically related (Cantonese 吸 kap and 息 sik).
