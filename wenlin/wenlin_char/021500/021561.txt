1144 吹 [chuī] blow  [chuì]
吹 ¹chuī* {A} v. ①blow; puff ②play (a wind instrument) | Nǐ huì ∼ dízi ma? 你会∼笛子吗? Do you play flute? ③〈coll.〉 ⓐboast; brag | Nǐ zhēn néng ∼! 你真能∼! Gosh, you can brag! ⓑbreak up (a relationship) | Tāmen liǎ ∼ le. 他们俩∼了。 The two of them are no longer boyfriend-girlfriend. ④fall through (of plans) | Nà bǐ shēngyì ∼ le. 那笔生意∼了。 That business deal has fallen through.
From 口 (kǒu) 'mouth' and 欠 (qiàn) 'breath'.
"To 欠 pant, blow with 口 the mouth" --Karlgren.
