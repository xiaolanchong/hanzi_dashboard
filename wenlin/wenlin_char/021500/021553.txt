2740 吱 [zhī] (sound of door creaking)  [zī] (squeak, chirp, peep)  [qì]
吱 ⁸zhī {E} on. creak | Mén “∼” de yī̠ shēng kāi le. 门 “∼” 的一声开了。 The door creaked open.
吱 ³zī {E} on. ①squeal (of mice) ②chirp; peep (of birds)
From 口 (kǒu) 'mouth' and 支 zhī phonetic.
There is a variant form 嗞 with 兹 zī instead of 支 as the phonetic component.
