1057 含 [hán] keep in mouth; contain  [hàn]
含 ¹hán {B} v. ①keep in the mouth | ∼ yị̄huìr ²zài yàn. ∼一会儿再咽。 Keep it in the mouth for a while before swallowing. ②contain | Shuǐ zhōng ∼ yǎng. 水中∼氧。 Water contains oxygen. ③nurse; cherish; harbor
From 今 jīn ('now') phonetic and 口 (kǒu) 'mouth'.
"Hold in the mouth; contain; cherish; restrain, tolerate, suffer" --Karlgren.
