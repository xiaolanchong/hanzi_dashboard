2363 呂(S吕) [Lǚ] (surname)
呂[吕] Lǚ n. Surname ◆char. 律吕 lǜlǚ
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 吕:) "Picture of the spine, the vertebrae; etymologically the same word as 膂" --Karlgren.
The full form 呂 has a 丿 stroke joining the two squares. The oldest forms, on oracle bones, have two circles with no joining stroke.
