1793 呈 [chéng] to present; 呈现 appear; 呈报 submit a report  [chěng]
呈 ⁵chéng {D} v. ①assume (a form/color/etc.) ②submit; present ◆b.f. petition; memorial 呈文 ²chéngwén
From 口 (kǒu) 'mouth' over 王. The bottom was originally 壬 tǐng phonetic; now it is usually written like 王 (wáng) 'king'. A re-interpretation is: to 口 speak to the 王 king, make an appearance.
