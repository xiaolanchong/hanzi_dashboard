1366 址 [zhǐ] 地址 dìzhǐ address
址 ⁸zhǐ {E} b.f. ①foundation of a building 遗址 ¹yízhǐ ②position 位址 wèizhǐ 地址 dìzhǐ*
From 土 (tǔ) 'ground' and 止 zhǐ (picture of a foot).
"Foot of a mountain, foundation, base" --Karlgren.
Etymologically the same word as 止 zhǐ 'stop' and 趾 zhǐ 'foot', specifyingly enlarged by 土.
