---- 圭 [guī] jade tablet used as sceptre by ancient feudal lords
圭 ⁹guī n. 〈hist.〉 ①jade tablet as a symbol of power ②tablet or flat scepter held by nobles/rulers on ceremonial occasions ③a burial object
From 土 (tǔ) 'land' doubled.
"The token used in conferring 土 fiefs (doubled)" --Karlgren(1923).
圭 should (at least in principle) be written with six strokes.
