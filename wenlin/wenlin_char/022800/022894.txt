1098 奮(S奋) [fèn] 奮鬥 fèndòu struggle; 興奮 xīngfèn excited
奮[奋] ⁴fèn {E} b.f. ①act vigorously 奋斗 fèndòu ②lift; raise 奋臂高呼 fènbìgāohū
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 奋:) From 大 (dà) 'big' over 田 (tián) 'field'. The full form 奮 has on top, not just 大, but 奞 (suí) 'spread the wings and fly', composed of 大 over 隹 (zhuī) 'bird'.
"Rise suddenly (as a bird), rush out, burst forth; to rouse; impetuous, energetic, courageous -- spread the wings and fly over 田 the fields" --Karlgren.
Compare 夺(奪) duó 'seize'.
