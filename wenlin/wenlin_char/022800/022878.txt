xxxx 奞 [suí] (archaic: 'spread wings and fly', part of 奮 and 奪)  [xùn]
No single-syllable words currently in dictionary.	add new s.s.w.
From 大 (dà) 'big' and 隹 (zhuī) 'bird'.
"Spread the wings and fly -- a 隹 bird making himself 大 large, extending his wings" --Karlgren.
奞 is co-signific in 奪(夺) duó and 奮(奋) fèn.
