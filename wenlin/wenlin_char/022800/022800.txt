xxxx 夐 [xiòng] (archaic:) far  [xuàn]
夐/敻 xiòng b.f. distant; far off (in space or time) 夐古 xiònggǔ 夐绝 xiòngjué 幽夐 yōuxiòng
Composed of ⺈ over 冖 over 人 over 目 over 夂.
夐 is phonetic in the full form of 琼(瓊) qióng.
夐 (abbreviated to just its top) is also phonetic in 奂(奐) huàn.
A variant form is 敻, with 攵 (four strokes) at the bottom instead of 夂 (three strokes).
