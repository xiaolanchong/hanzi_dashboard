1647 奉 [fèng] accept; offer; 奉命 receive orders; 奉承 flatter
奉 ³fèng {E} 〈wr.〉 v. ①give; present ②receive ③esteem; revere; respect ④believe in ⑤wait upon; attend to ⑥〈court.〉 have the honor to
The primary meaning is, to hold respectfully with both hands. The top is from  two hands holding a 丰 fēng bouquet, and the bottom is another hand ( = 扌 = 手 shǒu). 丰 is both signific and phonetic. Cognate with 捧 pěng; phonetic in 棒 bàng.
