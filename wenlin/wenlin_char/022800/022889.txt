==== 奩(S奁) [lián] women's toilet case in ancient times; 妝奩 trousseau
奩[奁] ¹²lián b.f. 〈trad.〉 women's makeup case 奁敬 liánjìng 陪奁 péilián
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 奁:) Originally 匳: 匚 a box, plus 僉 qiān phonetic. Somehow it became distorted into 大 (dà) plus 區 or 区 (qū).
A variant form is 匲.
