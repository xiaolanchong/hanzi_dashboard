==== 奂(F奐) [huàn] excellent, gay, beautiful
奂[奐] ¹⁴huàn char. 轮奂 ²lúnhuàn 美奂美轮 měihuànměilún
"From 廾 two hands, signific, now corrupted into 大, and 夐 xiòng ...abbreviated phonetic" --Karlgren.
Notice the slight difference between the full form 奐 (which has 儿 or 八 in the middle) and the simple form 奂.
