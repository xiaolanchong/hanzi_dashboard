2264 耍 [shuǎ] play
耍 shuǎ* {C} v. ①play (role/tricks/etc.) | Bụ̀yào duì wọ̌ ∼ tàidu. 不要对我∼态度。 Don't be rude to me. ②gamble ③play with; display; flourish (a sword/etc.) ④make fun of sb.; fool sb. | Nǐ shì zài ∼̣ wǒ a! 你是在∼我啊! You're making fun of me! ◆n. Surname
From 而 (ér) 'beard' and 女 (nǚ) 'woman'.
"Play, sport -- 女 woman putting on 而 a beard" --Karlgren.
Not to be confused with 要 yào.
