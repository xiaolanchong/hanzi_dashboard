2159 翁 [wēng] old man; (surname)  [wěng]
翁 ¹wēng* {E} b.f. ①old man 老翁 lǎowēng ②father jiāwēng ③father-in-law; in-law 翁姑 wēnggū 翁婿 wēngxù ④person in charge; head of the house 主人翁 zhǔrénwēng ◆n. Surname
From 公 gōng phonetic and 羽 (yǔ) 'wings'.
"Feathers at the neck of a bird; grey-beard, old man; Sir; father" --Karlgren.
