2482 翰 [hàn] writing brush; writing
翰 ⁹hàn {E} b.f. 〈wr.〉 ①writing brush 翰墨 hànmò* ②writing 书翰 shūhàn
From 倝(乾) qián phonetic and 羽 (yǔ) 'feathers'.
"A pheasant's feather, to fly; pen, pencil; write; notification" --Karlgren(1923).
In the phrase "翰音登于天" (易经), 翰 means some kind of bird (cockcrow or golden pheasant).
