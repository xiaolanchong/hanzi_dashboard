---- 耒 [lěi] plow handle; radical 127
耒 ⁷lěi b.f. handle of a plow 耒耜 lěisì 耒耨 lěinòu
"A piece of 木 wood with crosspieces, the teeth of the instrument" --Karlgren(1923).
The modern word for 'plough' is 耕 gēng.
耒 is the 127th of the 214 radicals.
In 简体字 orthography the first stroke should be 一 héng, straight horizontal. Among 繁体字 it is sometimes 丿 piě, falling to the left.
