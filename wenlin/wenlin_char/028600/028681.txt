2581 瀉(S泻) [xiè] 傾瀉 come down in torrents; 瀉藥 laxative
瀉[泻] ⁵xiè {D} v. ①flow swiftly; pour out ②have diarrhea
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 泻:) From 氵(水 shuǐ) 'water' and 写(寫) xiě.
According to Karlgren, 泻 xiè and 写 xiě are cognate words, the original meaning being 'dissipate, disburden'.
