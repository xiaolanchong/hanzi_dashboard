1995 濱(S滨) [bīn] 海濱 seashore; 濱臨 be close to (body of water)
濱[滨] ²bīn {E} b.f. ①bank; brink; shore 湖滨 húbīn ②waterfront 海滨 hǎibīn ③be close to; border on (water) 滨江 bīnjiāng
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 滨:) From 氵(水 shuǐ) 'water' and 宾(賓) bīn phonetic.
The character 濒(瀕) bīn is sometimes used instead of 滨(濱) for the meaning 'be close to' (滨临 bīnlín).
