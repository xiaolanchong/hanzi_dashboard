==== 瀆(S渎) [dú] ditch, drain; show disrespect  [dòu]
瀆[渎] ⁵dú {E} b.f. ①ditch; drain ②disrespectful; irresponsible 渎犯 dúfàn 贪污渎职 tānwūdúzhí 污渎 ²wūdú
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 渎:) From 氵(水 shuǐ) 'water' and 卖(賣) yú phonetic.
See 读(讀) dú and 卖(賣) mài for explanation of the phonetic.
