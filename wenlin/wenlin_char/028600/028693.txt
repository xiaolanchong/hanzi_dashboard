---- 瀕(S濒) [bīn] be close to (sea or river); be on the brink of
瀕[濒] ³bīn {E} b.f. ①be close to (the sea/river/etc.) 濒临 ¹bīnlín ②be on the brink of 濒危 ¹bīnwēi*
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 濒:) From 氵(水 shuǐ) 'water' and 频(頻) pín phonetic.
Etymologically the same word as 滨(濱) bīn as in 海滨 hǎibīn 'seashore'.
