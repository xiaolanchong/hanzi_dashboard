---- 瀋 [shěn] (S沈) 瀋陽 Shěnyáng; 墨瀋未乾  [chèn] [pán]
瀋[沈/渖] ³shěn {E} b.f. liquid essence 墨沈 mòshěn See also ¹chén
From 氵(水 shuǐ) 'water' and 審 shěn phonetic.
The standard simplified form is 沈, which is also the original way of writing the surname Shěn.
There is another simplified form 渖, apparently non-standard.
