==== 餳(S饧) [xíng] sugar cube   [táng] ( = 糖 sugar)
糖/餳/餹[-/醣/饧] ¹táng {A} n. ①sugar ②carbohydrate ③sweets; candy
餳[饧] ⁵xíng s.v. drowsy ◆v. become soft; sticky (of dough/candy/etc.) ◆b.f. malt treacle; sweet foods 粥饧 zhōuxíng
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 饧:) From 饣(食 shí) 'food' and 𠃓(昜) yáng.
