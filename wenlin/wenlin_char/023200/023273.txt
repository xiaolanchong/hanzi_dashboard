2017 嫩 [nèn] tender, delicate; light; inexperienced
嫩 ¹nèn {C} s.v. ①tender; delicate | Zhè ròu shāo de tǐng ∼. 这肉烧得挺∼。 This meat has been cooked tender. ②inexperienced; unskilled
From 女 (nǚ) 'woman', and 敕 (chì 'edict'). In earlier forms 媆 and 㜛, the right side was 耎 ruǎn or 軟 ruǎn phonetic. 敕 here "is a corruption of 軟 as phonetic" --Karlgren.
