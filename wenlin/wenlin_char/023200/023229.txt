606  媽(S妈) [mā] mother, mom; 媽媽 māma mama
媽[妈] ¹mā {E} n. ma; mom; mother ◆suf. (forming part of address for aunt or female servants) | gū∼ 姑∼ father's sister | nǎi∼ 奶∼ wet nurse | Wáng ∼ 王∼ female servant Wang
(Explanation from the entry for the simple form 妈:) From 女 (nǚ) 'woman' and 马(馬) mǎ phonetic.
妈妈 māma is one of the few words that tend to be the same even in unrelated languages.
