1933 碧 [bì] green jade; bluish green; 碧绿 dark green; 碧蓝 dark blue
碧 ⁷bì {E} b.f. bluish/emerald green; blue 碧绿 bìlǜ ◆n. 〈wr.〉 green jade; jasper
From 王(玉 yù) 'jade', 白 bái phonetic and 石 (shí) 'stone'.
"Green or blue jade -- double significs 玉 and 石" --Karlgren.
The top happens to be like 珀 pò 'amber'.
