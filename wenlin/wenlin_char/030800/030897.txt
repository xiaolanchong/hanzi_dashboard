2986 碱(F鹼) [jiǎn] alkali; soda  [xián]
碱/硷[鹼/礆] ⁴jiǎn {C} n. ①alkali ②soda
The full form 鹼 is composed of 鹵 (lǔ) 'halogen' and 僉 qiān phonetic.
The simple form 碱 is composed of 石 (shí) 'stone' and 咸 xián phonetic.
There are various variant forms: 硷, 礆, 鹻, 堿...
Not to be confused with 鹹(咸) xián 'salty'.
